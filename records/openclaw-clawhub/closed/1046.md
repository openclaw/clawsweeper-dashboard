## Best Possible Solution

Keep the permanent-deletion policy unless maintainers approve a controlled recovery path, and consolidate publish-rate-limit recovery through https://github.com/openclaw/clawhub/issues/1222 after checking production GITHUB_TOKEN and quota evidence.
