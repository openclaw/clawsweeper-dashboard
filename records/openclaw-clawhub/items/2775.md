## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.84

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Preserve community pagination when the probe hits its scan limit:** `convex/packages.ts:2280-2291`
  - body: This helper scans a bounded community slice with `take()` and then returns `false` if no visible digest is found. Because `take()` does not tell us whether more rows exist, a full official page followed by 200 pending/suspicious/private community digests and then a visible digest would be reported as `isDone: true`, so clients can never reach the later community results. Please return a community continuation when the bounded probe hits the scan limit, or use an equivalent cursor-safe path without adding a second `paginate()` in the same query.
  - confidence: 0.84
