The issue reports `clawhub publish` failing because personal-publisher creation triggered Convex's multiple-paginated-query limit during owner-publisher digest rebuilds.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the `9b27c1a1` page-per-mutation implementation, then have maintainers confirm production behavior and close or consolidate the remaining duplicate publish-pagination reports.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The current fix is the narrow maintainable solution because it preserves indexed pagination and continuation scheduling instead of replacing pagination with unbounded `collect()` calls as proposed in https://github.com/openclaw/clawhub/pull/1183.
