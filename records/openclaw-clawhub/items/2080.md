The issue asks for a supported recovery path when a UI-deleted or stale-owner plugin package remains reserved and blocks later publishing under the intended publisher.

Maintainers should inspect the affected production package documents, choose an audit-logged restore, transfer, identity repair, or explicit policy exception, and clarify owner-facing deletion and recovery semantics.
