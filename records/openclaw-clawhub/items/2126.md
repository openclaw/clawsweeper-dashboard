In `convex/httpApiV1/packagesV1.ts`, update the category validation in both `listPackages` and `searchPackages` so `category` is rejected for skills/unscoped mixed skill results, but allowed when `family=code-plugin` or `family=bundle-plugin` is present, and still allowed on `/api/v1/plugins`, `/api/v1/code-plugins`, and `/api/v1/bundle-plugins`. Add regression coverage for `/api/v1/packages?family=code-plugin&category=data` and `/api/v1/packages/search?q=demo&family=code-plugin&category=data`, including that unscoped `/api/v1/packages?category=data` and `family=skill` remain rejected. Do not change the taxonomy, schema shape, or UI design while fixing this blocker.
