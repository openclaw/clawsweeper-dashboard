Keep open. Current main still blocks OAuth sign-in for permanently deleted ClawHub users, the live issue remains part of an open account-deletion recovery cluster, and the remaining action is security-sensitive account support and product policy rather than conservative cleanup.

## Best Possible Solution

Define a maintainer-approved account recovery or re-registration policy, then implement or operate it with audited identity checks, ban separation, deletion-privacy semantics, clear user-facing errors, docs, and regression tests.
