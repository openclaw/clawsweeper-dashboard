Add aligned weekly, monthly, and all-time popularity to publisher, skill, and plugin browse surfaces, supported by bounded daily rollups and stable temporal leaderboard pagination.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue as the canonical RFC, approve one shared period/scoring/attribution contract, then land package and publisher daily rollups, snapshot-pinned per-entity leaderboards, and finally the three aligned browse UIs in staged PRs.
