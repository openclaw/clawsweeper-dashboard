## Summary

Keep this open: this is a recent security-moderation support question, and the reporter’s follow-up still needs a maintainer policy answer on whether external checkout confirmation is sufficient artifact evidence for purchase authority.

## What This Changes

The issue asks what artifact evidence is required to clear a Tool Misuse finding for a commerce skill that sends users to checkout or confirmation flows.

## Best Possible Solution

A maintainer should answer or document a commerce-specific evidence checklist covering per-transaction confirmation, payment and shipping scope, OAuth scopes, limits, reversibility, and when to request a rescan or appeal.
