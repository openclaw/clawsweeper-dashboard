## Summary

Keep open: this is a live moderation/support question about whether Shop's checkout confirmation flow is enough evidence to clear a ClawScan flag, and current policy leaves that judgment to maintainers or moderators rather than repository cleanup.

## What This Changes

The issue asks what artifacts are needed to address a ClawScan Tool Misuse and Exploitation flag for Shop's checkout, return, and reorder flows.

## Best Possible Solution

Provide moderator guidance that acceptable evidence should explicitly show checkout and reorder links stop at a user-controlled hosted confirmation/payment step, returns do not mutate orders without user action, and auth scopes, token handling, and quantity or transaction limits are clear.

## Solution Assessment
