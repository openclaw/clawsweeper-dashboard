## Summary

Keep this open because ClawHub cleanup policy does not auto-close issues, and the remaining question is an active security-moderation policy decision about what evidence is sufficient for commerce actions that can spend money or modify orders.

## What This Changes

The issue asks what artifacts are needed to clear a ClawScan finding for the Shop skill's buy, return, and reorder flows when Buy Now URLs route users to checkout confirmation.

## Best Possible Solution

Define a commerce artifact checklist in the security docs/spec for explicit per-transaction confirmation, OAuth scope disclosure, limits, and reversibility, then tell the publisher what to update before appeal or rescan.
