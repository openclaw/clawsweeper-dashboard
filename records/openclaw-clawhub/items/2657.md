Full review comments:

- **[P1] Keep content-rights on a separate service token:** `convex/httpApiV1/contentRightsV1.ts:81`
  - body: This wires the new Hermit content-rights proxy to `CLAWHUB_BAN_APPEALS_TOKEN`. Current ClawHub security intent scopes that token to the ban-appeals service path, while the dependent Hermit PR at https://github.com/openclaw/hermit/pull/15 also accepts it for reading content-rights cases and appending correspondence.   - confidence: 0.91
- **[P2] Preserve correspondence bodies verbatim:** `packages/clawhub-admin/src/commands/contentRights.ts:83`
  - body: `record-correspondence` advertises an exact plain-text body and the skill says it records exact correspondence, but this trims the file before upload. Inbound legal correspondence with leading or trailing blank lines, or a final newline, will be archived differently from what was received. Reject all-whitespace bodies without mutating the stored text.
  - confidence: 0.86
