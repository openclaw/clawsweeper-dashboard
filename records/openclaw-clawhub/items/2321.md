## Summary

Keep this open: this collaborator-authored moderation/security RFC is still an active maintainer product and security-policy decision. Current main has broad acceptable-usage guidance, ClawScan/SkillSpector context, capability tags, and moderation-state mechanics, but it does not yet provide the requested category-to-enforcement synthesis for public policy, reviewer guidance, discovery, installability, and account action.

The issue asks maintainers to define clearer content-policy categories and map them to review buckets, registry visibility, installability, upload review, and enforcement consequences.
