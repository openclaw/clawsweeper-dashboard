## Best Possible Solution

Expose a narrow owner/staff-supported path for existing staticScan code/file/line/message details in moderation-status and human inspect, document the stable fields and current version-JSON workaround, and keep raw evidence/rule catalogs behind a maintainer-approved disclosure policy.
