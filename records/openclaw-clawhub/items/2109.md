## Best Possible Solution

Define a narrow owner/staff diagnostics surface for existing staticScan code/file/line/message fields in moderation-status and inspect, document the supported fields and version-JSON workaround, and keep broader rule catalogs or raw evidence behind an approved disclosure policy.

## Solution Assessment

Yes directionally for narrow owner/staff disclosure of existing staticScan fields. Broader server-side rule catalogs, regexes, raw evidence, LLM findings, or VirusTotal internals need maintainer security/product approval first.
