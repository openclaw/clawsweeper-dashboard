## Summary

## What This Changes

Define a trustworthy community publisher or vetted-plugin path and clarify why code-plugin scans can remain pending.

## Best Possible Solution

Define package- or publisher-scoped vetted-community semantics in the focused RFC, clearly separate them from Official and automated scan verdicts, and investigate any still-pending named releases through current production scan records.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

No, not as one combined implementation. The trust-tier request should be resolved through the narrower vetted-community-plugin policy RFC, while pending scans should be diagnosed per release from production scanner records.
