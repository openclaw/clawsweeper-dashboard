## Best Possible Solution

Design a publisher-scoped preview and confirmation flow with family filters, per-item safety checks, and clear partial-failure reporting before implementing the chosen CLI/API contract.

## Solution Assessment

No, not as an unqualified install-all shortcut. The safer solution is a family-aware, previewable flow that preserves package-specific trust, moderation, compatibility, and install checks.
