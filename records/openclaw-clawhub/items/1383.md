Keep open under the ClawHub issue-cleanup policy. Current main still has relevance-ranked search, local installed-skill listing, and bounded registry browsing, but not deterministic exact-slug, slug-prefix, or list-style hub enumeration; the related merged search PR improved relevance/count honesty without providing this registry discovery contract.
