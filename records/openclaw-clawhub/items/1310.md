## Summary

The issue remains useful research and needs product/security judgment rather than stale cleanup or an immediate fix PR.

## What This Changes

Define structured, user-facing skill-quality signals covering documentation, maintenance, claim accuracy, dependency hygiene, complexity, and alternatives alongside existing security evidence.

## Best Possible Solution

Develop a focused product RFC defining separate explainable dimensions, evidence provenance and freshness, prompt-only applicability, appeal semantics, and the effect of each signal on display, ranking, installability, and moderation before choosing an external scoring engine or aggregate score.
