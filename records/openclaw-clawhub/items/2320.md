## Summary

Keep open: current main has deletion, recovery-purge, publisher, and reservation mechanics, but this collaborator-authored RFC still asks for the policy decisions those mechanics cannot supply: claim evidence, dispute adjudication, deleted/inactive namespace transfer, and public-vs-private evidence boundaries.

## What This Changes

Define a transparent policy path for organization namespace claims/disputes and account recovery after owner-initiated deletion.

## Best Possible Solution

Approve evidence, dispute, namespace-release, and deletion-recovery rules in durable specs; publish only the non-sensitive policy in docs; then split any admin tooling or data cleanup into bounded follow-up tasks.

Not applicable: this is a policy RFC, not a deterministic runtime bug. The linked reports at https://github.com/openclaw/clawhub/issues/1294 and https://github.com/openclaw/clawhub/issues/1824 are concrete decision cases.
