The collaborator-authored item has protected maintainer, product, and security review signals, so it remains necessary. ## What This Changes

Establish transparent policy for organization namespace claims and disputes, deleted or inactive owner namespaces, and recovery after intentional account deletion.

## Best Possible Solution
