## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.9

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Reserve lane-local backend env keys:** `scripts/ui-proof.mjs:269`
  - body: Because `opts.env` is spread after the generated lane-local Convex values, a caller can pass `--env VITE_CONVEX_URL=...` or `--env CONVEX_DEPLOYMENT=...` and route proof back to a shared backend. That contradicts the new full-stack invariant and can make UI proof look isolated when it is not.
  - confidence: 0.9
- **[P1] Keep proof env files out of copied artifacts:** `scripts/ui-proof.mjs:304`
  - body: This writes `.env.local` inside `$remote_out`, and the runner later rsyncs that whole directory into the proof artifacts. Since `--env` may carry secrets or private endpoints, the generated env file needs to live outside the copied artifact tree or be excluded before artifact sync/publish.
  - confidence: 0.91
