## Best Possible Solution

Land a revised full-stack proof runner after reserved backend env keys are protected, generated env files are kept out of artifacts, CI is green, real lane-local Convex proof is attached, and maintainers accept the proof-runner policy change.

  - body: `lane_env_file` is written under `$remote_out`, and artifact collection copies that output directory.   - confidence: 0.9

  - confidence: 0.88
