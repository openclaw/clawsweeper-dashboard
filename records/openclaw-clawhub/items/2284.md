## Summary

## Best Possible Solution

Land the fail-closed owner recheck and monotonic marker hardening after extending retimestamping and tests to cover legacy ban-hidden rows.

Yes. Current main's restore batch lacks an owner-state recheck, and the PR body includes live Convex output for the after-fix stale-page abort path; the legacy retimestamp gap is source-reproducible from the PR diff and tests.

## Solution Assessment

Not yet. Re-reading the owner and keeping ban markers monotonic is the right narrow fix, but the retimestamp guard should also cover legacy user.banned rows without moderationStatus to match the PR's own legacy restore support.
