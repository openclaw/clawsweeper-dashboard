Keep the released exact-publisher `officialPublishers` authorization model, which removes mutable handles and organization membership from the Official trust boundary.

## Solution Assessment

No; although the PR identifies the correct security problem, the released publisher-scoped record model is narrower and stronger than maintaining a mutable handle allowlist with reservation checks.
