## Repair Work Prompt

Implement a conservative false-positive appeal affordance for suspicious ClawHub skills. Add an owner-facing appeal link on the suspicious skill detail/security surfaces that includes safe context such as owner handle/id, slug, current version, canonical skill URL, and high-level scanner statuses. Prefer a private mailto to security@openclaw.ai or another maintainer-approved target over a public URL containing detailed scanner output. Keep the appeal flow distinct from owner/admin rescan, generic user report/moderation reports, and staff override behavior. Do not change scanner verdict logic, moderation policy, schema, report-count semantics, or auto-hide behavior. Add or update focused tests for the skill detail page and DetailSecuritySummary to cover appeal link visibility and target.
