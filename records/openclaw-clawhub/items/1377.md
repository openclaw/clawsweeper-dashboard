## What This Changes

The issue asks ClawHub to show a skill packaged as MIT as MIT with attribution-required copy instead of MIT-0/no-attribution copy.

## Best Possible Solution

A maintainer should decide whether ClawHub keeps MIT-0-only skill publishing or designs per-skill license support across schema, publish acceptance, API, UI, docs, and migration/backfill behavior.
