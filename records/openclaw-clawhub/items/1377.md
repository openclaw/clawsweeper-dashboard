This should stay open because changing it safely depends on a platform license policy decision: either enforce the MIT-0 publishing contract explicitly or support per-skill SPDX metadata consistently.

The issue asks ClawHub to show the reported skill as standard MIT instead of MIT-0 because the upstream package metadata and LICENSE declare MIT.
