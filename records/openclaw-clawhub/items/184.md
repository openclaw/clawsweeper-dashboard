Keep open. ClawHub cleanup policy keeps issues open, and current main still lacks a paid expedited human-review certificate or badge program; the existing manual override and badge paths are narrower moderation/admin tools, not a paid trust product.

Maintainers should first decide whether paid expedited human review belongs in ClawHub; if approved, define the trust policy and then implement the end-to-end workflow across schema, payment state, review queue, moderator tools, public API/UI badges, audit records, and docs.
