## Risks / Open Questions

- A paid approval badge could be interpreted as a security guarantee unless its review scope, version binding, expiration, revocation, conflicts-of-interest handling, and appeal language are defined.
- An expedited paid lane could be perceived as pay-to-trust or pay-for-visibility unless review outcome and search ranking remain independent of payment.
- The open plugin-focused RFC overlaps the trust model but does not clearly cover skill-specific payment and review operations, so closing this issue as a duplicate could lose a meaningful product question.
