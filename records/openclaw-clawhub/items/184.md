## What This Changes

Introduce paid expedited human security review for skills and grant passing submissions a visible approval badge.

## Best Possible Solution

Specify a version-bound, expiring, revocable human-review attestation distinct from publisher identity and automated scans, then evaluate charging only for expedited processing—not for approval.

## Risks / Open Questions

- Users may treat a paid approval badge as an ongoing guarantee after code, dependencies, permissions, ownership, or publisher account state changes.
- Payment can undermine trust unless expedited processing is clearly separated from the approval outcome, with transparent rejection and refund rules.
- A credible program requires reviewer qualifications, conflict handling, evidence retention, version binding, expiry, revocation, appeals, incident response, and sustainable review capacity.
