Keep open: ClawHub cleanup policy keeps issues open, and the published third-party `agentic-money` version 1.0.0 still has ambiguous wallet input-sanitization guidance in a transaction-signing flow. This needs skill-owner or security/moderation follow-up rather than source-repo cleanup closure.
