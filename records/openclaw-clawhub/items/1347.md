Restore production GitHub sign-in end to end for active accounts, preserve deleted/banned account rejection, and use the narrower open OAuth trackers such as https://github.com/openclaw/clawhub/issues/1717 and https://github.com/openclaw/clawhub/issues/1552 to split remaining variants after operator triage.
