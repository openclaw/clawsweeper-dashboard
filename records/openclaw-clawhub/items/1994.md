The closely related requests remain open, and ClawHub policy requires this non-implemented product decision to stay open rather than be cleanup-closed.

## What This Changes

Add or document a way for community publishers to obtain a Verified status for their published skill and plugin.

## Best Possible Solution

Define one documented verification program covering eligibility, evidence, reviewer authority, verification scope, renewal and revocation, then expose a consistent application/status workflow for both skills and plugins.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

No, ad hoc verification of one publisher is not the best maintainable solution. ClawHub should first establish a uniform trust policy and operational workflow, while ordinary scan or false-positive problems continue to use the existing rescan path.
