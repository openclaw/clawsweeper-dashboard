## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.86

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Drain all expired search dedupe rows:** `convex/searchTelemetry.ts:190-199`
  - body: The new retention job only deletes the first `batchSize` expired dedupe documents and then returns. Since the cron runs once per day with `batchSize: 500`, any day with more than 500 expired rows leaves the rest past the intended 14-day retention window, and the backlog can grow indefinitely. Please continue pruning when a full batch is deleted, or switch to a cursor/action loop that drains the expired range safely.
  - confidence: 0.88
