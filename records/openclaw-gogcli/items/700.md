## Risks / Open Questions

- Existing Pub/Sub push users that relied on hook failures returning HTTP success and advancing the cursor will now get retry/backlog behavior during downstream hook outages unless maintainers explicitly accept that policy change.
- The new pull runtime adds Google Pub/Sub subscriber credentials and a direct `cloud.google.com/go/pubsub/v2` dependency alongside the existing Gmail OAuth path.
- The ack/nack policy is message-delivery-sensitive: invalid, wrong-account, stale, and no-new-message notifications are terminal, while hook, Gmail, and rate-limit failures are retryable.
- This stack is coordinated with https://github.com/openclaw/rfcs/pull/8 and https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/90723, so merge order and product acceptance matter before landing.
- The proof is strong for controlled delivery and retry/rollback, but it intentionally does not claim long-running production soak or high-volume backpressure behavior.
