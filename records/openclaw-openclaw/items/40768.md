The issue reports Feishu group mention delivery failing when multiple app-scoped bot identities share a group and mention open_id does not match the receiving bot's self open_id.

## Best Possible Solution

Repair and review the linked Feishu identity PR or replace it with a current-main Feishu-channel fix that proves mention detection, mention stripping, and group admission across multi-bot accounts, while keeping the self-mention stripping issue separate unless one patch intentionally covers both.
