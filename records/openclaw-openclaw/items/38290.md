Land a refreshed patch that accepts pathless exact opaque extension origins, keeps path/query/hash allowlist entries fail-closed, and proves both the exact match and non-match cases in Gateway unit and handshake tests.

## Solution Assessment

No as submitted. The fallback belongs in `checkBrowserOrigin`, but the allowlist-side normalization must keep malformed or path-bearing entries fail-closed before this is the best fix.
