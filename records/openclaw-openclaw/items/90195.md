## Summary

## What This Changes

## Best Possible Solution

Land the hardening only after execute probing is guarded before wrapping, with a regression for sibling preservation and an explicit maintainer-owned decision on the stricter stable-name boundary.

No, not yet: stable-name pinning and safe descriptor reads are the right layer, but the best fix must also remove the pre-validation direct execute read or the stated sibling-isolation behavior remains incomplete.
