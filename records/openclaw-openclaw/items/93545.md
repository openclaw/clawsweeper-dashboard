- **[P1] Avoid replaying chunks after a partial rich send:** `extensions/telegram/src/send.ts:817-823`

With this catch around the whole rich send, if chunk 1 is accepted and chunk 2 fails, the fallback sends the entire legacy plan again, duplicating the already-delivered content. Fall back only before any rich message has been sent, or track unsupported-method failure per chunk without replaying delivered chunks.
  - confidence: 0.91

Move the fallback to a shared rich transport helper or cover those sibling paths with tests.
  - confidence: 0.84
