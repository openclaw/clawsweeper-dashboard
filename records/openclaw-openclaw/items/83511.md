## Summary

## What This Changes

The issue requests deferring Telegram visible text delivery for final-mode TTS so a successful voice response can be sent once with the text as a caption instead of producing text-then-voice churn.

## Best Possible Solution

Review and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/83988, or a maintainer-approved replacement, while keeping separate-delivery policy tracked outside this bug.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. Current-main and latest-release source inspection show visible text is delivered before the accumulated final TTS voice payload on the ACP and generic dispatch paths.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Capability-gated text deferral for caption-capable voice delivery targets the implicated dispatch layer and preserves fallback text when synthesis or delivery fails.
