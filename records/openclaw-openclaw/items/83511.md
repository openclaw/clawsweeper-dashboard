## Summary

## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to avoid Telegram final-mode TTS text-before-voice churn by deferring visible text until a captioned voice payload or fallback can be delivered.

## Best Possible Solution

Review and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/83988, or a maintainer-approved replacement, while keeping the separate delivery preference tracked in https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/42539 as a distinct product decision.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Capability-gated dispatch-layer deferral is the narrow maintainable shape because Telegram already supports captioned voice delivery while fallback text can remain in the dispatcher when synthesis fails.
