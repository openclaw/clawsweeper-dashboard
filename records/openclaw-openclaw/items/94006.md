## Best Possible Solution

Land the centralized `scripts/pr` environment normalization through the normal maintainer PR flow once exact-head checks remain green.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. Source inspection plus `gh help environment` gives a high-confidence path: inherit `GH_FORCE_TTY` or forced color settings into `scripts/pr` and the helper `gh --json` calls can receive terminal-style/colorized behavior unless the wrapper clears them.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Setting the environment once in the top-level wrapper before sourcing the helper libraries is the narrowest maintainable fix; per-call `gh` wrappers would duplicate policy across `scripts/pr-lib/*`.
