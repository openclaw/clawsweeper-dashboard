## Security Review

Status: needs_attention

Summary: The PR is security-sensitive and currently introduces concrete credential-store downgrade, format-stability, and write-hardening concerns.

Concerns:

- **[high] Sealed auth store can be downgraded to plaintext:** `src/infra/sealed-json-file.ts:113`
  - body: A missing passphrase can make the sealed store load as absent, then `saveSealedJsonFile` writes plaintext over the target during migration or sync. That breaks the PR's fail-closed claim and can expose or lose credentials.

- **[medium] KDF parameters are implicit and not stored:** `src/infra/sealed-json-file.ts:39`
  - body: The AES-GCM envelope omits scrypt parameters, making the credential format harder to audit and risky to tune or preserve across runtime changes.
  - confidence: 0.84
