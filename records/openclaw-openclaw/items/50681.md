Review, land, or replace https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/50682 with a narrow Telegram preflight implementation that updates config/schema/docs, shares behavior across normal messages and native commands, preserves topic delivery, constrains auto-leave, and proves cooldown plus leave behavior.
