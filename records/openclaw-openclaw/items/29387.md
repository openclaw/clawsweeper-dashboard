The issue asks OpenClaw to stop silently ignoring bootstrap-named files placed under a per-agent agentDir, either by loading them with clear precedence or by warning and documenting that only workspace bootstrap files are injected.

## Best Possible Solution

Land a bounded warning or doctor diagnostic for bootstrap-named files misplaced in agentDir while keeping workspace files canonical, unless maintainers explicitly approve agentDir merge or override semantics.
