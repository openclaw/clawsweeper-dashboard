Keep this as the umbrella tracker while maintainers review or replace the session-concurrency PR, leave the warning threshold to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/14747, and design auto-yield with explicit cancellation and transcript-ordering semantics.

Unclear as a single patch. Session concurrency, lane warning configuration, scheduler fairness, and automatic yield semantics are separable pieces, and auto-yield needs maintainer decisions around cancellation and transcript ordering before implementation.
