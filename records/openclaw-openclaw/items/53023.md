Keep this open as the umbrella scheduler/product decision; let https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/86676 or https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/14747 handle the diagnostic-threshold slice, and design the session concurrency and auto-yield semantics before implementation.
