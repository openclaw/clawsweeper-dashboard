## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.9

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Publish WS status from SDK readiness callbacks:** `extensions/feishu/src/monitor.transport.ts:270-276`
  - body: The pinned Feishu SDK does not make start() a handshake barrier: start() assigns the dispatcher and calls reConnect(true), while onReady/onReconnected are the connection callbacks. Publishing connected:true here can mark a slow or failed first handshake healthy, so wire status to the SDK lifecycle callbacks instead.
  - confidence: 0.93
- **[P2] Do not mark app events as transport liveness:** `extensions/feishu/src/monitor.message-handler.ts:332`
  - body: lastTransportActivityAt drives stale-socket restarts, and the shared policy says ordinary app events are not socket liveness. Keep Feishu app messages as lastEventAt unless the plugin also has recurring transport or heartbeat proof, otherwise quiet healthy WebSocket accounts can be restarted after the timestamp ages out.
  - confidence: 0.88
