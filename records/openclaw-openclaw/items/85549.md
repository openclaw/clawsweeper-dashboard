## Best Possible Solution

Land the provider-local heuristic after inspectable redacted Foundry proof shows DeepSeek-V4-Pro and DeepSeek-V4-Flash succeed on Responses; if maintainers want catalog-tight safety, restrict the match to exact proven IDs.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Mostly yes: the provider-owned helper is the right boundary and the current `deepseek-v4` prefix is much narrower than the earlier all-DeepSeek shape. The remaining gap is proof and, if maintainers want stricter compatibility, exact-ID scoping.
