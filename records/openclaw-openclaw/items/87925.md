Make the model-switch thinking contract explicit, then update sessions.patch, /model persistence, notification behavior, docs, and focused regression tests together to match that contract.

Unclear. The mechanical fix is bounded, but the best solution depends on the maintainer choice between preserving the stored preference and normalizing stored state for the active provider/model.
