Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Keep discovery private-network blocked by default:** `extensions/github-copilot/discovery.ts:98-99`
  - body: `configuredProvider.baseUrl` is passed into discovery, but this policy uses `ssrfPolicyFromHttpBaseUrlAllowedHostname`, whose `allowedHostnames` path skips private-network checks.
