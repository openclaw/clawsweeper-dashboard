Request that Discord channel/thread messages send an immediate accepted-message typing indicator when `agents.defaults.typingMode` is `"instant"`, before later session, memory, queue, or preflight work can make the bot appear idle.

Keep the remaining work in https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/79104 and land or replace https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/76091 with focused Discord accepted-typing coverage for actionable guild/channel/thread targets.
