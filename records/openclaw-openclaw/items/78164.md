## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.89

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Preserve live lock owners before reclaiming session-store locks:** `src/config/sessions/store-writer.ts:139-140`
  - body: This branch treats a PID-owned lock as stale once its `createdAt` is older than the stale timeout, then removes it. Current main's lock helper intentionally does not reclaim PID-owned locks from timestamp age alone because a long-running live writer may still own the file; removing it lets another process enter the writer and can lose session-store updates. Use the shared `withFileLock`/`shouldRemoveDeadOwnerOrExpiredLock` semantics or equivalent owner-liveness proof before deleting the lock.
  - confidence: 0.91
