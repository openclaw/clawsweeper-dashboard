## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes, with a compatibility caveat. The shared diagnostics-otel log-record builder is the right export layer for OTLP and stdout, the logger layer is the right place to reserve diagnostic semantics, and OpenTelemetry 0.219.0 supports `LogRecord.eventName`.
