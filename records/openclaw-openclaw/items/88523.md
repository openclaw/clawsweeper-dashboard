The branch persists a `lastHandoffPending` delivery flag and uses it in the cleanup and resume retry guards so parent-busy completion handoffs retry until the existing hard-expiry window, with regression coverage.

## Best Possible Solution

Land the scoped retry-accounting fix after maintainer review and required checks, keeping the hard-expiry bound and the existing caps for non-pending and non-completion failures.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Persisting the transient pending handoff outcome and sharing it between both guards is narrower than raising global retry caps or timeouts, and it keeps the hard-expiry bound intact.
