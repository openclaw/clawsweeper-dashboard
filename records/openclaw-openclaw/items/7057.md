## Best Possible Solution

Keep the deterministic replacement suites shipped in v2026.6.5; any future WSL-specific failure should be reported against the current `agent-tools.*` tests with fresh logs rather than restoring broad platform skips.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Isolating global cwd and process execution preserves the behavioral assertions and is more deterministic than skipping the suites on WSL.
