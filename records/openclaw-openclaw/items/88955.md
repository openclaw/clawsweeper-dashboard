The issue reports that qqbot WebSocket reconnects after QQ 4009 session timeouts leave heartbeat and cron announce outbound delivery failing while inbound and normal replies continue to work.

## Best Possible Solution

Fix the qqbot reconnect lifecycle so outbound delivery remains available after a 4009 reconnect, preferably with a regression test that simulates reconnect and then sends heartbeat or cron-style durable outbound delivery through qqbot.

## Solution Assessment
