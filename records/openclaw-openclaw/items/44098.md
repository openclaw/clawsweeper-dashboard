## Best Possible Solution

Land this or a narrow replacement after maintainers approve the PID cap and opt-out contract, with resolver, schema/types, docs, tests, and changelog updated together while leaving broader CPU/memory defaults to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/60827.
