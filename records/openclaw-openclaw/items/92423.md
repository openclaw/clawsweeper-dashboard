## What This Changes

## Best Possible Solution

Publish maintainer-approved guidance that separates diagnostics telemetry from trusted local raw-text capture, recommends the right hook surface per use case, points to the open hook-coverage issues, and documents WhatsApp allowlist/outbound-only caveats for CRM-style local logging.
