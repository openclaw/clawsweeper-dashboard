## Summary

Keep open: https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/91241 fixed the budget-deferred retry inflation, but current main and v2026.6.8 still have no Feishu reconnect or ready-state drain, and startup recovery still runs independently of Feishu transport readiness. The remaining retry reset and readiness behavior needs maintainer policy plus live Feishu restart proof.

## What This Changes

Ensure pending Feishu outbound deliveries survive gateway restarts by replaying only after transport readiness and avoiding phantom retry exhaustion.

## Best Possible Solution

Add a Feishu-owned transport-ready or reconnect delivery drain through the existing delivery-queue runtime seam, while preserving the merged budget-deferral fix and making any retry reset policy explicit.

## Reproduction Assessment

Partly. The retry-accounting part is source-proven and already fixed, while the remaining Feishu readiness gap is source-reproducible from the missing Feishu reconnect drain but still needs live Feishu restart proof.

## Solution Assessment

No. The local fixed sleep and unconditional retry reset are useful debugging context, but the maintainable fix is a channel-owned readiness or reconnect drain plus an explicit retry policy.
