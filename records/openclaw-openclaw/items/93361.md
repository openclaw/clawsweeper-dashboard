## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Yes, this is the right owner boundary for the proposed fix: OpenShell owns mirror-mode remote roots, and the PR routes the relevant OpenShell mirror mutations through an extension-local pinned helper. The remaining decision is whether maintainers accept the stricter symlink-component behavior without live SSH proof.
