## Best Possible Solution

Extend the same non-delivery protection to the established `cron-event` and `exec-event` system provider ids, keep the webchat/heartbeat/runtime proof, and then land after CI and maintainer session-state review.

## Solution Assessment

No, not yet. The shared merge-helper location is the right layer, but the implementation should use the complete established system-event/non-delivery provider contract before merge.
