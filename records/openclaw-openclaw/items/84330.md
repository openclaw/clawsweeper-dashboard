## Repair Work Prompt

Fix the iMessage docs gap reported in https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/84330. Update `docs/channels/imessage.md` near the remote Mac/custom `channels.imessage.cliPath` wrapper section to state that wrappers must behave as transparent long-lived stdio pipes for JSON-RPC: promptly forward stdin/stdout chunks or lines, preserve newlines, avoid EOF-only or large fixed-size blocking reads, and keep stderr separate from JSON-RPC stdout. Mention the observed symptom `imsg rpc timeout (chats.list)` as a troubleshooting clue. Keep the change docs-only, do not change runtime behavior or config schema, and keep wording concise and production-focused.
