- **[P1] Preserve successful manual due slots after recompute:** `src/cron/service/ops.ts:948`
  - body: Passing `isManual: true` here stops `applyJobResult` from deleting the job, but `finishPreparedManualRun` still calls `recomputeNextRunsForMaintenance(..., { recomputeExpired: true })` afterward.
