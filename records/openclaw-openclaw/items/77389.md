The issue requests that Telegram preview/progress drafts stop persisting or stacking after an OpenClaw restart and always finalize or clear into the final response.

## Best Possible Solution

Fix the Telegram plugin draft lifecycle so active or archived previews are finalized or cleaned after lost-ID and restart-like paths, with regression coverage and no new config workaround.
