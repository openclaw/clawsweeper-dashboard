## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.86

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Downgrade reaction system events before waking:** `extensions/telegram/src/bot-handlers.runtime.ts:1537-1540`
  - body: `message_reaction` updates can be accepted from non-owner users under open/all or group allowlist configs, but the new wake path enqueues the reaction without `forceSenderIsOwnerFalse`.
