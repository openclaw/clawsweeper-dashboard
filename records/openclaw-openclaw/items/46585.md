## What This Changes

The issue reports that macOS iPhone Continuity Camera captures from the rear camera for both `--facing front` and `--facing back` when an explicit Continuity `device-id` is passed.

## Best Possible Solution

Define the macOS Continuity Camera `deviceId` plus `facing` contract, then either refine selection when front/back Continuity devices are exposed or return a clear unsupported-facing diagnostic with docs for single external Continuity devices.
