Full review comments:

- **[P1] Keep plugin entry pages inside the sandbox:** `ui/src/ui/app-render.helpers.ts:352-362`
  - body: The `same-window` and `new-window` paths navigate directly to `/plugins/<id>/...` on the Control UI origin.
