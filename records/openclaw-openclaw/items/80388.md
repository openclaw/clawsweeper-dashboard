## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Keep plugin entry pages inside the sandbox:** `ui/src/ui/app-render.helpers.ts:352-361`
  - body: The same-window and new-window paths navigate directly to `/plugins/<id>/...` on the Control UI origin. Keep v1 entries sandboxed or move top-level plugin pages to an isolated origin before exposing these modes.
