Full review comments:

- **[P1] Mint launch tokens with entry-scoped permissions:** `src/gateway/server-methods/plugin-host-hooks.ts:145-148`
  - body: This launch path finds the requested entry point, but then stores the caller's entire scope list in the launch token.
