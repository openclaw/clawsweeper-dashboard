Keep open. ## What This Changes

The issue reports that completed or timed-out subagent work can fail to notify the requester, lose visible channel delivery, or claim missing files without verification.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the broad tracking issue until OpenClaw has a durable async completion contract covering child completion, requester resumption, external delivery confirmation, timeout/restart or explicit-failure policy, idempotent replay, deduplication, and result/file verification.

## Solution Assessment
