## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the canonical WhatsApp reconnect missed-message tracker, then land one plugin-owned bounded recovery policy with live WhatsApp proof: either validated history/backfill for eligible monitored chats or an explicit unrecoverable-gap warning when backfill cannot be trusted.
