Keep open. Current main and the latest release still skip stale WhatsApp `append` catch-up messages and have no reconnect-history listener or cursor, while two open WhatsApp PRs cover competing replay policies that need maintainer choice and live Baileys proof.

The issue requests WhatsApp reconnect backfill so missed group messages during outage windows are delivered or at least logged after reconnection.

## Best Possible Solution

Land one bounded WhatsApp-plugin reconnect recovery design that filters to eligible monitored chats, caps replay, dedupes by message ID, preserves startup-history safety, documents Baileys limits, and proves replay or gap-warning behavior with real reconnect evidence.
