- `impact:data-loss`: Completed generated narratives are silently discarded and replaced by fallback snippets.

## Summary

The current-main defect is the same delayed post-wait session-message visibility race tracked by the canonical issue, and a clean, proof-positive open PR already provides the focused bounded-read repair; the distinct early-archive race remains separately tracked.

## What This Changes

Prevent memory-core dreaming from substituting fallback snippets when completed narrative text becomes readable shortly after the subagent wait returns.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/87306 canonical for delayed message visibility, review and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/89091, and continue handling archived-session recovery separately under https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/87182.
