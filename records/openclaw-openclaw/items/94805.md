## Risks / Open Questions

- Merging creates a new documented core CLI and snapshot manifest/artifact contract that operators may depend on for sync and recovery.
- Global and per-agent snapshots operate on databases that can contain live session, auth-adjacent, plugin, and agent state; restore misuse could affect user state even though artifacts are verified and targets must be fresh.
