## Summary

This PR adds useful plugin-health visibility with strong live proof, but the refreshed head still has two blocking defects: persisted records accept unverifiable process identities, and arbitrary `/status <text>` messages are intentionally intercepted instead of reaching the agent.

## What This Changes

The PR adds compact plugin health to `/status`, detailed health through `/status plugins`, typed channel setup diagnostics, and SQLite-backed cross-process quarantine records.

## Best Possible Solution

Recognize the exact `plugins` subcommand without reserving unknown `/status` suffixes, and surface persisted cross-process records only when the recorder process incarnation can be positively verified through a portable identity or equivalent fail-closed rule.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. The changed handler and command-control test explicitly establish that `/status please` becomes a local command, while the liveness helper directly establishes that missing or unreadable process start-time identities are accepted.

## Solution Assessment

No. Plugin health can be exposed through exact subcommand recognition without changing unknown-suffix routing, and persisted health should fail closed when process incarnation cannot be established.
