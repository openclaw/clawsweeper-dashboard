## Reproduction Assessment

Yes for the patch defect from source inspection: configure account `a` with `tools.bitable=false`, account `b` with `tools.bitable=true`, register Bitable tools, then execute from `agentAccountId: "a"`. The aggregate registration gate leaves the tool callable and the executor resolves account `a` without rechecking its Bitable gate.

## Solution Assessment

No. Registration gating is the right direction, but the maintainable fix must also enforce the resolved account's Bitable/Base gate and prove the disabled-config runtime behavior before merge.
