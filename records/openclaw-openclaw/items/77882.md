- **[P1] Normalize aliases before merging account tools:** `extensions/feishu/src/accounts.ts:195`
  - body: With `channels.feishu.tools.bitable=true` and `channels.feishu.accounts.a.tools.base=false`, the new nested merge leaves both keys on the account tools object, and `resolveToolsConfig` makes `bitable` win.
