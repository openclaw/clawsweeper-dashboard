Keep open. The report is recent, detailed, and still not proven fixed on current main: OpenClaw records liveness stalls and has channel-local recovery paths, but the cross-channel event-loop blocker and missed-reply behavior are not covered by a shipped or main-only fix.

## What This Changes

The issue reports gateway-wide Node event-loop stalls in v2026.4.29 that delay or drop replies and cause Telegram, Slack, and WhatsApp transport failures.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open as the cross-channel incident tracker, collect a stability bundle plus CPU profile from an affected run, and split fixes only after the root blocker is proven: core event-loop/session-lane work in core, tool/auth hot-path work under the related focused tracker, and WhatsApp 428 handling under #75736.
