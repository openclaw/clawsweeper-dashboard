## What This Changes

The PR adds queued JSONL rotation and retention settings for cache-trace and Anthropic payload diagnostics, with config/env/schema/docs/changelog updates and targeted tests.

## Best Possible Solution

Land the diagnostic JSONL rotation work after bounding archive-count inputs, leaving gateway.err.log, journald, and session JSONL starvation to their separate reports.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, with one small hardening fix. Extending the shared queued writer and wiring the two diagnostic sinks is the narrow maintainable solution, but maxArchives should be capped or schema-limited before it drives filesystem work.
