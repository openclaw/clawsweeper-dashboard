## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Not yet as a merge-ready fix. `readProviderJsonResponse` is the right layer, but the hard-coded 64 MiB aggregate cap is a compatibility policy that maintainers need to accept or adjust.
