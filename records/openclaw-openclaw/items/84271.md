## Best Possible Solution

Review and land or replace the linked PR with a narrow middleware fallback that preserves verified `message.send` delivery identifiers while keeping fail-closed behavior for other tools.

Yes, the requested direction is the maintainable fix shape: preserve minimal verified delivery identifiers for non-idempotent sends and surface middleware failure as a warning, while leaving unrelated middleware failures fail-closed.
