The issue asks OpenClaw to make scheduled self-upgrade failures visible, report notification delivery accurately, and recover macOS LaunchAgent gateways after update failures.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and split the fix into conservative macOS update recovery plus cron delivery accounting, with provider-preflight semantics changed only after a focused current-main repro.
