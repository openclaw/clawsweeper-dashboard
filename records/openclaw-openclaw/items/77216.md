  - body: Adding `autoTitle` to `SessionEntry` without adding it to `SESSION_ENTRY_RESERVED_SLOT_KEY_LIST` breaks the repository's reserved-key assertion; CI is already failing with `Type '"autoTitle"' does not satisfy the constraint 'never'`.
