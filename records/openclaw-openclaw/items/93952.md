## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.9

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Prevent timed-out OAuth work from writing after lock release:** `src/agents/auth-profiles/oauth-manager.ts:488-492`
  - body: Wrapping the entire locked callback in `withRefreshCallTimeout` only rejects the promise returned to `withFileLock`; it does not cancel the callback. After that rejection, `withFileLock` releases the global OAuth lock, while the abandoned callback can still continue into credential persistence or mirroring after another refresher owns the lock.
  - confidence: 0.92
- **[P1] Suppress late refreshOperation mutations after deadline:** `src/agents/embedded-agent-runner/run/auth-controller.ts:228-230`
  - body: `withAuthDeadline(refreshOperation, ...)` rejects the single-flight promise but does not cancel `refreshOperation`. If the provider hook later resolves while generation/profile/source still match, the old operation can still update authStorage, model overrides, and `runtimeAuthState` after the handle was cleared or a retry is active.
  - confidence: 0.9
- **[P2] Give the outer auth deadline real headroom:** `src/agents/runtime-auth-refresh.ts:26`
  - body: The PR adds a 150,000 ms OAuth in-lock deadline but keeps the outer runtime auth deadline at 300,000 ms. A caller can wait behind one full in-lock attempt and then spend its own full in-lock budget, leaving no margin for scheduling, lock release, or jitter before a legitimate refresh is timed out.
  - confidence: 0.86
