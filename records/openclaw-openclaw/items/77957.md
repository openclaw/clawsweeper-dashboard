## Summary

Keep open: current main still persists completed voice-call transcripts and tears down call state without any post-call notification, Telegram delivery, or user-visible failure path. The related earlier report was closed as stale, and the currently open related PRs/features do not cover completed-call transcript delivery back to the user.

## What This Changes

Request a voice-call post-call notification or delivery path for completed transcript-bearing inbound calls, especially to Telegram or the originating session.

## Best Possible Solution

Define a plugin-owned post-call completion contract that summarizes completed transcript-bearing calls, delivers to an approved session or channel target with visible failure handling, documents the config/API, and covers realtime close plus call.ended regression paths.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. Source inspection plus the reporter's live evidence show completed realtime calls are persisted and finalized through call.ended without a post-call delivery path; no live Twilio run was executed in this read-only review.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainers choose the delivery policy. A plugin-owned post-call hook or delivery contract is the maintainable direction; an external calls.jsonl scanner or Telegram-only special case would be brittle.
