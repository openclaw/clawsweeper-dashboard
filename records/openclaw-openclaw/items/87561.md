## What This Changes

The issue requests a core contract that sanitized final fallback messages on durable text-capable channels are either delivered with explicit receipt/outcome or reported as failed/suppressed with diagnostics instead of silently disappearing.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the canonical maintainer-owned delivery contract and land or close the narrower WhatsApp, empty-receipt, and silent-completion PRs as slices under the broader SDK/core conformance plan.
