Full review comments:

- **[P2] Clamp compaction waits to the remaining delivery window:** `src/agents/subagent-announce-delivery.ts:290`
  - body: The loop checks `Date.now() < compactionDeadlineMs` before sleeping, but then waits the full scheduled delay and retries even if that delay carries the call past `deliveryTimeoutMs`. With a small configured announce timeout, or when only a little time remains, this can still steer after the timeout instead of falling back/canceling within the promised delivery window. Clamp the delay to the remaining time and break once the deadline has elapsed before another queue attempt.
