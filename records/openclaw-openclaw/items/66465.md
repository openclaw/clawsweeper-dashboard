## Risks / Open Questions

- Persisting child stderr can retain provider tokens, bearer headers, private keys, local paths, proxy/bootstrap diagnostics, or other sensitive values unless redaction and access expectations are explicit.
- The proposed stderrLogDir and stderrLogRetentionHours settings would add ACPX configuration surface, which needs maintainer agreement under the repo's high bar for new config options.

- The scoped ACPX policy says shared ACP runtime behavior should land in openclaw/acpx first; an OpenClaw-only wrapper patch would risk drift from the published runtime.
