## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.86

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Match collisions against the final redirected request:** `extensions/matrix/src/matrix/sdk/transport.ts:254-255`
  - body: `fetchWithMatrixGuardedRedirects` follows redirects and can change POST to GET for 301/302/303 responses, but the rewrite still checks the original `url` and initial method. Please match against the final URL/effective method and add a redirected pass-through test.
