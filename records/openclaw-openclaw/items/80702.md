Keep the rehydration sanitizer direction, but match the existing managed-block boundary semantics, strengthen the fuzzy-straddle regression to prove clean promotion, and land it with real workspace proof while leaving the CJK half to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/80620.
