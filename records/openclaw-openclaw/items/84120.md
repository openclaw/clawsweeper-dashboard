## What This Changes

## Best Possible Solution

Patch or consume a fixed upstream Signal typing runtime, then update OpenClaw's Signal install/runtime path and validate 1:1 and group typing separately.

Yes on direction, but not as an OpenClaw-only TypeScript patch. The reporter's preferred upstream/dependency fix appears to target the right layer, while OpenClaw needs a maintainer choice on waiting for upstream, consuming a fixed signal-cli release, or carrying a temporary patched runtime.
