The issue asks OpenClaw to resume and complete an active user turn after automatic compaction instead of silently abandoning or hiding the final response.

## Best Possible Solution

Define one tested compaction-resume contract that preserves active turn identity, retry state, originating delivery context, and final-reply obligations, then coordinate the narrower delivery-race and cron/system retry-loop fixes under that contract.
