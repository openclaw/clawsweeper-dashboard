The issue reports that automatic compaction during an active turn can lose or fail to deliver the pending user-facing response instead of resuming the original task.

## Best Possible Solution

Define one compaction-resume contract that preserves active turn identity, originating delivery context, retry-produced output flushing, and pending final-reply obligations across auto-compaction and internal continuations.
