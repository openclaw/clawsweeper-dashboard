## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial denoising for transcript scaffolding, but REM reflection ranking still rewards repeated concept tags and Dream Diary still consumes those recurrence-shaped themes without a semantic-importance selector; related open issues overlap but do not fully subsume this REM/Dream Diary material-selection request.

## What This Changes

Request Dreaming material selection that prioritizes semantically important decisions, preferences, conclusions, and unusual events over high-frequency transcript terms.

## Best Possible Solution

Add an internal material-importance scorer before REM reflections and Dream Diary input, coordinate it with the open Dreaming scoring/contamination work, and avoid adding a public promptStyle knob until the ranking contract is clear.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level: current main still builds REM themes from repeated concept-tag counts and feeds those themes into Dream Diary. A high-confidence live reproduction of the reporter's exact output is not available because their private corpus is not in the repo.

## Solution Assessment

Partly. A semantic-density or material-importance selector before REM and Dream Diary is the maintainable direction, but adding a new promptStyle/config knob first would expose product surface before fixing the internal ranking problem.
