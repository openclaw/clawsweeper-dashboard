## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial denoising for transcript scaffolding, but REM still selects/refines material from recurrence, recall, score, and concept-tag counts rather than semantic importance, so the central Dreaming quality complaint is not resolved.

## What This Changes

The issue asks Dreaming to prioritize meaningful user decisions, preferences, conclusions, and unusual events over high-frequency transcript terms when selecting material for REM and Dream Diary summaries.

## Best Possible Solution

Add an internal material-importance scorer before REM reflections and Dream Diary input, coordinate it with the open Dreaming hygiene/scoring work, and avoid adding a public promptStyle knob until the internal ranking problem is fixed.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level: current main still builds REM themes and Dream Diary inputs from recurrence/count-based signals without a semantic-importance selector. A full live reproduction of the reporter's private corpus is not available in this read-only review.

## Solution Assessment

Partly: the semantic-density/material-importance selector direction is maintainable, but a public promptStyle/config knob would be premature before the internal selection and hygiene rules are improved.
