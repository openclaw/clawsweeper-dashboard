## What This Changes

The issue reports that `/v1/chat/completions` SSE can drop a leading `<` and close before the final streamed assistant fragment when XML-style tags are split across chunks.

## Best Possible Solution

Review and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/82355, or an equivalent narrow patch, with proof that SSE emits the literal leading `<` and the final content fragment before `[DONE]`; then close this issue from the merged fix.
