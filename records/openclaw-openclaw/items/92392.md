Full review comments:

- **[P1] Avoid double-running Telegram message_sending hooks:** `src/auto-reply/dispatch.ts:553`
  - body: Adding `buildMessageSendingBeforeDeliver` here makes Telegram run `message_sending` in the dispatcher hook and then again in `deliverReplies`, because Telegram supplies an identity `beforeDeliver` and its delivery layer already calls `runMessageSending`.
