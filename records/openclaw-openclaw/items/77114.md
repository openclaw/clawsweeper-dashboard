## Best Possible Solution

Land the focused WhatsApp helper sanitizer after normal merge gates, while leaving broader cross-channel filename hardening to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/72973.

Yes. Centralizing the strip in `resolveWhatsAppDocumentFileName` is the narrowest maintainable fix for the affected WhatsApp document filename boundary and keeps MIME fallback behavior intact.
