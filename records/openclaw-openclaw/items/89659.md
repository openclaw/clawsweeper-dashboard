## Solution Assessment

Yes as an acceptable mitigation, not a full queueing solution. Bounded retry in the Feishu send helper is narrow enough for common transient 230020 bursts, but maintainers should explicitly accept the shared-helper scope and the remaining extreme reply-burst limitation.
