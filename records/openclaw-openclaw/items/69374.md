## Best Possible Solution

Land a narrow additive WhatsApp-owned fix, preferably by reviewing or adapting #46291, that extracts forwarded status and score, carries them through the normalized inbound context, and exposes them as untrusted channel metadata on both plugin hook events with focused tests.

## Solution Assessment
