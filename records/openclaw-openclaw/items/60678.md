## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.88

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Keep MP3 files out of the Opus upload path:** `extensions/feishu/src/media.ts:542-545`
  - body: This routes `.mp3` and `audio/mpeg` through `fileType: "opus"`/`msgType: "audio"` while uploading the original MP3 bytes. Feishu voice uploads expect Opus data, so this can reject the upload or create broken playback; current main’s safer behavior is to transcode for voice intent or fall back to a generic file.
  - confidence: 0.9
- **[P2] Clear the Ollama timeout timer on fetch errors:** `extensions/ollama/src/stream.ts:711-738`
  - body: The new timeout is cleared only after `fetch` resolves successfully. DNS, connect, TLS, or other fetch failures skip that cleanup and can leave a pending timer until it fires, which can keep the process alive under repeated failures; move cleanup into a `finally` path.
  - confidence: 0.86
