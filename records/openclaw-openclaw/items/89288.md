## What This Changes

The PR makes `openclaw sessions cleanup` preview and apply sweeps prune stale deleted/reset transcript archives while honoring the separate reset-archive retention setting.

## Best Possible Solution

Share one archive-expiration/removal contract between normal store maintenance and explicit cleanup, retain dry-run reporting, and prove both preview and apply against a full current-head checkout for default, longer, shorter, and disabled reset retention.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level with high confidence: current main feeds only `pruneAfterMs` into the unreferenced-artifact sweep, so a reset archive older than that cutoff is eligible even when reset retention is longer or disabled. No real current-main CLI run was performed during this read-only review.

## Solution Assessment

No, not in its current shape. Threading the separate retention value fixes the immediate semantics, but duplicating the archive-expiration policy in `disk-budget.ts` is less maintainable than sharing the established archive cleanup contract.
