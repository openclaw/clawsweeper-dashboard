The issue asks for media-understanding to validate configured image-capability claims for provider models and produce deterministic, actionable diagnostics when image routing fails.

Land a focused media-understanding fix that rejects or skips known catalog text-only image candidates, preserves legitimate unknown/custom image-capable config, and logs failed attempts with provider/model plus full capability detail.
