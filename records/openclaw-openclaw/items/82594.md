The issue reports that Windows-native `openclaw onboard` can appear stuck for around 10 minutes while loading OpenCode Zen catalog models during QuickStart onboarding.

## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Unclear. The requested investigation is appropriate, but the safest fix should be narrower than broad plugin validation changes: first add stage timing/progress and then scope or timeout catalog discovery where the evidence points.
