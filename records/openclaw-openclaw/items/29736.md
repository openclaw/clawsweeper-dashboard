Keep the shipped canonical state-dir resolution and doctor-owned migration, with runtime failing closed whenever legacy approval policy has not yet been migrated.

Yes. A single canonical state-root resolver plus doctor migration and fail-closed legacy handling is narrower and safer than a runtime fallback or shared global approvals file.
