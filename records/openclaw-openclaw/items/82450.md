Keep open. Current main still centers chat around sessions, session pickers, `/new`/`/reset`, and session-list tooling; it does not provide the requested first-class linear persistent workspace for blind users, and the right fix needs accessibility/product design rather than conservative cleanup.

Requested an accessibility-focused linear persistent workspace mode with screen-reader-friendly timeline, jump, bookmark, and recovery behavior for blind users.
