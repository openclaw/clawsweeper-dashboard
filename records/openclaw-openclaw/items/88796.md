## Solution Assessment

Yes, this appears to be the narrowest maintainable fix: it stays in the Discord plugin, keeps search guild-scoped, derives the missing guild from channel info, and preserves read-target authorization. Adding currentGuildId to core/tool context is a plausible alternative, but it expands protocol/core surface for a plugin-local bug.
