## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.9

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Do not union accounts after disabled-plugin reloads:** `src/gateway/server-reload-handlers.ts:487`
  - body: When a plugin reload disables or removes a channel, the pre-replace stop can leave that account recorded as `restartPending`, and the final loop skips the restart because the channel is absent from `activeChannels`. On a later plugin reload, this unconditional `includeKnownAccounts` call unions that stale account into the fresh plugin list and can restart an account the operator removed while the plugin was disabled. Gate the safety net to channels that stayed active across the reload, or clear the preserved known-account state for intentionally inactive channels.
  - confidence: 0.88
