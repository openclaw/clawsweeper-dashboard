## Best Possible Solution

Review https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/89155, settle the opt-in config contract and identifier-egress policy, then either land that provider-owned implementation or explicitly decline the feature.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainer review. A provider-owned, default-off wrapper is the likely maintainable boundary, but the final config path/name and raw-versus-derived identifier policy are product/security decisions.
