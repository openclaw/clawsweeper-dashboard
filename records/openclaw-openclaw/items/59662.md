## What This Changes

## Best Possible Solution

Add provider-owned private sanitation or capture before transcript persistence and channel delivery, after maintainers choose strip-only versus quota capture and confirm the live Anthropic Max payload.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear: the suggested regex strip is only a plausible mitigation because the alert shape may change and quota values may be useful. The best maintainable fix is a provider-owned strip-or-capture path before persistence and delivery, with live payload proof and regression coverage.
