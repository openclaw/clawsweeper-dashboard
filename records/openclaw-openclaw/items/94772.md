  - body: `isGatewayRestartPending()` only covers transient preparation or an unconsumed SIGUSR1 marker.   - confidence: 0.93

If SIGUSR1 restart becomes pending while that await is in flight, the continuation still calls `params.startChannel(name)`, leaving the duplicate-start race open; recheck immediately before starting the channel and cover that interleaving.
