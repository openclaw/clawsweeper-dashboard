Request adding a native `openclaw sessions cleanup` mode to enumerate orphan/unindexed transcript files and safely archive or prune them with audited dry-run metadata.

## Best Possible Solution

Decide whether aggregate cleanup is the supported product answer or extend `sessions cleanup` with an explicit audited archive/prune mode that reports per-file evidence and preserves current store safety rules.
