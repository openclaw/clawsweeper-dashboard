The issue requests a developer workflow that makes missing `FIELD_LABELS` entries obvious and quick to fix when new `FIELD_HELP` keys are added.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Improving the existing parity-test diagnostic is the narrowest maintainable fix; a standalone write-mode generator would be broader than needed for the acceptance criteria.
