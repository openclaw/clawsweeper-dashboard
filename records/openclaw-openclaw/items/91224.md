The issue asks OpenClaw to prevent internal inter-session confirmation acknowledgements from being delivered to an external user channel while preserving genuine inter-session completions.

## Best Possible Solution

Land a maintainer-approved structural suppression at the delivery boundary or an equivalent upstream metadata contract, with regression coverage for sessions_send and subagent_announce acknowledgements plus a preserved genuine-completion case.
