The issue reports missing Discord tool/skill-phase status reaction updates during multi-step processing, leaving the queued reaction static until final or warning states.

## Best Possible Solution

Land one canonical narrow fix that forwards reaction-only tool-start lifecycle signals with verbose off while preserving suppressed text summaries, then close this issue after merged and proven on Discord.

## Solution Assessment
