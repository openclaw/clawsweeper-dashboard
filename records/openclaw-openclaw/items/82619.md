## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.88

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Watch install-record discovery paths before caching stale snapshots:** `src/plugins/plugin-metadata-snapshot.ts:1064-1075`
  - body: `resolvePluginDiscoveryWatchPaths` only adds discovery roots and configured load paths, but plugin discovery also calls `discoverFromPath` for primary install-record paths.
