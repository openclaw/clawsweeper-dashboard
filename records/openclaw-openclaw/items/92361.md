## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to surface empty `allOf`/`anyOf` tool availability groups as descriptor-authoring warnings instead of silently hiding the tool.

## Best Possible Solution

Land one canonical PR that keeps the evaluator pure, surfaces malformed availability once at descriptor registration or planner integration, and covers empty plus malformed nested group cases before closing this issue.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. Source inspection shows `{ anyOf: [] }` and `{ allOf: [] }` produce `unsupported-signal` diagnostics, and `buildToolPlan` only places them in `hidden` without surfacing a warning or callback.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The issue's requested boundary is the maintainable direction: keep the evaluator side-effect free and surface descriptor-authoring diagnostics once where descriptors are registered or integrated into a plan.
