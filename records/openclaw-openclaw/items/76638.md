The issue reports that long Dreams Diary entries can scroll the date day-chip buttons out of reach, preventing users from switching diary days.

## Best Possible Solution

Land the linked Dreams UI fix or an equivalent narrow change that keeps day chips reachable during long diary scrolls and adds focused regression coverage for the layout behavior.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes, the narrow maintainable fix is to keep the existing day-chip navigation reachable while long diary content scrolls, either by making that rail sticky or by constraining the entry scroll area. The linked PR or a replacement should be reviewed and landed before closing the issue.
