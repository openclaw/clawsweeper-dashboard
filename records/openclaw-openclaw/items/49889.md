## Summary

Keep open. Current main still lacks the requested operator-visible delivery outcome taxonomy: Telegram has internal lane states, but preview-finalized delivery only bridges to an internal sent hook without an outcome field or plugin hook parity, and canceled/retained/skipped paths remain hard to distinguish.

## What This Changes

Request clearer observable Telegram partial-stream final delivery outcomes such as fresh send, preview finalized, preview retained, hook-canceled, transport-failed, and intentionally skipped.

## Best Possible Solution

Add a backwards-compatible delivery outcome field or companion delivery event, wire it through Telegram fresh-send, preview-finalized, preview-retained, canceled, failed, and skipped paths, then document the contract for hooks and operators.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. A source-level reproduction is clear on current main: Telegram lane delivery can return preview-finalized or preview-retained, while public/internal hook contexts still only expose generic success/error/messageId state.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The narrow maintainable fix is additive observability on the existing delivery contract, not changing Telegram partial-stream delivery policy or adding a strict-mode config.
