## Summary

## Best Possible Solution

Land the scoped memory-wiki templating after redacted real behavior proof, fresh or accepted merge gates, and maintainer acceptance of reserved placeholder syntax; keep broader per-agent plugin config design under https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/63829.

## Solution Assessment
