## Summary

Keep open. Current main has useful mitigations for Baileys timing, reconnect draining, and stale runtime-deps cleanup, but liveness warnings still lack blocker attribution and direct WhatsApp auto-replies still bypass durable reconnect-safe replay.

## What This Changes

The issue reports WhatsApp 408 disconnects and dropped direct replies during long event-loop stalls, asking for blocker attribution, reconnect-safe replay, Baileys timing controls, and stale runtime-deps cleanup.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as an active WhatsApp/Gateway reliability investigation: add privacy-safe blocker attribution, remove confirmed synchronous blockers, and route direct WhatsApp replies through durable reconnect-safe delivery with dedupe protection.
