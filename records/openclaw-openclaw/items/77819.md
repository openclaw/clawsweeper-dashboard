## Summary

Keep open: source inspection shows reset archives are retained on disk but are not discoverable through the current Sessions or WebChat history paths, and some media-history shapes can still be dropped without a visible placeholder.

## What This Changes

The issue asks for reset/archived WebChat transcripts to remain visible in history and for historical image attachments to render or show placeholders after reload/reset.

## Best Possible Solution

Expose reset transcript archives through an intentional history surface, and render durable previews or explicit unavailable/omitted placeholders for historical media instead of letting attachments vanish.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, source-level reproduction is clear: create history, call sessions.reset, and the old transcript is rotated while sessions.list and chat.history continue from only the new store entry. The image half is also source-reproducible for omitted or unavailable media blocks, but the exact reporter transcript shape still needs confirmation.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear; the narrow attachment-placeholder piece is straightforward, but archive browsing needs a maintainer decision on whether reset archives appear as session rows, a separate archive view, or a history branch selector.
