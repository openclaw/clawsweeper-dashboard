## Best Possible Solution

Land a corrected Mattermost handler update that normalizes each media candidate independently, adds direct `handleAction` tests for `media`, `filePath`, `path`, and blank-media fallthrough, and keeps https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/67784 open until the related thread and media-root fixes are handled.
