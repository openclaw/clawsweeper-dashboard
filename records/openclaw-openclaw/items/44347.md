Request Google Chat support for keeping bot replies in the originating thread and optionally receiving non-mention messages from spaces.

Keep this issue open until thread placement is fully landed or explicitly split, then design a Google Chat plugin-owned opt-in all-message space delivery mode with OAuth or Workspace Events credentials, safe defaults, and docs.
