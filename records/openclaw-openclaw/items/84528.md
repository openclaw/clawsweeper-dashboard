Keep open: current main and v2026.6.10 already support several OAuth/subscription-style provider routes, but the central request still needs provider-by-provider entitlement, status wording, and billing-mode product decisions rather than a cleanup close.

Keep provider-owned auth and entitlement logic in provider plugins, avoid scraping or subscription bypasses, and let maintainers define a shared status/billing-mode vocabulary only after provider-specific proof exists.
