## Summary

Keep open: current main and the latest release still derive the macOS app Launch at login toggle from live launchd registration, and I did not find durable desired-state persistence or Sparkle post-update reconciliation that would make the reported update-related drift obsolete.

## What This Changes

The issue reports that the macOS app Launch at login preference appears to reset or stop matching actual auto-launch behavior after app updates.

## Best Possible Solution

Persist the user's desired Launch at login setting separately from transient launchd status, then reconcile or repair the app LaunchAgent after app updates without misleading the UI.

## Reproduction Assessment

No high-confidence live reproduction was established in this read-only review. Source inspection does show a plausible current-main path where the UI follows live launchd status instead of a durable desired preference, matching the reported drift shape.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainers choose the product contract for the toggle. The narrow maintainable direction is desired-state persistence plus update-time launchd reconciliation, rather than treating one transient `launchctl print` result as the user preference.
