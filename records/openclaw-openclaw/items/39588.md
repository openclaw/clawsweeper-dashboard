Keep open: current main and v2026.6.6 still derive the macOS app Launch at login toggle from live launchd state, with no separate persisted desired-state or Sparkle post-update reconciliation. Related launchd fixes address nearby gateway or KeepAlive behavior, but not the reported toggle drift after app updates.

## What This Changes

The issue reports that the macOS app Launch at login setting appears to unset after app updates and auto-launch becomes unreliable.

## Best Possible Solution

Make the macOS app own a durable Launch at login desired-state model, reconcile ai.openclaw.mac after signed app replacement, and prove fresh-install plus update behavior on macOS.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes at source level: current main still loads the visible toggle from LaunchAgentManager.status and does not persist a separate desired launch-at-login preference. I did not live-reproduce the signed Sparkle update sequence in this read-only review.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear: persisted desired state plus reconciliation is the likely repair shape, but maintainers still need to choose the permanent macOS startup contract and mechanism before automation is safe.
