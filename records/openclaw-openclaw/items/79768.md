Keep the fix plugin-owned by adding a Bedrock policy artifact that reuses the Bedrock-aware resolver or normalizes Bedrock-prefixed Claude IDs, with policy-level coverage for `global.anthropic` and `us.anthropic` Claude 4.6 refs and unrelated provider changes split out.
