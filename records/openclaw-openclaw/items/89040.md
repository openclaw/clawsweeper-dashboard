## Summary

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the async bootstrap-read repair, hide bootstrap-only timing and scheduling behind private internals, preserve `fs.glob` no-match semantics, and merge only after maintainers accept the remaining hook-interleaving and glob-walker compatibility tradeoffs.
