## Summary

Keep open: current `main` and the latest `v2026.5.2` tag still route WhatsApp login QR output through direct console/stdout paths, and #76556 is an open closing PR that should be reviewed before this issue closes.

## What This Changes

WhatsApp CLI re-authentication can stall because `channels login --channel whatsapp` may not expose the Linked Devices QR through the active runtime output path.

## Best Possible Solution

Land #76556 or an equivalent narrow WhatsApp/channel-auth fix that routes the QR heading and ASCII art through the injected runtime, adds regression coverage, and then close the issue after merge.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, by source inspection: current `loginWeb` still enables socket-owned QR printing, and the QR handler writes through `console.log` and `process.stdout.write` outside the injected runtime. I did not live-reproduce a WhatsApp pairing session.

## Solution Assessment

Yes, the narrow maintainable direction is to disable socket-owned terminal QR printing for explicit login and render QR output through the active runtime, with focused tests. #76556 is the active implementation candidate, but it should be reviewed before the issue closes.
