## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.78

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Keep equal-priority selection sequence-based:** `src/process/command-queue.ts:307-308`
  - body: `pickNextIndex` compares `enqueuedAt` before `sequence` for every same-effective-priority entry. Existing FIFO within a priority is sequence-based, and `enqueuedAt` is wall-clock or preserved runtime state, so a non-monotonic timestamp can let a later entry drain before an earlier one. Compare `sequence` after effective priority; use `enqueuedAt` only to decide whether an entry is starvation-promoted.
  - confidence: 0.82
