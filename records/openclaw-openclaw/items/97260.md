## Solution Assessment

Yes, this appears to be the best narrow fix: reusing the shared helper immediately after `loadSessionEntry` keeps the invariant centralized. Routing through `sessions.resolve` would not cover this direct `agent` RPC path, and duplicating parser logic would drift from sibling guards.
