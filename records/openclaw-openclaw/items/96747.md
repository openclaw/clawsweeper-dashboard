## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Yes, with maintainer acceptance of the upgrade/API contract. Fixing chunk assembly is the right layer, and index identity versioning prevents stale persisted chunks; downstream snippet cleanup alone would leave hashes and embeddings corrupted.
