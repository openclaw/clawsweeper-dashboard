Request a default-off Slack placeholder config that posts a visible “Thinking…” message immediately, edits it into the final reply, and deletes it on NO_REPLY.

## Best Possible Solution

Extend the existing Slack draft/progress delivery path with a maintainer-approved default-off early placeholder lifecycle, reusing its edit, finalization, cleanup, docs, and regression-test structure.
