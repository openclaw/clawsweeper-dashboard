Full review comments:

- **[P2] Keep stale retries inside the acquire timeout:** `src/agents/session-write-lock.ts:844`
  - body: After a `file_lock_stale`, this loop checks the elapsed time before sleeping, but the next iteration still calls `SESSION_LOCKS.acquire` with the original `timeoutMs`. A stale report near the end of a 60s acquire window can therefore spend almost another full timeout on normal contention before `SessionWriteLockTimeoutError`, even though the documented/configured budget is `session.writeLock.acquireTimeoutMs`. Track the remaining budget and pass that to the next acquire attempt, with a persistent stale/contended-lock regression.
