## Summary

Keep open. Current main and v2026.6.8 include the shipped volatile-file retry/skip mitigation, but they still do not implement the broader large-directory exclusion or smart-exclude behavior requested here, and the linked closing PR remains open with merge/proof blockers.

## What This Changes

The issue asks for `openclaw backup create` to handle multi-GB `.openclaw` directories by completing reliably or failing clearly, with possible exclude/progress/size controls.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the shipped volatile-file mitigation, then land or rework the linked exclude/smart-exclude PR, or choose a narrower core backup-size policy that makes large backups complete predictably or fail with clear diagnostics.

## Reproduction Assessment
