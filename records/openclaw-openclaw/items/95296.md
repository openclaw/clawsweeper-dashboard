## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Mostly yes: sharing one resolver across both OAuth builders is the maintainable repair and avoids subprocess work on the auth path. The remaining best-fix questions are maintainer acceptance of the env override, refreshing or accepting the static default, and stronger live OAuth/runtime proof.
