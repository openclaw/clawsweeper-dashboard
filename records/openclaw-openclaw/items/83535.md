## Solution Assessment

No as-is: the latency and observability direction is reasonable, but uploaded heap snapshots are not a safe default and the group-hydration predicate is too narrow. The safer path is uploadable timing/RSS/trace summaries, private or excluded heap dumps, and group-policy coverage before merge.
