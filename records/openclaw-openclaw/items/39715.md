Full review comments:

- **[P2] Keep sessions out of fallback removal while draining:** `src/process/supervisor/supervisor.ts:229-234`
  - body: Finalizing the supervisor record and deleting it from `active` before the `setImmediate` drain means a background session is still present in the process registry while `supervisor.getRecord()` already reports it as exited. A `process remove` or `process kill` in that turn falls through to the PID fallback, marks/deletes the session as failed/removed, and the later wait continuation can then publish the completed session again. Please keep the supervisor/session state coherent until `managedRun.wait()` publishes the final output, or teach process actions to treat this in-between state as completing.
