Land a shared, code-fence-aware outbound sanitizer at normal and streaming delivery boundaries, keep helper/API exposure intentional, and validate with focused regression tests plus real Telegram or equivalent channel proof.

Yes, the narrow maintainable direction is a shared outbound delivery-boundary sanitizer, not a Telegram-only regex and not waiting for the broader registry RFC to land first.
