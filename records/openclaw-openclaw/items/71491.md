## Summary

Keep open. Current main still has source-reproducible replay gaps for `reasoning_content` after thinking blocks or stripped replay history, and the relevant repair work is still in open, unmerged PRs rather than shipped code.

## What This Changes

The issue reports `reasoning_content` 400s in long thinking-enabled tool-call sessions after compaction or cross-provider replay, especially Kimi K2.6 and related providers.

## Best Possible Solution

Land narrow replay normalization that preserves or reconstructs `reasoning_content` for reasoning-content-required providers after compaction, with focused regression tests and redacted real provider proof; keep LCM-side preservation tracked separately.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level. Current main still serializes `thinkingSignature: "reasoning_content"` blocks without top-level `reasoning_content` in the Anthropic transport and lacks Moonshot missing-field backfill, matching the reported provider 400 path after compaction strips or reshapes replay history.

## Solution Assessment

Yes for the repair direction. Provider/transport-owned replay normalization is the narrow maintainable fix, but the best next step is to review and land the existing open PRs after real-behavior proof rather than spawn another duplicate repair.
