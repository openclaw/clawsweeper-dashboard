## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.88

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Chain the update broadcast into activation:** `ui/public/sw.js:46`
  - body: The new `matchAll()` call runs outside `event.waitUntil()` and in parallel with `clients.claim()`. Since `matchAll()` defaults to clients already controlled by this worker, the new worker can observe no windows before `claim()` finishes, drop the message, and leave the stale tab unreloaded. Put claim, cache cleanup, and postMessage in one awaited activate chain, with a guard for real prior-version upgrades. ([developer.mozilla.org](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Clients/matchAll))
  - confidence: 0.9
