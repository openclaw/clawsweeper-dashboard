Full review comments:

- **[P1] Remove the targetOrigin from Client.postMessage:** `ui/public/sw.js:48`
  - body: This is the service-worker `Client.postMessage()` API, whose second argument is `transfer` or `options`, not a Window-style `targetOrigin`; passing `"*"` can prevent the update notification from being delivered. Remove that argument or use the supported options shape and keep lint satisfied another way. ([developer.mozilla.org](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Client/postMessage))
  - confidence: 0.92
- **[P1] Await and guard the update broadcast:** `ui/public/sw.js:46`
  - body: The new `matchAll()` runs after the activate `waitUntil()` promise, so the worker can finish activation before stale tabs are notified. Move claim, cache cleanup, and notification into one awaited activation path, and guard notification so fresh installs or same-version activations do not reload unnecessarily. ([developer.mozilla.org](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/ExtendableEvent/waitUntil))
  - confidence: 0.88
