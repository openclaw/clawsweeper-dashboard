Yes. Current main has a high-confidence source-level reproduction: with a forward lid-mapping-{phoneDigits}.json present, createWebSendApi still converts the phone number through toWhatsappJid and sends to {digits}@s.whatsapp.net because authDir is not consulted.

## Solution Assessment

Yes, with one process fix. Resolving phone-number recipients against the listener account authDir inside createWebSendApi is the narrowest maintainable fix for sendMessage, sendPoll, and composing presence while preserving unmapped and no-authDir behavior.
