Keep this draft open until accessible exact-head proof, Codex runtime acceptance or pinning, and explicit maintainer approval of the fresh-thread upgrade behavior are in place; fold the alias into https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/90994 only if that becomes the viable canonical path.
