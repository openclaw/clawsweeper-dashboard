Full review comments:

- **[P1] Keep workspace hooks explicitly opt-in:** `src/hooks/policy.ts:96-97`
  - body: Because `defaultEnableMode` is parsed from every HOOK.md and then used for all non-plugin sources, a workspace hook can set `defaultEnableMode: "default-on"` and be loaded whenever broad internal hook discovery is configured, without `hooks.internal.entries.<name>.enabled=true`.
