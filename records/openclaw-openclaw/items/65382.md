Full review comments:

- **[P2] Avoid labeling field-step cron as uniform intervals:** `ui/src/ui/presenter.ts:83-89`
  - body: Croner treats `*/N` as matching positions within the field, not as a continuous interval when `N` does not divide the field range. Please fall back or use accurate wording unless the cadence is actually uniform under Croner.
