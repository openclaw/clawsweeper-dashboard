## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

No. Stateful UTF-8 decoding is the right primitive, but the maintainable current-main fix belongs in createWindowsOutputDecoder with tests, not as a child-local replacement that bypasses Windows codepage handling.
