Keep the fix at the Google provider model/thinking boundary so Gemini 3 Flash remains reasoning-capable for cron, with regression coverage that preserves explicit `reasoning:false` semantics for unrelated custom models.

## Solution Assessment

Yes for the central regression: the maintainable fix is to make Google Gemini 3 Flash thinking support authoritative at the provider/catalog boundary and add focused cron/provider regression tests. A broader fail-closed cron policy for unsupported thinking levels should be treated as a separate maintainer choice.
