TUI `/new` should preserve isolated per-client `tui-*` sessions while again emitting hook-visible `command:new` behavior for session automation.

Add a narrow target-repo fix that restores TUI `/new` hook emission against the session being left, preserves unique `tui-*` isolation, avoids a public `hookSourceKey`-style RPC expansion, and locks the behavior with focused TUI and gateway hook tests.
