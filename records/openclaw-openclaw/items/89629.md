- **[P1] Do not default provider limits to OAuth:** `src/infra/provider-usage.limits.ts:30`
  - body: `usageState.authMode` records the turn mode, but `limits` is resolved without passing it, and this helper turns a missing credential type into `oauth`.   - confidence: 0.9
- **[P1] Carry usageState through queued reply hook delivery:** `src/plugins/hook-types.ts:546`
  - body: Adding `usageState` to `PluginHookReplyPayloadSendingEvent` makes it a plugin-visible contract, but routed durable delivery still persists a queued hook with only kind/channel/sessionKey/runId/context and recovery replays it without this field. A plugin footer would work for normal dispatcher replies and disappear for routed or recovered deliveries, so carry the field through or explicitly exclude those invocations from the contract before exposing it.
  - confidence: 0.86
