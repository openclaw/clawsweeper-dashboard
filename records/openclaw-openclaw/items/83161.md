## Best Possible Solution

Keep the Telegram-layer ownership, but suppress duplicate finals only after stopping or materializing the draft stream and confirming `lastDeliveredText` matches the final or block being suppressed.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

No, not as patched. The Telegram channel layer is the right owner boundary, but the suppression should reuse or extend the lane deliverer's confirmed-delivery contract instead of finalizing from dispatcher `lastPartialText`.
