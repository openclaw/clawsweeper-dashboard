## Best Possible Solution

Land the selector-boundary fix after normal maintainer review, keeping promotion limited to complete `agent:` keys and preserving explicit `sessionKey`/`sessionId` precedence.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Promoting only complete `agent:` keys at the selector boundary and stripping the consumed `to` before delivery is narrower and more maintainable than a WeChat-specific workaround.
