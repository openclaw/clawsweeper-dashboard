Full review comments:

- **[P2] Preserve Response.json BOM handling:** `src/infra/push-apns.relay.ts:287`
  - body: This swaps `response.json()` for `JSON.parse(buffer.toString("utf8"))`, but `Buffer.toString` leaves a leading UTF-8 BOM that `Response.json()` accepts. A small relay body like BOM-prefixed `{"ok":false,"status":410}` now falls into the absent-body fallback, so a 2xx HTTP response can be reported as delivered instead of honoring the relay failure. Decode with `TextDecoder` or strip the BOM before parsing.
  - confidence: 0.87
