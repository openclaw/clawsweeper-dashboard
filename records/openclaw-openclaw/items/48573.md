The issue reports embedded-run or ACP child sessions persisting after parent termination and poisoning later session/task state.

## Best Possible Solution

Land a narrow task-maintenance fix, likely the existing focused PR or a corrected successor, that treats stale ACP rows without real runtime/transcript backing as missing while preserving legitimate persistent or bound sessions.
