Request to expose channel lifecycle start, stop, and restart controls through the CLI so operators can recover a linked but wedged channel without QR re-pairing or a full container restart.

## Best Possible Solution

Review and land or replace https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/93028 as the focused CLI wrapper over the existing Gateway lifecycle methods, while keeping automatic retry/backoff policy in separate lifecycle work.
