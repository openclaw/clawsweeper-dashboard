## Best Possible Solution

Land this or an equivalent resolver-boundary guard after secops approves the fail-closed one-line-or-JSON contract, document the plaintext rule, and keep the broader linked security issue open until same-line corruption is resolved or separately scoped.

Unclear. Rejecting ambiguous multi-line plaintext is a narrow security-favorable guard, but the best merge path depends on secops accepting the compatibility break and deciding how this relates to the same-line corruption in the linked issue.
