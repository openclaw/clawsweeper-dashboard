The PR adds an internal stable hashed `promptCacheKey` for embedded isolated cron runs, threads it through the embedded runner, and prefers it over `sessionId` in OpenAI Responses and opted-in Completions payloads.

## Best Possible Solution

Land the internal stable cron prompt-cache affinity path after normal CI and maintainer review, then let the linked issue close on merge while keeping broader prompt-prefix drift work separate.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Separating provider prompt-cache affinity from transcript/session identity is the narrow maintainable fix, and the PR keeps it internal while covering isolated cron, session-bound cron, CLI cron, Responses, and compatible Completions behavior.
