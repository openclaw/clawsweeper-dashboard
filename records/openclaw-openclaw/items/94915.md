## What This Changes

## Best Possible Solution

Land the narrow readiness wiring after maintainer review and exact-head checks, keeping `/healthz` as liveness and leaving stuck-drain reset behavior in the existing restart-reset path.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Reusing `isGatewayDraining()` inside readiness before the cached channel snapshot is the narrowest maintainable fix; `/healthz` liveness and restart reset behavior stay in their existing owner paths.
