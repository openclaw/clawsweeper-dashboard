## Summary

Keep open: current main and v2026.6.8 cover important pieces of the skill lifecycle, including agent-aware `skills check`, ClawHub install/update/verify, SKILL.md-rooted archive policy, and install-policy hooks, but the umbrella still has unresolved public security/API and product decisions around capability manifests, enforcement, post-install audit, trigger metadata, composition, and ClawHub publisher/service ownership.

## What This Changes

The issue requests a reliable community skill lifecycle around discovery, publishing, watching, trust/security, composition, and ClawHub operations.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as a product/security umbrella, then route concrete work into narrower maintainer-sponsored issues: capability/permission manifests, enforcement grants, post-install audit hooks, trigger/composition metadata, and ClawHub-owned publishing reliability.

## Reproduction Assessment

Partly, at source level. Current main and v2026.6.8 show that several requested capabilities are still absent or only partially covered, but the umbrella is not one runnable bug with a single high-confidence reproduction path.

## Solution Assessment

No, not as one broad implementation. The best solution is a maintainer-led split into specific security/API and ClawHub ownership decisions, with small implementation PRs only after each contract is chosen.
