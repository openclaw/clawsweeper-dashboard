## Best Possible Solution

Land this logger-layer coalescer after maintainer acceptance of summarized duplicate auth diagnostics, while keeping broader provider quarantine/status policy in https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/47910.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes. For the narrow log-spam bug, coalescing at the fallback decision logger is the smallest maintainable fix because it reduces duplicate diagnostics without changing auth cooldown, provider selection, fallback routing, or callbacks.
