## Best Possible Solution

Keep agents.files.* as the compatibility surface, consolidate workspace-recursive management under the canonical open issue and PR, and require a separate security-approved design for any host-system browsing scope with explicit roots, auth, limits, and write policy.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear as proposed. An additive workspace API/UI plus a separately approved host-system design is safer than removing the allowlist and adding broad filesystem scopes in one change.
