The PR replaces the heartbeat active-hours seek iteration cap with a 60s minimum seek step and adds unit tests for sub-minute and 1ms interval behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the seek bounded while preserving phase-aligned candidates, then add coverage for non-divisor sub-minute intervals and redacted real heartbeat-runner proof.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes by source inspection. A sub-minute interval that does not divide 60s, such as 45s, makes the PR’s 60s seek step drift off the phase-aligned sequence that the helper contract and runner scheduling rely on.

## Solution Assessment

No. Flooring to 60s is a plausible availability fix, but the best fix must remain bounded without returning candidates that are not whole-interval steps from the original phase-aligned `startMs`.
