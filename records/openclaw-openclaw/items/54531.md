Keep this as the canonical maintainer-reviewed cross-surface origin-delivery item, then implement sticky per-turn external origin or explicit opt-in force-origin semantics with privacy safeguards, regression tests, and live channel proof.

No, not as a broad config-only fallback. The best fix needs maintainer-chosen sticky per-turn origin or explicit opt-in delivery semantics with route freshness, account/thread authorization, and privacy safeguards.
