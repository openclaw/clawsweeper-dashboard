  - body: `session.created` is sent when the socket opens, before the adapter's `session.update` is acknowledged. Resolving setup here can emit `session.ready` and flush queued media before voice, instructions, audio settings, and tools are applied; complete setup on `session.updated` instead.
  - confidence: 0.9
