## What This Changes

The issue asks WebChat to preserve queued messages, visible conversation history, and unsent draft text across browser refresh or reconnect.

## Best Possible Solution

Land or replace a scoped fix that keeps gateway-backed history reload, restores same-tab draft and queue state under an accepted retention policy, preserves attachment behavior safely, and avoids premature queue delivery after reconnect.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear. Gateway-backed history reload is the right contract, but queue and draft persistence still need maintainer-approved retention, attachment, and active-run replay semantics before either open PR is the final fix.
