Land a current-main fix that sweeps both backup-owned artifact classes: output-adjacent `<outputPath>.<uuid>.tmp` files and stale `openclaw-backup-*` directories under the selected temp root, with tight matching, active-writer protection, regression tests, and redacted terminal proof.
