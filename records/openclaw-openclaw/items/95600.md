Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Protect active backup writers before deleting stale artifacts:** `src/infra/backup-create.ts:749`
  - body: `removeStalePath` deletes every matching artifact once its mtime is more than one hour old. A second `backup create` can therefore remove the staging directory or temp archive for a concurrent long-running or stalled backup, and the active process later still needs that path for archiving or publish. Please add an owner signal such as a lock/marker, or another active-writer guard, before deleting matched artifacts.
