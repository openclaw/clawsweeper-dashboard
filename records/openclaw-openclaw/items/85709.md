- **[P1] Do not time out visible reply admission:** `src/auto-reply/reply/reply-turn-admission.ts:57-59`
  - body: `admitReplyTurn` calls `waitForIdle` without a timeout, so visible turns inherit the registry's 15s default. Visible turns need to wait abortably until ownership is available or preserve/retry the inbound instead of committing it as processed.
