## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level. A bootstrap entry with a valid `path`, oversized content, and missing `name` can reach `trimBootstrapContent(..., file.name, ...)` and `formatBootstrapTruncationWarningLines`, both of which still lower-case unguarded names on current main and v2026.5.20.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The narrow maintainable fix is to normalize or reject malformed bootstrap metadata at the bootstrap-file boundary and keep lower-level helpers null-safe, as the open fixing PR attempts; patching generated `dist/` alone would not be sufficient.
