Keep open. ## Best Possible Solution

Define a redacted, structured ACP parent-progress/audit event contract with correlation ids and focused tests, then render concise human-readable summaries from that contract.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainers choose the visibility and redaction policy. A raw string relay would be weaker than structured, redacted summaries aligned with the parent-session trust boundary.
