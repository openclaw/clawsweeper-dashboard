Land this narrow deletion guard after adequate redacted after-fix runtime proof, while keeping the broader direct-session isolation work owned by https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/69467 and https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/57831.

## Solution Assessment

Yes for the narrow destructive-delete bug. Reusing the existing `isCronSessionKey` helper at the deletion point is the smallest maintainable fix; the broader direct-session binding policy should stay with the related open issue and PR.
