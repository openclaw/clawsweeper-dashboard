  - confidence: 0.91

  - body: resolveEchoTargets suppresses origin echoes only when channel, to, account, and thread match, but the hook never passes originThreadId for sent or received turns. A Telegram topic target with a threadId can therefore be treated as different from the origin and echoed back into the same topic, breaking the advertised circular-echo prevention.
