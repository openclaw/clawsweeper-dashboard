## Best Possible Solution

Land the narrow internal Anthropic transport guard after real non-test proof is added, the overflowed/cancel contract is cleaned up, and maintainers accept or tune the 16 MiB fail-closed boundary; keep SDK promotion and sibling SSE parsers in separate PRs.

Yes, with cleanup: guarding the reader at the Anthropic Messages parser boundary is narrower than buffering through readResponseWithLimit, which would lose streaming semantics. The helper should track overflow separately from cancellation, and the cap needs real behavior proof plus maintainer acceptance.
