- **[P1] Scope poison retries per Telegram spool:** `extensions/telegram/src/polling-session.ts:155`
  - body: The poison counter is module-global and keyed only by Telegram `updateId`, but polling sessions and ingress queues are account-scoped. Include the account or spool identity in the poison key and cover same-update-id isolated spools in tests.
