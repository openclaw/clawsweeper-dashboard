## Best Possible Solution

Land this narrow mismatch diagnostic after required checks are reconciled, while leaving broader first-run warning remediation to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/68780 and https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/78105.

## Solution Assessment
