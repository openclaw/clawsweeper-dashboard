The issue reports Slack assistant replies that are finalized in the transcript but never or only much later appear in Slack, and asks for visible delivery failures, safer in-flight handling, and Slack receipt confirmation.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open while maintainers rebase, review, and either land or replace https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/80749; the end state should not mark Slack finals delivered without a concrete Slack `ts` or durable failed-delivery record, with broader cross-channel outbox semantics tracked by https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/87561.
