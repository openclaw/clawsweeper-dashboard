## Best Possible Solution

Land or replace the focused Slack receipt/failure-recording PR for normal final delivery, and keep broader cross-channel durable-final semantics in the separate canonical contract issue.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes for the immediate Slack bug: require concrete Slack delivery identity or record a durable failure before treating normal final delivery as complete. Auto-repost policy and cross-channel durable outbox semantics should remain separate maintainer work.
