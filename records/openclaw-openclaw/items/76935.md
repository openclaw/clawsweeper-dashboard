## Best Possible Solution

Have a QQ Bot owner decide the intended default for split streaming, collect redacted screenshots/logs for the repetition/context-bleed symptom, then fix the proven QQ Bot streaming, queue, or history boundary with focused regression coverage.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear. A narrow change that treats undefined QQ Bot streaming as final-only would reduce split replies, but it may conflict with recent intentional streaming behavior; owner confirmation plus targeted proof is the safer path.
