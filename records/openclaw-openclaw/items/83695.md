## Risks / Open Questions

- Merging changes state-path hardening from requiring chmod success to tolerating failed chmod when access and mode checks pass, so fsGroup directories may remain group-accessible and other-readable/executable instead of becoming `0700`.
- Private SQLite sidecars with other bits beyond the requested `0600` still fail closed; operators with existing root-owned unsafe sidecars may need cleanup rather than a transparent upgrade.
- The proof covers Docker/Kubernetes-style filesystem behavior and all affected chmod call sites, but not a full OpenClaw gateway startup against a real Kubernetes PVC.
