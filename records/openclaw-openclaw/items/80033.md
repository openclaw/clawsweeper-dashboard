    fix(opencode-go): enable xhigh/max thinking levels for DeepSeek V4 models via provider hooks
    
    DeepSeek V4 Flash and V4 Pro natively support reasoning_effort: "max",
    and OpenClaw's stream layer already maps xhigh/max to "max" on the wire.
    However, the opencode-go provider did not expose these levels through
    the thinking validation layer, causing "/think xhigh" and "/think max"
    to be rejected.

    add supportsXHighThinking and resolveThinkingProfile hooks to the
    opencode-go provider registration, declaring a thinking profile
    that includes xhigh and max for the deepseek-v4-flash and
    deepseek-v4-pro models.
