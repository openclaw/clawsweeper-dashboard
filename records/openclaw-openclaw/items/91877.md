- **[P1] Keep priming out of the playback capture gate:** `extensions/discord/src/voice/receive-prime.ts:91`
  - body: `primeVoiceReceive` plays the synthetic burst through the same `entry.player` that `handleSpeakingStart` treats as normal playback.
