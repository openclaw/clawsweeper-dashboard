  - confidence: 0.86

  - body: Mermaid 11.13.0 documents `render()` returning `{ svg, bindFunctions }` and calls `bindFunctions?.(element)` after inserting the SVG. This code drops `bindFunctions`, so Mermaid diagrams with tooltips or click handlers render without their interactions.
