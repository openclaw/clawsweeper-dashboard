## Solution Assessment

Yes, conditionally: keeping this inside the bundled Feishu plugin and reusing DM ingress, pairing, session, and reply routing is the narrow owner-boundary solution. The unresolved part is whether event subscription alone is the right opt-in model or a default-off Feishu config gate is safer.
