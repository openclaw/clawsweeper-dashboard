## Best Possible Solution

Keep the source-aware exec-event deferral bypass, but count successful exec-event attempts as schedule advancement, add stale due-slot scheduler coverage, and align the retryable reason type.

## Solution Assessment

No. The source-aware exec-event bypass is the right layer, but skipping schedule advancement after exec-event runs is broader than needed; the safer fix preserves advancement while bypassing only the exec-event deferral gate.

- **[P2] Advance the schedule after exec-event runs:** `src/infra/heartbeat-runner.ts:2167-2169`
  - body: Returning before agent.nextDueMs is advanced means a successful exec-event that lands when the current interval slot is already due leaves the due slot at or before now. Keep the exec-event bypass in shouldDeferWake but still advance the schedule after a terminal run, and cover the stale due-slot case.
