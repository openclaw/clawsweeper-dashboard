The issue reports silent WhatsApp turn loss after `stream-ready` when long-lived session trajectory files exceed a few MB and asks whether size-based compaction settings are actually enforced.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open until a maintainer confirms whether the supported top-level `maxActiveTranscriptBytes` plus `truncateAfterCompaction` resolves the WhatsApp case, or narrows a fix to trajectory flush/stuck-turn recovery with logs from the reporter.
