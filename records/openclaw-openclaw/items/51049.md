## Summary

Keep open. Current main and v2026.6.6 still rely on Baileys `messages.upsert` plus passive watchdog/reconnect signals, and the newer active-liveness and reconnect-backfill threads are partial follow-ups rather than merged fixes for the outbound-capable but inbound-silent WhatsApp state.

## What This Changes

The issue reports WhatsApp inbound messages never reaching the gateway in Docker/k3s-style environments while outbound WhatsApp sends and linked-session health appear to work.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the canonical external-inbound-loss tracker, use live WhatsApp container proof to choose one plugin-owned recovery or diagnostic design, and coordinate narrower work in https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/93406 and https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/50093.
