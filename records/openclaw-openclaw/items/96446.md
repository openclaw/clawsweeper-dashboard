## Best Possible Solution

Land the focused sanitizer unification after normal maintainer approval so inline parsing and directive emission share `sanitizeReplyDirectiveId` as the single reply-id normalization contract.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Reusing `sanitizeReplyDirectiveId()` at the parse boundary is the narrowest maintainable fix; re-sanitizing every downstream caller would duplicate the contract and still risk missed consumers.
