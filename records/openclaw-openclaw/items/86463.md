## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Mostly yes: warning without auto-delivery preserves the documented privacy contract, and the PR now proves the WARN path. The remaining safer adjustment is to reuse the shared silent-token helper and let maintainers decide whether warn-only is sufficient for the linked issue.
