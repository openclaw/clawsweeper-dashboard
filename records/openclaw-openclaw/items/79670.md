## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to stop recommending Gemini preview models during Google API-key onboarding without quota validation, either by warning about quota limits, showing per-model limits, or defaulting to stable models.

## Best Possible Solution

Decide the Google API-key onboarding policy explicitly, then align the provider default, model picker behavior, public docs, and tests around either a stable production-safe default or a clear preview quota warning.
