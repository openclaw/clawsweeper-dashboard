The issue asks Google onboarding to stop recommending Gemini preview models without quota validation or warnings, preferably by defaulting paid API-key users to a stable model such as `google/gemini-2.5-flash`.

The Google plugin owner should choose a clear onboarding policy: keep the preview default with an explicit quota/tier warning, or switch the default and aligned docs/tests to a stable model while preserving the plugin-owned onboarding boundary.
