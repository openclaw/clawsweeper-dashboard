## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. Source inspection gives a high-confidence reproduction path: Silent quick-create creates a main-session `agentTurn` job, and the backend rejects main-session cron jobs unless the payload is `systemEvent`.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The narrow fix is to make the Silent quick-create preset emit a backend-supported `systemEvent` payload; relaxing the backend cron invariant would be broader and riskier.
