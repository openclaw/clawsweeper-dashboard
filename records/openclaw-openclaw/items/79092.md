- **[P1] Preserve Teams group allowlist isolation from DMs:** `extensions/msteams/src/monitor-handler/access.ts:112`
  - body: Setting `groupAllowFromFallbackToAllowFrom` to `true` makes Teams group sender checks inherit `channels.msteams.allowFrom` whenever `groupAllowFrom` is unset or empty.
