- **[P2] Verify required asset names, not total count:** `.github/workflows/verify-release-assets.yml:53-57`
  - body: The workflow succeeds as soon as any three release assets exist, but stable macOS readiness requires the specific `OpenClaw-$VERSION.dmg`, `OpenClaw-$VERSION.zip`, and `OpenClaw-$VERSION.dSYM.zip` artifacts.
