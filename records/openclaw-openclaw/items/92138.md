## Summary

## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to prevent WhatsApp LID/phone identity resolution from delivering replies to the wrong contact, cross-feeding inbound replies into another session, or leaving sessions stuck with pending final delivery.

## Best Possible Solution

Add and prove a WhatsApp identity invariant across inbound LID/phone normalization, session route selection, last-route state, durable final delivery, and outbound sends so ambiguous or stale mappings fail visibly instead of targeting a different contact.

No high-confidence live reproduction was established. ## Solution Assessment
