  - body: The self-escalation guard only checks OPENCLAW_SESSION_KEY and OPENCLAW_AGENT_ID in the CLI, and current main does not populate those variables in production paths.   - confidence: 0.84
- **[P1] Preserve or reject hard-deny policies:** `src/infra/exec-approvals.ts:624-629`

  - confidence: 0.88

  - confidence: 0.87
