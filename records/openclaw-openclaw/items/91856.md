Request a Microsoft Teams plugin config/surface detector that enables streaming for Copilot custom-engine conversations while keeping normal Teams DMs, group chats, and channels non-streaming by default.

Keep the issue open until maintainers choose a reliable Copilot surface detector and a plugin-owned streaming config shape that preserves the current global default.
