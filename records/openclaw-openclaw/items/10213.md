## Solution Assessment

No, the exact nested global `serverSide` config in the issue is not yet the best-proven design. A maintainer-approved provider/model-owned opt-in contract appears safer because it keeps Anthropic-specific behavior at the provider boundary and avoids a second global compaction policy source.
