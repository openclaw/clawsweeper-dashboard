## What This Changes

The issue reports that DeepSeek V4 reasoning-only turns with unsigned thinking blocks do not trigger the visible-answer continuation retry and can fall through to idle-timeout retry behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Capture a sanitized DeepSeek V4-Pro failing transcript or SSE fixture, then patch the narrow path it proves: unsigned-block classification, signed `reasoning_content` replay, custom proxy detection, or timed-out partial-thinking continuation, while preserving reasoning privacy and replay-safety boundaries.
