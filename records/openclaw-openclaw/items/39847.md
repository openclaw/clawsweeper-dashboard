The issue asks OpenClaw to strip echoed inbound metadata from assistant reply payloads before outbound channel delivery so Discord and other channels do not show internal Conversation/Sender blocks.

Yes. The best fix is at the shared outbound payload plan boundary before directive parsing, because that covers Discord and sibling delivery projections without per-channel compiled-bundle patches.
