## Best Possible Solution

Keep the lifecycle-owned retry loop, but make the periodic pass recover eligible pending restart-continuation entries beyond the startup cutoff and prove that exact failed-after-startup path.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes from source inspection: the gateway captures maxEnqueuedAt before the restart-sentinel sidecar enqueues the continuation, and recovery filters out entries newer than that cutoff. I did not run a live or local repro because this review is read-only.

## Solution Assessment

No. A periodic retry in gateway scheduled services is the right owner boundary, but reusing the startup cutoff makes the proposed fix miss the central failed-after-startup continuation path.

- **[P1] Do not reuse the startup cutoff for periodic retries:** `src/gateway/server-runtime-services.ts:188`
