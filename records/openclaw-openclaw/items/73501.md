## Summary

Keep open. The report is fresh, corroborated by multiple users, and current main shows several partial mitigations and diagnostics but no source, test, release, or live-timing proof that the 4.22/4.23 to 4.26/v2026.4.29 reply-latency regression is resolved across Feishu/Lark, Telegram, dashboard, pre-LLM startup, and embedded-run paths.

## What This Changes

Investigate and fix the reported upgrade regression where OpenClaw 4.26 and later respond much slower than 4.22/4.23, especially around Feishu/Lark reactions, visible replies, and pre-LLM startup delay.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the broad upgrade-regression tracker until maintainers collect same-host stage timings on current main, identify the dominant remaining bottleneck, and split targeted fixes for the measured layer while preserving the already-landed runtime-deps, model-resolution, diagnostics, and Active Memory mitigations.
