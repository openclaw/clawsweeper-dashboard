## Best Possible Solution

Keep the centralized helper approach, but constrain or guard the newly added unanchored request verbs so negated and non-directive prompts do not trigger act-now retries, with regression coverage.

## Solution Assessment

No, not yet. The central helper/test direction is the right narrow fix, but the request fallback must stop matching newly added verbs inside negated or non-directive prose before merge.
