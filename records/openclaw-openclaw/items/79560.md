## Summary

Keep open: current main has ACP enable/allowlist/concurrency controls and thread-bound `spawnSessions`, but not the requested general per-channel ACP deny/warn policy or identical completion-event coalescing for noisy child sessions.

## What This Changes

Request adding per-channel ACP spawn policy and/or completion coalescing/permission-denial bounds to stop runaway ACP child sessions from flooding non-interactive channels such as Telegram DMs.

## Best Possible Solution

Define a small channel-aware ACP safety contract, then implement the chosen guard with focused coverage around `sessions_spawn(runtime="acp")`, announcement delivery, and Telegram topic/DM behavior.

## Reproduction Assessment

No high-confidence live reproduction was established. Source and docs do confirm the relevant non-interactive ACP path and the absence of a general per-channel ACP spawn guard or same-shape completion coalescer on current main.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear as proposed: per-channel deny/warn is plausible, but maintainers should choose whether the durable fix belongs in channel policy, announcement coalescing, acpx retry handling, or a combination before implementation.
