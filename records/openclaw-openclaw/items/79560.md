## Summary

Keep open: current main still has ACP availability checks, agent/runtime policy, thread-bound spawn gates, and per-run task delivery suppression, but not a general per-channel ACP deny/warn policy or same-shape completion coalescing for retrying child sessions.

## What This Changes

Add channel-aware ACP spawn policy and/or completion coalescing to prevent ACP child sessions from non-interactive channels such as Telegram DMs from flooding the parent chat.

## Best Possible Solution

Define a small channel-aware ACP safety contract, then implement the chosen guard/coalescer with focused coverage around `sessions_spawn(runtime="acp")`, task completion delivery, and Telegram DM/topic behavior.

## Reproduction Assessment

No high-confidence live reproduction was established. Source inspection confirms the relevant ACP spawn and completion-delivery paths, plus the absence of the requested general channel ACP deny/warn policy and same-shape coalescing on current main.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear as proposed: per-channel deny/warn is plausible operator control, but maintainers should choose whether the durable fix belongs in channel policy, completion coalescing, acpx retry behavior, or a combination.
