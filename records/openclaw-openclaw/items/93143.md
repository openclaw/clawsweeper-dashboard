## Best Possible Solution

Land this only after maintainers explicitly accept the wider opt-in iMessage latency tradeoff, and keep the teardown item open until the imsg floor and doctor migration decision is settled.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes: current main and v2026.6.9 use a 2500 ms implicit opt-in window, while the PR’s live proof shows a Messages.app URL-preview row arriving about 6 seconds after the text row and splitting before the wider window.

## Solution Assessment

Yes as an interim fix, not as the final cleanup. Preserving the opt-in compatibility path while narrowing URL-balloon merges is maintainable until maintainers decide the supported imsg floor and remove the local fallback.
