## Summary

## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Yes, with maintainer threshold acceptance. Separating the tight non-OK body cap from the larger successful-stream sanitizer cap fixes the false positive without removing the OOM guard; bypassing or unbounding the sanitizer would be worse.
