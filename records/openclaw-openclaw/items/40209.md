## Solution Assessment

No as written: normalizing at the queue boundary is the right direction, but unconditional toLowerCase violates the current opaque Signal group ID preservation contract. The safer fix is to use normalizeSessionKeyPreservingOpaquePeerIds and test Slack plus Signal cases.
