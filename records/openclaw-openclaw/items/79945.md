The Discord logging change is narrow, but the archive fix should be reconciled with https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/78057 and https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/78195 and should preserve MIME verification instead of adding generic ZIP to the early allowlist.
