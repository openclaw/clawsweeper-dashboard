## Best Possible Solution

Land a centralized reserved-namespace guard after preserving effective-mapping compatibility, collecting redacted real hook-runtime proof, and getting maintainer acceptance of the intentional fail-closed behavior.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Mostly yes: a shared reserved-prefix guard at config and dispatch boundaries is the right shape. It should preserve the existing effective-mapping shadowing compatibility or get explicit maintainer sign-off before merge.
