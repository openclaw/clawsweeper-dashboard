## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to avoid silently ignoring or overwriting a hot `channels.whatsapp.enabled=true` config change when the WhatsApp plugin was not loaded at gateway startup.

## Best Possible Solution

Review and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/78414 or a replacement that makes unloaded-channel activation observable and recoverable while preserving the reload-mode contract, then close this issue after merge.

## Solution Assessment
