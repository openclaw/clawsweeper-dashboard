## What This Changes

The issue reports a Mattermost regression where public/group channel WebSocket messages are silently dropped while DMs still work.

## Best Possible Solution

Mattermost should reliably process allowed public, private, and group `posted` WebSocket frames, including @mentioned `oncall` posts under open group policy, with focused regression coverage and useful redacted diagnostics when frames are filtered or never reach the parser.
