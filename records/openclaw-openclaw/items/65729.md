## Best Possible Solution

Keep the shipped WebSocket keepalive, then split the remaining umbrella into focused Mattermost tracks: a maintainer-approved DM threading contract, thread-history backfill after empty in-memory history, and bare-mention wake handling.

## Solution Assessment

No, this is not best handled as one broad implementation PR. The maintainable path is to split the shipped keepalive from the remaining product/config decision and the two narrower behavior repairs.
