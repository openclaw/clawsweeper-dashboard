## What This Changes

The issue reports WhatsApp turns timing out or ending with incomplete final fallback payloads that are suppressed before delivery, leaving users without a visible reply.

## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Yes for the narrow bug: allowing sanitized final user-facing error payloads through WhatsApp while still suppressing reasoning, compaction notices, and non-final tool noise is the maintainable path. The broader cross-channel semantics should stay in the linked maintainer issue rather than expanding this report.
