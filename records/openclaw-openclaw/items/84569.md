## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes for the immediate bug: the narrow maintainable fix is to let final user-facing isError payloads reach WhatsApp while still suppressing non-final tool, reasoning, compaction, and internal noise. The open linked PR is the active implementation path to review before broader timeout or session-rotation behavior is added.
