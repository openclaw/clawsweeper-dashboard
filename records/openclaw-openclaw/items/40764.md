- **[P2] Avoid offline failure states on language changes:** `apps/android/app/src/main/java/ai/openclaw/app/NodeRuntime.kt:640`
  - body: `setLanguageOverride()` now calls `refreshGatewayConnection()` after every local preference change. After rebase, the override needs to reach that active `TextToSpeech` engine or fallback TTS will still ignore the new setting.
  - confidence: 0.84
