## Summary

Keep open. Current main implements the narrow per-agent `thinkingDefault` and flat `agents.list[].params` pieces, but the broader requested public API is still missing: no per-agent model map or `generation` block, and no `thinkingStrategy` contract for rules, failure escalation, or AGENTS.md-sourced policy. Related open items overlap with slices of the request but do not supersede the whole design item.

## What This Changes

Request per-agent thinking defaults, per-agent generation/model parameter overrides, and adaptive thinking strategies driven by rules, failures, or AGENTS.md policy.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the design-level tracking item, preserve the shipped `thinkingDefault` and flat `params` behavior, coordinate generation-parameter forwarding with #47852/#32496, and split any adaptive thinking work into a versioned config/runtime policy after maintainers choose the public contract.
