## Best Possible Solution

Keep the selector and selected-agent gateway scoping, but make Dreaming status and diary loads keyed, forced, cancellable, or stale-response-safe before merge.

Yes. Source inspection gives a high-confidence path: start a Dreaming status or diary load, switch the Dreaming selector before it completes, and the selected-agent load can return early while the old response still writes state.

## Solution Assessment

No. The selector and agent-scoped RPC direction is the right fix, but it is not complete until the selected-agent loads are forced or stale-response-safe under in-flight refreshes.
