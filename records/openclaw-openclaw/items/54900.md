## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.9

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Keep startup expiry opt-in for existing queues:** `src/infra/outbound/delivery-queue-recovery.ts:215-216`
  - body: `recoverPendingDeliveries` now defaults `maxEntryAgeMs` to 10 minutes, and gateway startup calls it without passing an override. That means upgrading can move old first-attempt queue files to `failed/` even for users who expected durable crash recovery; keep the existing no-expiry behavior by default or wire this through an explicit documented config/policy.
  - confidence: 0.87
- **[P2] Preserve stale filtering after budget deferrals:** `src/infra/outbound/delivery-queue-recovery.ts:219`
  - body: The stale gate only matches `retryCount === 0` entries with no `lastAttemptAt`, but recovery-budget deferral calls `failDelivery(...)` for unprocessed entries. That increments `retryCount` and sets `lastAttemptAt` without a send, so an old never-sent entry can bypass this guard on the next startup and still replay out of context.
  - confidence: 0.92
- **[P2] Reconcile uncertain sends before expiring entries:** `src/infra/outbound/delivery-queue-recovery.ts:219`
  - body: This predicate also matches old entries with `recoveryState` such as `send_attempt_started` or `unknown_after_send`. On current main those entries go through adapter reconciliation before any replay; moving them to `failed/` solely by age can skip a successful already-sent reconciliation and its commit hooks.
  - confidence: 0.84
