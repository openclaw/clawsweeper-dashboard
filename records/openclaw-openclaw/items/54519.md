## Best Possible Solution

Choose a maintainer-approved no-outbound channel/account contract, then land focused WhatsApp or shared plugin enforcement with config/docs/tests/live proof while tracking hooks-only/no-agent ingestion separately in https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/78963.
