## Best Possible Solution

Land a narrowed replacement that classifies the exact Anthropic specified-API-usage-limit/regain-access text as billing while preserving route- and alias-aware message-tool dedupe, with redacted real multi-identifier messaging proof.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment
