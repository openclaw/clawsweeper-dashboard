## Label Justifications

- `P1`: The issue reports a broken Telegram retry path that can permanently fail real group-chat messages after transient send failure.
- `impact:message-loss`: Malformed retry targets are exhausted into `delivery-queue/failed/`, leaving Telegram group sends undelivered until manually discovered.

## Summary

Keep open: current main still replays malformed Telegram durable retry targets unchanged, and an open linked PR is the active fix path rather than a reason to close this issue before merge.

## What This Changes

Telegram durable retries should normalize legacy `group:-...` group targets to numeric chat IDs so failed sends are not exhausted into the failed queue.

## Best Possible Solution

Land or replace the Telegram-owned outbound-boundary normalization in the linked PR, keeping the generic durable queue contract unchanged and covering legacy queued entries.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes at source level. A queued Telegram entry with `to: "group:-100..."` is replayed unchanged, preserved by current target normalization, and rejected by the Telegram send resolver.

## Solution Assessment

Yes, the linked PR's direction is the right narrow fix. Normalize the legacy durable retry form at the Telegram outbound boundary or preserve the canonical numeric target without teaching the generic queue Telegram-specific syntax.
