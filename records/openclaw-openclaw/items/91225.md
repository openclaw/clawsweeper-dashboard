No, not yet. The owner-boundary direction is right, but the shared read helper should not fail open for managed writes; move that fallback to the optional diary-context reader only.

- **[P1] Keep safe-read races strict on managed writes:** `extensions/memory-core/src/dreaming-dreams-file.ts:57-58`
