This PR adds Telegram direct/group/topic `replyToMode` and preview `streaming` overrides, routes them into normal and native reply paths, updates docs/tests, and includes unrelated secrets/test changes.

Ship a focused Telegram plugin change that layers scoped `replyToMode` and nested `streaming` overrides over account defaults for normal and native replies, with docs/tests and redacted real Telegram proof.
