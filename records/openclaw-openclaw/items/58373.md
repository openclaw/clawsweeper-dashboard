- **[P1] Preserve inherited authHeader transport policy:** `src/agents/models-config.ts:299`
  - body: `authHeader` is not a secret value; it is the provider transport policy that makes `ModelRegistry.getApiKeyAndHeaders()` inject `Authorization: Bearer <apiKey>`.
