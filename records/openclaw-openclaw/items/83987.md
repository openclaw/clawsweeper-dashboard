## What This Changes

## Best Possible Solution

Land after maintainers explicitly accept the bounded successful-turn relay grace while preserving immediate cleanup for failed, interrupted, aborted, and timed-out turns.

Yes, with maintainer risk acceptance: deferring only successful cleanup and making handles generation-aware is a focused fix for late callbacks without keeping aborted or error turns open.
