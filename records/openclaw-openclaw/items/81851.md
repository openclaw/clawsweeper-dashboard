Full review comments:

- **[P1] Pin every proxy alias to the local MITM:** `extensions/anthropic/interactive-proxy/wrapper.ts:488-495`
  - body: The wrapper inherits the whole host env and then only overwrites uppercase `HTTPS_PROXY`. OpenClaw preserves proxy env for child CLIs, and its env-proxy contract gives lower-case vars and `NO_PROXY` precedence, so `https_proxy` or `NO_PROXY=api.anthropic.com` can make Claude bypass this proxy and drop the SSE stream this backend depends on. Clear the bypass vars and set every relevant proxy alias, or prove the spawned Claude runtime cannot honor them.
  - confidence: 0.88

  - body: After the wrapper forces Claude through the loopback proxy, CONNECTs for non-api `*.anthropic.com` are allowed and then opened with direct `net.connect`. That strips any operator-managed proxy or audit route from those Anthropic calls; chain pass-through traffic through the inherited managed proxy or fail closed instead of opening a raw direct socket.
  - confidence: 0.84
