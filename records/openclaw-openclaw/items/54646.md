- **[P2] Validate tasks audit limits after tightening the parser:** `src/cli/program/helpers.ts:18-20`
  - body: The PR tightens `parsePositiveIntOrUndefined`, but `tasks audit` still passes `parsePositiveIntOrUndefined(opts.limit)` straight into `tasksAuditCommand`. Please either validate explicit `--limit` at that caller or keep the strict parser local to timeout options.
