## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to treat generated media artifacts as successful even when completion delivery fails, preserve the artifact, and reliably deliver or wake the completion back to the original channel/session.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open as the canonical tracker until maintainers review or land the linked fix PRs and verify that successful generated-media artifacts are either delivered or reported as delivery failures without being mistaken for generation failures.
