## Solution Assessment

Yes, with a proof gap. The patch uses the established channel-owned adapter plus shared `resolveConfiguredBindingRoute` and `ensureConfiguredBindingRouteReady` seams rather than adding a WhatsApp-only fallback or new config surface.
