## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Yes with maintainer approval: capability-gated deferral in shared dispatch covers both ACP and non-ACP paths better than a Telegram-side delete/rewrite workaround. The remaining decision is whether `captionedFinalText` should become the public plugin contract while separate-delivery policy remains open.
