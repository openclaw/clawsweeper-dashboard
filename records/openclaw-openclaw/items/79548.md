## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.88

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Route bound replies through the channel target resolver:** `src/auto-reply/reply/bind-aware-dispatch.ts:97-105`
  - body: The fallback sends `to` as `binding.conversation.conversationId` and drops `parentConversationId`. Telegram topic bindings are represented as a topic conversation plus parent chat, and the channel contract resolves that to `to=<parent chat>` with `threadId=<topic>`. Bypassing that projection means the reported supergroup topic can still fail or misroute; resolve the binding through the existing conversation route helpers and cover the parent-conversation shape.
  - confidence: 0.92
- **[P1] Do not count bind-routed blocks as dispatcher-visible sends:** `src/auto-reply/reply/dispatch-acp-delivery.ts:469-475`
  - body: After a bind-aware fallback succeeds, `delivered` is true for a block even though no dispatcher send was queued. The later visible-text settlement still compares against the prior dispatcher `failedCounts.block/final`, so a first failed block followed by successful bind-routed blocks can be repeated as the final accumulated fallback. Track bind-routed visible delivery separately, or otherwise prevent the dispatcher-failure fallback from replaying already routed text.
  - confidence: 0.86
