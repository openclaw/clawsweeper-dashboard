Full review comments:

- **[P2] Use a fail-closed reader for success bodies:** `extensions/openrouter/oauth.ts:79-81`
  - body: `readResponseTextLimited` is a diagnostic prefix reader: it cancels at the byte budget and returns the captured text instead of throwing.
