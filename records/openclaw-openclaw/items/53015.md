## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.9

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Preserve QUIT when guarding closed sends:** `extensions/irc/src/client.ts:175-176`
  - body: The new `sendRaw` guard rejects whenever `closed` is true, but `quit()` sets `closed = true` before it calls `sendRaw("QUIT ...")`. Every graceful shutdown can now swallow the QUIT frame and close the socket uncleanly, including the transient path this PR tries to make safer.
  - confidence: 0.94
- **[P2] Tie reclaim timers to client shutdown:** `extensions/irc/src/client.ts:354-369`
  - body: The delayed NickServ GHOST/NICK timers are never stored, cleared, or unref'd. Once scheduled, they can keep one-shot IRC sends alive after `quit()` and can fire against a client that has already closed.
  - confidence: 0.87
- **[P2] Only account-qualify IDENTIFY on fallback nick:** `extensions/irc/src/client.ts:338`
  - body: This call always passes `desiredNick`, so normal connections change from `IDENTIFY <password>` to `IDENTIFY <nick> <password>` even when no fallback nick is in use. Keep the existing form for ordinary connects, or document and prove that maintainers want the compatibility change.
  - confidence: 0.78
