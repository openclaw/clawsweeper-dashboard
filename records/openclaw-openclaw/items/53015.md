## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.9

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Preserve QUIT when guarding closed sends:** `extensions/irc/src/client.ts:175-176`
  - body: The new `sendRaw` guard rejects whenever `closed` is true, but `quit()` sets `closed = true` before calling `sendRaw("QUIT ...")`. Every graceful shutdown now throws inside `quit()`, swallows the error, and closes without sending QUIT; move the closed transition until after QUIT is written or bypass the guard for shutdown.
  - confidence: 0.94
- **[P2] Tie reclaim timers to client shutdown:** `extensions/irc/src/client.ts:354-369`
  - body: The delayed NickServ GHOST/NICK timers are not stored, cleared, or `unref()`'d. Once scheduled, they can keep a one-shot IRC send alive after `quit()` and can fire against a closed client; track these timers and clear or unref them during shutdown.
  - confidence: 0.87
