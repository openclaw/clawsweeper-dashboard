- **[P1] Preserve QUIT when guarding closed sends:** `extensions/irc/src/client.ts:166-168`
  - body: The new `sendRaw` guard rejects whenever `closed` is true, but `quit()` sets `closed = true` before calling `sendRaw("QUIT ...")`.   - confidence: 0.94

  - confidence: 0.87
