The issue reports stale assistant-text replay in long-lived Telegram `openai-codex/*` sessions after a prior tool-call turn, with proposed fixes in OpenAI Codex transport state, Responses payload sanitization/replay policy, and external lossless-claw ignore patterns.

## Solution Assessment
