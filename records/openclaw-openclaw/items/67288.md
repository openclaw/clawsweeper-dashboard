The issue requests that amazon-bedrock-mantle avoid unnecessary implicit IAM discovery and repeated diagnostics when Bedrock Mantle is not configured or needed.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the shipped `discovery.enabled: false` support and resolve the remaining default no-credentials behavior through the linked focused PR or an explicit maintainer decision on that PR.

## Reproduction Assessment
