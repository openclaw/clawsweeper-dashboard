The issue asks OpenClaw to stop Telegram `sendChatAction` typing calls from retrying indefinitely after transient failures by adding bounded retry/backoff or cooldown behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Rebase, repair, and land the existing Telegram handler cooldown PR, or replace it with a fresh narrow fix that preserves 401 suspension, chat-action coalescing, shared retry classifiers, focused tests, and redacted live Telegram proof.

## Solution Assessment

Yes, the requested direction is the right narrow fix boundary. The maintainable solution is handler-local transient cooldown/backoff that keeps current coalescing and retry-helper contracts, not a duplicate issue repair while the open PR is under review.
