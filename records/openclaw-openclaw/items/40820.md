Port the panel into `extensions/telegram/src`, reuse plugin-local native command, callback, and config-write contracts, preserve `streaming.mode` including `progress`, keep access-control defaults fail-closed, add focused extension tests, and attach redacted real Telegram proof.

  - confidence: 0.88
