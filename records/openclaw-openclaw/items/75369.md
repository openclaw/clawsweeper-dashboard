Land a rebased implementation that keeps the `sessions_send` hook and trust-boundary hardening, preserves current-main ACP-child skip plus inter-session provenance and target-scoped lanes, exports/documents the full hook contract, and then supersedes or closes #68622 after merge.

## Solution Assessment

No. The hook and trust-boundary direction is plausible, but this branch is not the best mergeable solution until it is cleanly rebased and the current-main A2A skip, provenance, lane, SDK export, and docs contract are preserved.
