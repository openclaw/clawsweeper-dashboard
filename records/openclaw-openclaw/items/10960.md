The issue requests a soft-steer capability that can interrupt an active generation or long-running operation, inject the user's new message, and resume with that context.

Keep this as the canonical runtime tracker and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/73365 or a clean successor after porting to the current embedded-agent-runner path, deciding delivery bounds, and updating docs/tests for streaming, non-streaming, abort, fallback, and resume behavior.
