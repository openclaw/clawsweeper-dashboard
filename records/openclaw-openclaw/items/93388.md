Ship dependency-checked model mappings: Google Flash as the default, Google Pro preview refs to `gemini-3-pro-image`, and OpenRouter defaults/migrations only to model IDs currently present in OpenRouter's catalog.

No. The Google Flash default is a plausible part of the fix, but the best fix must keep OpenRouter on catalog-backed IDs and preserve Google Pro users by migrating to the stable Pro image model instead of Flash.
