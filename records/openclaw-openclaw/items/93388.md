## Best Possible Solution

Ship provider-contract-backed mappings: Google Flash as the Google default, OpenRouter defaults only on OpenRouter-listed image IDs until its stable ID is published, and Pro preview refs migrated to the stable Pro image model.

No. Updating Google's Flash default is a plausible part of the fix, but the best fix must keep OpenRouter on catalog-backed IDs and preserve explicit Pro-tier configs during doctor migration.
