- **[P1] Preserve topic roots before choosing reply targets:** `extensions/feishu/src/bot.ts:823`
  - body: Clearing `rootId` whenever `mentionedBot && parent_id` runs before `groupSessionScope` is known. In `group_topic` and `group_topic_sender` modes, that can remove the only topic key, collapse the turn into the chat-level session, and route replies away from the intended topic context.
  - confidence: 0.9
