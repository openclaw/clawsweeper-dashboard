## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Yes for the immediate bug: the PR fixes the provider-owned final payload boundary and preserves explicit managed-provider and policy-denied cases. A shared OpenAI/xAI helper may be the cleaner long-term factoring, but that is a broader SDK/OpenAI-surface follow-up.
