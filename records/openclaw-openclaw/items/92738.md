## Best Possible Solution

Land the narrow `message_tool_only` delivery fix after aligning commentary enablement with the exact item-event forwarding predicate, keeping automatic final text private and the final visible answer owned by the `message` tool.

## Solution Assessment

Yes, mostly: forwarding opted-in room-event progress while preserving `message_tool_only` final-text suppression is the narrow owner-boundary fix. The safer refinement is to make commentary enablement use the same forwarding predicate as the callback itself.
