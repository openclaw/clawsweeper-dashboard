- **[P2] Preserve per-agent legacy recall owners during selection:** `src/plugins/slots.ts:144-146`
  - body: The new contract accepts `agents.list[].plugins.slots.memory` as a legacy recall override, and `resolveMemoryRoleSlot` will use it for that agent.
