- **[P1] Do not cache unresolved lineage misses:** `src/gateway/server-node-subscriptions.ts:157`
  - body: When lineage resolution misses, this stores `null` for the child key. Cache only positive ancestor resolutions or add invalidation tied to session writes, and cover the late-spawnedBy case.
