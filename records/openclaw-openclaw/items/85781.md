Keep the shared design on main: preserve valid native Anthropic continuity during normal replay, prevent known signature corruption, strip compaction-stale signatures, and perform one error-triggered retry with all thinking removed; then ship it and verify a formerly poisoned direct-Anthropic session continues without resetting or losing visible context.
