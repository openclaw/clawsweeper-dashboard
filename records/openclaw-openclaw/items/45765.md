## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. Source inspection shows `resolveRequiredHomeDir()` honors explicit `OPENCLAW_HOME`, then `resolveStateDir`, `resolveDefaultConfigCandidates`, and `resolveConfigDir` append `.openclaw`, producing the reported nested path.

## Solution Assessment

No, not yet. The basename guard is the right fix shape for fresh writes, but the issue should be solved by a merged PR that also handles already-created nested state through doctor recovery or an explicit maintainer-owned recovery plan.
