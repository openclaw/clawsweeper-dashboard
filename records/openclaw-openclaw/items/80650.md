The issue reports that full `openclaw backup create` backups fail or hang on Windows with long workspace paths while `--only-config` succeeds.

## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Unclear. Replacing the backend with 7-Zip or excluding workspace `node_modules` by default may be too broad until the current node-tar/Node filesystem failure is reproduced; a narrower path-handling or diagnostic fix may be better.
