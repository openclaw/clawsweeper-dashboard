## What This Changes

Investigate and prevent an upgrade-time cron restart path from treating a correctly API-updated schedule as missed and executing it unexpectedly.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep SQLite authoritative and reproduce two consecutive gateway starts around an API-updated recurring job, capturing the job row after every update, preflight, shutdown, and startup; then repair the first operation that incorrectly makes the future slot due and add a regression test proving the job is not replayed.
