## Summary

## What This Changes

The branch composes `message_sending` into configured dispatcher `beforeDeliver` paths, adds a Telegram delivery-owned opt-out, and gates Telegram streamed preview-finalized replies with cancel/rewrite handling and tests.

## Best Possible Solution

Land the shared dispatcher repair and Telegram streamed-final gate only after suffix sends preserve exactly-once `message_sending` semantics and a Telegram/channel owner confirms the internal opt-out boundary.

## Solution Assessment

No, not as currently written. The combined dispatcher repair plus Telegram streamed-final gate is the right fix shape, but suffix sends must avoid re-entering `deliverReplies` after the full-message gate or the PR breaks its exactly-once contract.
