## Summary

Keep open: current main still has DM-only pairing and group access remains limited to open, disabled, or allowlist behavior, with no pending group approval state, owner notification path, group approve/reject command, or pending reader-agent route. The related WhatsApp auto-leave, Telegram audit, listen-only, and closed neverReply/plugin discussions are adjacent but do not supersede the requested cross-channel group approval flow.

## What This Changes

Request an additive group pairing flow that notifies the owner when a bot joins a new group and optionally admits unconfigured groups in read-only/pending mode.

## Best Possible Solution

Define an additive cross-channel group approval contract with persisted pending state, owner-visible join notifications, authorized approve/reject operations, and Telegram/WhatsApp implementations; split or coordinate pending reader mode with listen-only/hook work.

## Reproduction Assessment

Not applicable as a feature request. Current-main source, docs, and tests give a high-confidence verification path that the requested capability is absent: schemas reject group pairing, pairing remains DM-only, and Telegram/WhatsApp group paths allow or block without pending approval.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainers settle the product and security model. The maintainable direction is a deliberate group approval contract rather than silently reusing DM pairing or treating the listen-only/plugin path as a replacement.
