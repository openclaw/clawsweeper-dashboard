## Summary

Keep open for maintainer review. ## What This Changes

The PR adds optional AbortSignal propagation through QMD memory search and QMD/mcporter CLI subprocess helpers, with regression tests for direct, grouped, mcporter, and already-aborted cancellation paths.

## Best Possible Solution

Land after maintainers accept optional AbortSignal cancellation on the exported QMD helper, or revise to keep cancellation internal while preserving direct, grouped, and mcporter route coverage.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes, with maintainer acceptance. Fixing cancellation at the spawn boundary and threading the signal through direct, grouped, and mcporter QMD paths is narrower than one-off route patches; the remaining decision is the public helper contract.
