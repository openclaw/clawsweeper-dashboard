## What This Changes

Adds optional AbortSignal cancellation to QMD memory search and the exported QMD process helper so timed-out memory_search calls kill orphaned qmd/mcporter subprocesses.

## Best Possible Solution

Land after maintainers accept the exported helper cancellation contract and clear the proof gate; otherwise keep the same route coverage behind a memory-core-owned internal wrapper.

## Solution Assessment

Yes, with maintainer acceptance. Fixing cancellation at the spawn boundary and threading the signal through direct, grouped, and mcporter QMD routes is narrower than patching a single route and preserves no-signal behavior.
