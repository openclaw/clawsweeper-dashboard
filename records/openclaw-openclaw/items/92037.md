- **[P1] Update cron clients and filters for on-exit jobs:** `packages/gateway-protocol/src/schema/cron.ts:226-228`
  - body: Adding `schedule.kind: "on-exit"` to returned cron jobs needs the sibling consumers updated too. Treat `--on-exit-cwd` without `--on-exit` as an error before resolving positional schedules.
  - confidence: 0.78
