## Summary

## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to stop losing or blocking a local plugin's `agent_end` cleanup hook policy after upgrade and to provide a safe way to grant or avoid `allowConversationAccess`.

## Best Possible Solution

Preserve explicit operator-authored hook policy through doctor/update/install or add a maintainer-approved state-only `agent_end` contract, while keeping raw conversation access out of model-mutable config unless the trust model changes.
