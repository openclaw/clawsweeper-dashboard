## Best Possible Solution

Define an upgrade-safe Dreaming promotion contract with provider-aware or explicit threshold calibration plus a privacy-safe signal-source policy, then align memory-core tests/docs and prove it in a live Gemini-backed workspace.

Unclear. Lowering minScore alone is a partial mitigation; the maintainable fix needs maintainer direction on provider calibration, upgrade behavior, and which access paths may become promotion signals.
