## What This Changes

Add a per-channel or general policy that prevents selected sub-agent completion announcements from being delivered directly to user channels.

## Best Possible Solution

Define one backward-compatible completion-delivery policy at the core sub-agent boundary, apply it before both agent-mediated and fallback sends, and then consolidate the duplicate reports under a single canonical issue.
