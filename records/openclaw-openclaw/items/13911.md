Keep this issue open as the per-channel suppression variant and coordinate one backward-compatible completion-delivery policy with the broader suppression and announce-routing trackers before adding public config/tool surface.

Unclear. A per-channel key is plausible, but the maintainable solution should be one completion-delivery policy shared with deterministic skip and parent-routing work rather than competing knobs.
