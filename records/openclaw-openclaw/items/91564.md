## What This Changes

Investigate and fix a Telegram forum topic-specific inbound delivery failure that persists after stuck-session recovery and gateway restarts.

## Best Possible Solution

Add targeted diagnostics around update admission and each pre-dispatch skip reason, reproduce the recovery sequence with a real Telegram forum topic, then fix the single owning invariant so aborted or resumed work cannot leave fresh messages for that topic suppressed across restart while replies and commands take a different path.
