The branch drains internal `message:received` and `message:sent` hook replies into routed/outbound delivery, threads `skipMessageHooks` through route and queue recovery, and adds regression coverage plus a changelog entry.

## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level. Registering an internal `message:received` or `message:sent` hook that pushes text into `event.messages` exercises current-main paths that trigger the hook but never drain those messages into delivery.

## Solution Assessment

Mostly yes, but the final scope needs maintainer judgment.
