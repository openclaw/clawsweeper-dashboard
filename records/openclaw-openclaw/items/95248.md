## What This Changes

The issue reports that Telegram channel ingress claims held by live but non-progressing workers are not released by diagnostic recovery, leaving inbound events stuck until gateway restart.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this open until maintainers either land an ingress recovery design that covers live-but-stalled claims or explicitly narrow the remaining work into the stale-claim and diagnostic recovery PRs already open.

## Reproduction Assessment

Unclear as a direct suggested fix. The right owner is core channel/gateway ingress recovery plus diagnostic progress handling, but force-failing claims from live workers needs a maintainer-approved safety boundary to avoid duplicate or lost delivery.
