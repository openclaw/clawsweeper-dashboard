## Solution Assessment

Yes. Download-failure guards plus structural validation are a bounded maintainable fix for empty, HTML, and truncated downloads; checksum pinning would be brittle for frequently changing third-party setup scripts. The remaining fix is to make the binary test fixture deterministic.
