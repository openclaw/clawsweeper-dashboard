## Summary

Keep open. The Bonjour fix is needed, but the branch is not ready because the new shared classifier can still trigger advertiser recovery in one path, and the bundled WhatsApp QR change is incomplete for restarted login sockets.

## What This Changes

The branch adds a Bonjour/CIAO no-valid-addresses classifier and warning path with tests/changelog, and also routes WhatsApp login QR rendering through the runtime.

## Best Possible Solution

Land a narrow Bonjour fix that classifies this CIAO assertion and treats it as handled without recovery in every Bonjour handler path, with focused tests; complete or split the WhatsApp runtime QR change.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, by source inspection rather than a live IPv6-only interface setup. Current main misses the exact CIAO assertion, @homebridge/ciao 1.3.7 throws it, and unaccepted unhandled rejections reach the fatal handler.

## Solution Assessment

No, not as-is. The classifier direction is right, but every consumer of the shared `no-valid-addresses` kind should treat it as no-recovery, and the WhatsApp QR change should either be completed or split from this Bonjour PR.
