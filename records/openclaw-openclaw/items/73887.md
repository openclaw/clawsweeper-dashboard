## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.86

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Preserve synthetic final payload before retrying:** `src/auto-reply/reply/dispatch-acp-replay.ts:352-364`
  - body: If `recordProjectorPreparedSyntheticFinal()` throws after `resolveSyntheticFinalPayload()` builds the final TTS payload, the retry starts with a fresh coordinator and skips previously checkpointed block effects, so the synthetic final payload can no longer be reconstructed and final TTS is silently skipped. Persist or otherwise carry the payload across that retry, and add a regression for prepare-write failure.
  - confidence: 0.83
