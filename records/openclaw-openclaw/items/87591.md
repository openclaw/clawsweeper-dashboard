## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.78

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Do not rely on a frame delay for picker activation:** `ui/src/ui/chat/session-controls.ts:597`
  - body: This still leaves the original race open: `requestAnimationFrame` runs before a repaint, while `click` is delivered as a user-interaction task, so a held pointer or frame boundary between blur/mousedown and mouseup/click can let the deferred search clear and re-render the picker before the button's `@click` fires. Please fix this at the activation boundary, for example by preventing pointer-induced blur for internal picker controls or by keeping the rows mounted until activation has completed. ([developer.mozilla.org](https://developer.mozilla.org/docs/Web/API/window/requestAnimationFrame))
