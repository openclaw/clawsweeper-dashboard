## Best Possible Solution

Land this narrow runtime/test fix, or an equivalent maintainer-owned patch, so QMD mcporter `search` and `vsearch` preserve their lexical/vector-only contract.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Setting `rerank:false` at the unified mcporter call site for `search` and `vsearch` is the narrowest maintainable fix; direct CLI modes already use dedicated commands and full `query` mode keeps its config-controlled behavior.
