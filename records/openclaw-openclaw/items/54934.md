## Best Possible Solution

Keep the empty-card guard at the Feishu helper/runtime boundary with focused tests, remove the stale send/thread raw-card handling from `channel.ts`, and preserve the shared `presentation` contract for message sends.

## Solution Assessment

No. The helper validation is the narrow maintainable fix, but the PR’s channel hunk revives raw Feishu `card` handling in the shared send action instead of leaving native cards behind the runtime/presentation boundary.
