## Summary

Keep open: current main and v2026.5.20 still only block or drop unauthorized WhatsApp group traffic; they do not provide opt-in auto-leave, a group-join event, or a WhatsApp leave action. This needs maintainer product/security design rather than conservative cleanup closure.

## Best Possible Solution

Design this as an explicit WhatsApp plugin feature with schema/docs, authorized group-leave behavior, participant or join-event handling, minimal metadata exposure, focused tests, and coordination with https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/11489.
