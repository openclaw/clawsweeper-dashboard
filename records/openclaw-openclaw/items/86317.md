## Best Possible Solution

Land the focused handoff fix after maintainer acceptance of the scheduled-sweep policy and normal required checks, keeping direct REM and fallback behavior intact.

## Solution Assessment

Yes: returning light-staged keys from `runLightDreaming` and passing them only through the scheduled sweep is the narrow maintainable fix, with fallback preserving standalone and empty/non-eligible REM behavior. The only remaining judgment is whether maintainers accept the intended consolidation policy change.
