## What This Changes

The PR preserves subagent `alsoAllow` entries in sandbox/tool policy types, includes them in explicit plugin-tool allowlist collection, keeps plugin-group expansion aware of them, and adds `collectExplicitAllowlist()` regression coverage.

## Best Possible Solution

Yes. ## Solution Assessment
