## Best Possible Solution

Land only a rebased Feishu-local or shared-ack revision that has an approved config/default boundary, suppresses the acknowledgement as soon as any visible Feishu output starts, preserves final replies, covers slow DM, fast DM, group chat, and early-visible-output cancellation, and includes redacted real Feishu proof.
