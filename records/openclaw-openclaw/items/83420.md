## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Yes, the wrapper-only direction is the best fit: normalize the agent-tool output in `createWikiLintTool` while keeping lint core, CLI, and gateway callers on their existing contract. The linked PR is the right place to settle the remaining compatibility decision.
