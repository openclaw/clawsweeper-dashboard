## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The heartbeat runner is the right boundary because it has the forced heartbeat-tool state, explicit visibility config, reply metadata, exec-completion state, and final delivery decision; broader dispatch suppression would risk Codex fallback and marked notice behavior.
