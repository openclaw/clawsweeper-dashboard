## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

No as-is: using the existing `sessions.messages.subscribe` and `session.message` contract is the right direction, but the user-echo dedupe needs a narrower local-echo guard and the live iOS group-chat behavior still needs proof or maintainer override.
