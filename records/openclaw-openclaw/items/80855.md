## What This Changes

Preserve WebChat visible messages and final/subagent reports when switching away from a conversation and back.

## Best Possible Solution

Land or reject the focused UI fix in https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/81037, preserving per-session visible WebChat state while reconciling it with durable `chat.history`.
