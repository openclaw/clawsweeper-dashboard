## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.88

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Pass the live config into attachment delivery:** `src/plugins/host-hook-workflow.ts:278-292`
  - body: `sendPluginSessionAttachment` resolves the session route and validates files with `params.config`, but then calls `sendMessage` without `cfg`. Because `sendMessage` falls back to `getRuntimeConfig()` when `cfg` is omitted, hot session-store, channel, account, or media-policy changes can be lost after validation and route lookup, causing the attachment to fail or deliver under stale policy. Pass `cfg: params.config` into this call and cover the handoff.
  - confidence: 0.88
