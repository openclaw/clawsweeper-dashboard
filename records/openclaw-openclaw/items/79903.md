## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue as the focused tracker until maintainers either land a narrow Gateway/SDK lineage-discovery contract or explicitly make https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/79902 plus https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/79962 the canonical remaining path.
