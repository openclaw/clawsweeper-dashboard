  - confidence: 0.93

  - confidence: 0.88

  - body: `startHeartbeat()` runs after the async streaming start is launched, but failed startup and `onCleanup` do not stop it. A dispatcher teardown without `onIdle` or `onError` can leave the interval running after the streaming state is gone.
  - confidence: 0.86
