## Best Possible Solution

Keep raw `ctx.chatId` for dispatcher lifecycle state, add a separate normalized delivery target for visible sends including streaming cards, and require redacted Feishu p2p proof or maintainer override before merge.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

No. Routing p2p visible sends to `user:<open_id>` is the right goal, but replacing dispatcher `chatId` is the wrong boundary because that field also drives streaming-card receive IDs and conversation typing.
