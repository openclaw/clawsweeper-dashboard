## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

No. Routing p2p visible sends to `user:<open_id>` is the right goal, but replacing the dispatcher `chatId` is the wrong shape because that field also drives streaming-card and lifecycle state.
