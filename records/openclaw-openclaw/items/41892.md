## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.97

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Build the timeline from the complete job set:** `ui/src/ui/views/cron.ts:539`
  - body: `props.jobs` is only the currently loaded `cron.list` page, while the page explicitly supports `jobsHasMore`. With more than 50 matching jobs, or before subsequent pages are loaded, the timeline silently omits schedules and cannot provide the advertised daily overview. Fetch a complete bounded timeline dataset or expose aggregated occurrence data separately from list pagination.
  - confidence: 0.98
- **[P1] Use the scheduler's exact per-job stagger offset:** `ui/src/ui/helpers/cron-timeline.ts:384-388`
  - body: Adding `staggerMs / 2` does not match runtime behavior: `src/cron/service/jobs.ts` hashes the job ID to choose a stable offset anywhere within the stagger window. The timeline can therefore place a job minutes away from its actual run and incorrectly classify it as past or pending. Share or expose the exact scheduler-computed occurrence instead of using the midpoint.
  - confidence: 0.99
- **[P1] Do not treat unsupported Croner constraints as wildcards:** `ui/src/ui/helpers/cron-timeline.ts:431-436`
  - body: Croner 10.0.1 supports `L`, `W`, and `#`, but this parser skips those tokens and tells callers to treat an empty restricted field as unrestricted. A last-day or nth-weekday job can consequently be plotted every day. Enumerate occurrences through Croner or fail closed for expressions the timeline cannot evaluate exactly.
  - confidence: 0.97
