## Solution Assessment

No, not quite yet; the broad fix is maintainable, but accepted interaction status should be reported when the Discord command/component is accepted, not after the entire handler finishes.

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Record accepted interactions before awaiting handlers:** `extensions/discord/src/monitor/listeners.ts:72-74`
