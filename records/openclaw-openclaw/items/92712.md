## Best Possible Solution

Replace the blanket flag clear with an owner-scoped recovery path that releases or fails only the exact stale main-session owner while preserving subagent and queued recovery markers.

## Solution Assessment

No. Clearing `abortedLastRun` globally after retries is too broad; the safer fix is to identify and release or fail the stale main-session owner without changing other recovery contracts.
