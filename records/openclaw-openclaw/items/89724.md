- **[P1] Gate Teams transcripts on recording status:** `extensions/voice-call/src/providers/msteams.ts:279-287`
  - body: This persists `call.speech` and starts response generation for media-derived Teams transcripts without any protocol state proving the external worker first called Microsoft Graph `updateRecordingStatus` and received success. Add a bridge recording-status message/state machine and hold or drop transcript processing until recording is confirmed active.
  - confidence: 0.91
- **[P1] Fail before binding Teams without STT/TTS:** `extensions/voice-call/src/runtime.ts:545-564`
  - body: When `streaming.enabled` is false or the STT/TTS provider cannot be resolved, this branch still starts the Teams WebSocket listener, so calls can connect and then close as `not-ready` or fail on playback. Make msteams config/runtime validation fail before `msteamsProvider.start()` unless the full speech pipeline is available.
  - confidence: 0.88
- **[P2] Buffer early Teams audio until STT connects:** `extensions/voice-call/src/providers/msteams.ts:263-265`
  - body: Audio frames that arrive after `session.start` but before the async STT connection flips `isConnected()` are silently dropped, which can lose the caller's first words on a real Teams call. Keep a small bounded pre-connect buffer and flush it after connect, or do not let the bridge send media until STT is ready.
  - confidence: 0.9
- **[P2] Document Teams setup before exposing the provider:** `extensions/voice-call/src/config.ts:422-435`
  - body: The provider enum/config is public, but the voice-call docs still list only Twilio, Telnyx, Plivo, and mock and do not explain the external worker, HMAC headers, bind address, recording-status obligation, inbound-only scope, or required STT/TTS setup.
