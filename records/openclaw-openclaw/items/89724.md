  - confidence: 0.92

  - confidence: 0.88

  - body: The provider drops every caller audio frame received before `sttSession.isConnected()` becomes true. On a live call, early speech can arrive immediately after `session.start`, so the first words of the caller's turn can be silently lost; buffer bounded pre-ready audio or block the bridge until STT readiness is acknowledged.
  - confidence: 0.86
