The issue reports that Mattermost edit-in-place streaming overwrites or batches multi-turn assistant responses instead of preserving each turn as a separate streamed post.

## Best Possible Solution

Finish, rebase, or replace https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/70508 with a Mattermost-local fix that seals streamed assistant text before assistant, reasoning, and tool boundaries, plus focused regression coverage.
