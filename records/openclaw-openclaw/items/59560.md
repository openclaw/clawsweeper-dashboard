## What This Changes

Request a WhatsApp per-contact messaging policy that independently controls inbound handling, explicit outbound sends, and autonomous/autoreply delivery at root and account config scopes.

## Best Possible Solution

Design one WhatsApp-owned typed contact policy with schema/docs, root/account field-level merge semantics, inbound admission enforcement before side effects, explicit outbound policy errors, autonomous-reply dispatch guards, and focused regression coverage.

Not applicable as a feature request, but source inspection is high-confidence: current schema, docs, account resolution, inbound access, outbound target resolution, and sendPolicy behavior lack the requested per-contact policy.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainers choose the public config contract. The maintainable direction is hard WhatsApp runtime enforcement across inbound, explicit outbound, and autoreply paths, not prompts, memory, dummy agents, or session sendPolicy alone.
