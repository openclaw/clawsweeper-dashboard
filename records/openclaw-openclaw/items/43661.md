The issue reports that timeout-triggered compaction can wedge a session and replay outbound message side effects, causing duplicate sends and manual recovery.

## Best Possible Solution

Define one replay-safe timeout-compaction contract that carries stable outbound idempotency through compaction retries or blocks replay after side effects, with a separate recovery path for oversized wedged sessions.
