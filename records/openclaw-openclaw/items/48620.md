## Summary

Keep open: current main and v2026.6.10 still do not provide one admin-facing inventory for approved WhatsApp contacts with merged DM/group permission state and last-active data. Existing pairing, directory, channel status, and `/allowlist` surfaces cover pieces, but this remains a security-sensitive product decision rather than conservative cleanup.

## What This Changes

The issue requests a CLI/admin surface to list and manage approved WhatsApp paired contacts, permissions, and last-active details.

## Best Possible Solution

Define one account-scoped admin inventory and permission-management surface that coordinates approved-pairing state, configured allowlists, group policy, and directory lookup without duplicating `/allowlist`.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level: current `pairing list`, `directory peers list`, `channels status`, and `/allowlist` do not expose one approved WhatsApp contact inventory with merged permission state and last-active data.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainers choose the product and security semantics. A CLI inventory is plausible, but it should be account-scoped and coordinated with https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/56621 and `/allowlist`.
