## Risks / Open Questions

- Automatic preservation and synthesis can retain sensitive conversation content unless retention, redaction, and sanitizer rules are explicit.
- Provider-backed synthesis can send recent transcript content to the configured model provider and may affect reset/startup latency unless the behavior is opt-in and bounded.
- Existing raw or poisoned memory artifacts may need operator cleanup or quarantine even after a forward-only synthesis/sanitizer design lands.
