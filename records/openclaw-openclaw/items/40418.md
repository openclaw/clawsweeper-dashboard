## Risks / Open Questions

- Automatic transcript preservation and rehydration can retain sensitive or prompt-hostile content unless retention, redaction, and sanitizer policy are explicit.
- Model-backed synthesis during reset/startup can expose recent transcript content to a provider and affect reset latency unless it is clearly opt-in and bounded.
- The related synthesis PR and sanitizer issue remain open, so current main should not be treated as having accepted or shipped this broader feature.
