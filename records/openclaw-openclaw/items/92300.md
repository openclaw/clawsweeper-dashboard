Preserve separate Responses output items by default and mitigate the Mantle anomaly only through a defensible provider or response-level signal that both consumers can apply without treating ordinary text similarity as identity.

## Solution Assessment

No. The proposed mitigation solves the reported sequence but discards output-item identity and applies the provider anomaly generically, so it needs a narrower and demonstrably safe recognition boundary.
