Recover Anthropic Claude sessions that become stuck when preserved thinking or redacted_thinking blocks are rejected after a prompt-cache miss.

## Solution Assessment

Yes, the narrow code direction is the best fit. A one-shot retry after the exact Anthropic thinking-block modification error is safer than stripping thinking at persistence time or changing the general Claude 4.6 replay policy.
