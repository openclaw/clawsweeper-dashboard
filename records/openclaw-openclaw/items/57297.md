## Summary

Keep open: current main and v2026.6.8 have TTS provider-attempt diagnostics plus WebChat audio rendering, but they still do not provide explicit WebChat TTS route/delivery outcome events, correlation ids, or a final user-visible TTS delivery status. The open playback work at https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/81468 and https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/86759 overlaps the delivery boundary but does not supersede this diagnostics/status request.

## What This Changes

Add explicit WebChat TTS generation, route, and delivery telemetry with correlation ids plus optional final UI delivery status.

## Best Possible Solution

Define a small correlated WebChat TTS diagnostics contract at the Gateway delivery boundary, reuse existing provider-attempt metadata, emit route/delivery outcomes, and surface final status without storing arbitrary diagnostic prose in transcripts.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes at source level. Current main has synthesis attempt metadata and WebChat audio rendering, but the requested route/delivery events, correlation ids, and final WebChat TTS delivery status are absent from the relevant source paths.
