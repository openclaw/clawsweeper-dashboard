## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.9

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Acquire the delivery lane before queue work can reorder sends:** `src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts:1278-1279`
  - body: The new lock is only taken here, after `enqueueDelivery(...)` and `withActiveDeliveryClaim(...)` have both awaited. Two same-recipient sends can still enter the serializer in queue-write completion order rather than caller order, so the later reply can be delivered first. Acquire the per-target lane before persisting/claiming the queue entry and keep the queue send/cleanup inside that lane.
  - confidence: 0.93
- **[P1] Key delivery lanes from canonical channel targets:** `src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts:1274`
  - body: The key uses raw `params.accountId` and `params.to`, while current channel paths normalize account ids and destinations later, including Telegram/Signal prefixes and Discord thread routing. Equivalent destinations can bypass the serializer, and distinct threads can block one another. Build the key from the canonical target ref, including thread/topic identity.
  - confidence: 0.9
- **[P2] Make queued lanes abort- and stall-aware:** `src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts:1278-1279`
  - body: Once a send enters this wrapper, every later same-key delivery waits for its promise. The serializer has no abort or timeout/release path, and abort is only checked inside `deliverOutboundPayloadsCore`, so a cancelled or never-settling adapter call can wedge later replies to that conversation until restart. Add bounded release/abort behavior and tests before holding this lane.
  - confidence: 0.82
