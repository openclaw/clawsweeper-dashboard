## Summary

Keep open. Current main and v2026.5.22 still do not expose or apply a hook/webhook-specific lifecycle policy for on-complete archive/delete, delayed cleanup, transcript retention, or completed-session visibility, and the adjacent ephemeral-session issue plus persistent-hook-session PR do not supersede this hook cleanup request.

## What This Changes

Request a configurable cleanup and archival policy for completed hook/webhook sessions, especially Gmail hook sessions, with optional transcript retention and completed-session visibility behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Define an additive opt-in hook lifecycle policy covering direct /hooks/agent, hook mappings, Gmail presets, generated hook:* keys, delayed or on-complete archive/delete behavior, transcript retention, and completed-session visibility.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainers choose lifecycle semantics. The maintainable direction is an explicit opt-in hook cleanup policy, not overloading generic session maintenance, cron retention, subagent archive settings, or deleteAfterRun.
