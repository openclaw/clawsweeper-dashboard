## Summary

Keep open: current main has partial durable final-delivery work, but the remaining Telegram polling-stall loss is still source-reproducible through non-durable streamed follow-up chunks and the lack of a Telegram polling-restart drain for queued outbound work. Recent issue comments also add latest-version WSL2 evidence, so this is not stale or implemented.

## What This Changes

The issue asks for Telegram outbound delivery recovery during polling stalls so computed replies are not silently lost and safe pending sends can recover without requiring a gateway restart.

## Best Possible Solution

Add a maintainer-approved Telegram recovery design that makes final and overflow reply chunks durable, drains eligible queued Telegram work after polling recovery as well as startup, and preserves ambiguous sends instead of blindly replaying them.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level. Force a streamed final follow-up chunk or a queued Telegram final delivery to fail during polling recovery; current main either sends follow-up chunks without durable state or only recovers queued work at startup, not from a Telegram polling-restart drain.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear. The durable queue direction is the right foundation, but the remaining implementation needs maintainer judgment for non-idempotent Telegram sends, polling-restart drain timing, and streamed overflow semantics.
