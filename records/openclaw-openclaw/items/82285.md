  - body: The proxy binds to localhost and forwards non-Anthropic CONNECT targets instead of rejecting them. During a run, any same-host process that can discover the port may use this OpenClaw child process as a transient CONNECT proxy, so maintainers should approve or narrow the forwarding model.
