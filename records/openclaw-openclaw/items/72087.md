Start from `src/entry.ts`, `openclaw.mjs`, `src/entry.respawn.ts`, `src/entry.compile-cache.ts`, `src/cli/run-main.ts`, `src/infra/net/undici-global-dispatcher.ts`, `src/infra/net/proxy/proxy-lifecycle.ts`, and `extensions/openai/image-generation-provider.ts`.
