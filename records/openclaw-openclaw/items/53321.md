## What This Changes

Request a configurable automatic session split near the context-window limit that flushes memory, creates a fresh backing session, injects continuity context, and routes later messages to it.

Design one core session-lifecycle policy for context-pressure rollover with canonical config, durable handoff semantics, delivery identity preservation, idempotent rotation, docs, and upgrade tests.
