The issue asks OpenClaw to preserve OpenRouter MiniMax M3 `reasoning` and `reasoning_details` in assistant history across plain-text and tool-call turns.

## Best Possible Solution

Preserve MiniMax M3 OpenRouter reasoning at the parser, replay, or model-attribution boundary with regression coverage for both plain-text and tool-call turns.
