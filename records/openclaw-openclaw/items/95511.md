## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

No as written. Preserving launchd-managed gateways in PortGuardian is the right layer, but the matcher should require tokenized `gateway` subcommand detection and known OpenClaw entrypoints instead of a broad `openclaw/dist` substring.
