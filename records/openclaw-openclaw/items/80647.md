## Best Possible Solution

Land one privacy-preserving warning diagnostic for suppressed message-tool-only finals, keeping normal finals private and leaving auto-forwarding as a separate product decision.

Yes for the canonical direction: the open warning-diagnostic PR is narrower and safer than auto-repair because it surfaces the failure without posting private final text into the group topic.
