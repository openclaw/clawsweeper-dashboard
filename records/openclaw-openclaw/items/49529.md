## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.84

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Scope the persisted Z.AI tool_stream wrapper:** `src/agents/pi-embedded-runner/extra-params.ts:468`
  - body: This wrapper is installed during a Z.AI run, but `createZaiToolStreamWrapper` patches every later payload it sees and is never stripped or gated by the call model. Since the runner can reuse and further wrap `agent.streamFn` across attempts, a later non-Z.AI OpenAI-compatible provider can inherit `tool_stream=true`, which strict backends may reject as an unknown top-level field.
  - confidence: 0.86
- **[P2] Unwrap stale Google safety wrappers in real runs:** `src/agents/pi-embedded-runner/extra-params.ts:333-338`
  - body: `stripGoogleSafetySettingsWrapper` only removes the wrapper when it is the top-level `streamFn`, but `run/attempt.ts` wraps the stream again after `applyExtraParamsToAgent`. On the next attempt the stale safety wrapper can sit underneath cache/transcript/tool wrappers, so updated or removed `safetySettings` can still be injected despite the new tests that only cover direct top-level reapplication.
  - confidence: 0.82
