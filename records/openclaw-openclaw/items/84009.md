## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

No, not yet. Reusing the gateway reset service from maintenance is the right shape for daily wall-clock rollover, but the scheduler should not let idle expiry trigger proactive resets unless that broader behavior is explicitly chosen and documented.
