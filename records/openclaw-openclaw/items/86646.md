## Best Possible Solution

Keep the same-run dedup approach, but make duplicate visible terminal payloads skip stream fallback entirely, add stale-stream regression coverage, and prove the WebChat or Gateway race after the fix.

## Solution Assessment

No, not yet; same-run dedup in the WebChat controller is the right layer, but duplicate visible terminal payloads must be treated as handled instead of falling through to stream fallback.
