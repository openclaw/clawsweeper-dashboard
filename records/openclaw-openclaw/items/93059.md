## Best Possible Solution

Put the parse-error fallback on `sendTelegramText` or a shared helper used by ordinary reply delivery, keep HTML-mode retries readable, and require redacted live Telegram proof before merge.

## Reproduction Assessment

Do we have a high-confidence way to reproduce the issue? ## Solution Assessment

Is this the best way to solve the issue? No. The fallback belongs in the Telegram plugin boundary, but the linked ordinary direct-reply bug needs coverage at `sendTelegramText` or a shared helper used by reply delivery, plus readable HTML-mode fallback text.
