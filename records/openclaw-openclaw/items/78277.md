Yes. Source inspection gives a high-confidence path where independent setup-code and Gateway processes can load stale `devices/bootstrap.json` snapshots and later overwrite bootstrap-token binding or revocation under only process-local serialization.

No. Cross-process locking is the right narrow direction, but this patch leaves one reachable mutating writer on the old lock path and does not prove the actual stale-snapshot race.
