Keep open. Current main and the latest release support manual multi-agent workspaces plus sender-scoped tool-policy primitives, but they still lack the requested first-class team scaffolding command, declarative deployment manifest, and reviewed session/RBAC authorization contract; related issues and PRs cover narrower slices rather than superseding this umbrella request.
