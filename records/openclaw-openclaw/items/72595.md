## Best Possible Solution

Settle one Feishu-owned egress contract across Lark SDK REST/token HTTP, WebSocket bootstrap/socket traffic, guarded-fetch calls, docs, and focused tests before merging or closing the partial PRs.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainers choose the egress policy. Docs-only NO_PROXY guidance is the lowest-risk mitigation, while a proxyMode or default runtime change needs product and security approval.
