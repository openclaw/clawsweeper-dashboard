## Summary

## Best Possible Solution

Land a maintainer-approved one-shot native `sessions_spawn.toolsAllow` contract here if the API/security semantics are accepted, while keeping broader deny-list, durable policy, and unsupported-runtime decisions tracked through https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/15032.
