- **[P2] Measure interleaved caps after Telegram rendering:** `extensions/telegram/src/bot-message-dispatch.ts:1329-1333`
  - body: This caps and spills the Thinking lane based on raw markdown, but the draft stream sends `renderTelegramHtmlText(...)` and enforces Telegram's 4096-character limit on that rendered HTML. Please compute spill/truncation against the rendered Telegram text or reuse the Telegram chunking path, and add an escaped-text regression.
