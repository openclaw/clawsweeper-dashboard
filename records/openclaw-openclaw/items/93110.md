Full review comments:

- **[P1] Retire route leases from the completion lifecycle:** `src/infra/delivery-lease-store.ts:54-62`
  - body: This store evicts the oldest active lease once the process exceeds 2000 entries, but PR head does not call `retireDeliveryLease` from production. The lease needs a shared background completion/delivery retirement hook after final delivery settles, not TTL/cap-only cleanup.
  - confidence: 0.9
