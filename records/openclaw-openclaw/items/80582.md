## What This Changes

Prevent markdown placeholder and skeleton snippets from being recorded, ranked, relocated, or applied as memory-core short-term promotion candidates.

## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Yes. A shared unpromotable-snippet predicate at every promotion entry and reuse boundary is the narrow maintainable fix, and the linked PR follows that shape.
