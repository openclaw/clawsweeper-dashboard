## What This Changes

The issue reports repeated context-overflow auto-compactions in tool-heavy sessions after successful compaction, causing confusing recovery loops and lost useful replies.

## Best Possible Solution

Settle one shared overflow-recovery contract: use reliable current-prompt usage for overflow decisions, add a circuit breaker for successful-compaction-then-irreducible recovery, and prove tool-heavy Telegram/Slack/subagent paths do not re-enter repeated compaction loops.

Unclear as a single narrow patch. The best fix should cover overflow ordering, no-real-conversation circuit breaking, and usage-accounting sanity together rather than landing one isolated mitigation that leaves sibling loops intact.
