## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Likely yes: deferring release until after the active retained writer unwinds is narrower and safer than force-releasing while that writer still owns the write scope. Broader force-release or bounded-dispose fixes may still be needed for adjacent failure modes, but they are not safer replacements for this active-scope fix.
