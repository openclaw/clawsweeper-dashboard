- **[P2] Bound DSML recovery buffering:** `src/agents/openai-transport-stream.ts:2583-2588`
  - body: This appends every DeepSeek text delta into `pendingDeepSeekDsmlText`, and `tryFlushDeepSeekDsmlToolText(false)` scans that whole accumulated string on each subsequent delta until a DSML close tag or final flush.
