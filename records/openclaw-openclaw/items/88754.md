  - confidence: 0.92

  - body: The new fixtures close with `『/log』`, which normalizes to `</log>` and is not recognized by the reasoning/final tag matchers. These assertions exercise malformed or unclosed paths rather than proving fullwidth close-tag stripping; use valid close tags such as `『/think』` and keep malformed coverage separate if needed.
  - confidence: 0.9
