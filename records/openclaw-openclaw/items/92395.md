## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to stop losing queued human follow-up messages when a long-running turn keeps or reacquires the session JSONL write lock long enough for later turns to time out.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the remaining engineering discussion and fix work on https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/86538, then either land a rebased linked PR or split a narrower follow-up that specifically makes human queued follow-ups durable and user-visible on lock contention.
