## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.88

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Overlay current pricing before disk warm return:** `src/gateway/model-pricing-cache.ts:1182-1185`
  - body: A fresh disk cache returns before the refresh collects explicit models.providers.*.models[].cost and manifest catalog costs. That lets stale persisted remote data win over current local pricing until the cache TTL expires; merge seeded pricing over disk data before returning.
  - confidence: 0.91
- **[P2] Retry after the circuit cooldown:** `src/gateway/model-pricing-cache.ts:1232`
  - body: When the circuit is open, this calls scheduleRefresh, but that helper waits the 24h cache TTL. After upstream recovery, pricing refresh can stay skipped until the daily timer instead of retrying after the 10-minute cooldown.
  - confidence: 0.9
- **[P3] Schedule disk refresh for the remaining TTL:** `src/gateway/model-pricing-cache.ts:1184`
  - body: A warm disk cache always schedules the next live refresh for a full TTL from process start. A 23-hour-old cache can remain in use for almost another day, and repeated restarts can keep extending that delay.
  - confidence: 0.84
