Keep open. Current main now has a narrow Telegram startup `getMe` probe limiter, but the reported v2026.5.12-beta.1 failure also includes session-reset hangs, polling stalls, `sessions.list` latency, and no-active-work recovery behavior that are not proven fixed by the partial landed change.
