The issue asks for non-critical loop-detection warnings from fresh isolated cron sessions to be logged at info instead of warn so they stop filling gateway.err.log.

Review and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/90257 or a narrow replacement that threads cron/isolation context into the loop-warning emitter while preserving critical and non-cron warn behavior.
