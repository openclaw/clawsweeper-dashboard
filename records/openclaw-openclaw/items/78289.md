Fix the Feishu interactive-card readback bug tracked by https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/78289. On current main, `message(action=read)` delegates to `getMessageFeishu`, and both `getMessageFeishu` and `listFeishuThreadMessages` fetch Feishu messages without requesting full card content, so interactive cards can parse as `[Interactive Card]`. Do not change core message-tool APIs, add config, or refactor unrelated Feishu inbound/card parsing; coordinate with the broader open PR https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/56795 rather than duplicating unrelated inbound RawBody work.
