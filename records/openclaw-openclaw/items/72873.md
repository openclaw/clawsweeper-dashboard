## Summary

## What This Changes

Handle Telegram `edited_message` updates so edited or streaming Telegram bot messages are debounced and dispatched as final inbound content.

## Best Possible Solution

Land or replace #73104 with a Telegram-owned fix that registers an edit-aware inbound handler, debounces by chat/message id, avoids bot echo loops, and adds focused regression coverage.
