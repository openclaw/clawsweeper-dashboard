## What This Changes

The issue proposes a model-callable `clear_goal` tool that clears terminal or paused goals while archiving bounded goal history on `SessionEntry`.

## Best Possible Solution

Decide the model-authority boundary first; if approved, implement a narrow canonical-store transition with documented clearable states, mutation/catalog coverage, lifecycle tests, docs, and real agent-turn proof.

## Risks / Open Questions

- Allowing models to clear paused, budget-limited, or usage-limited goals could bypass states that are currently operator/session-controlled.
- Persisting `clearedGoals` on `SessionEntry` needs explicit retention, reset, export, synchronization, and privacy semantics before it becomes a durable contract.
- A fixed archive of full goal snapshots could grow canonical session state and expose historical objectives or notes to every session-store consumer without a defined retrieval requirement.
