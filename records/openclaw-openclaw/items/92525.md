## Best Possible Solution

Decide the model-authority boundary first; if approved, implement the narrowest allowed clear states with canonical single-write storage, metadata/docs/tests, and real agent-turn proof.

## Reproduction Assessment

- Allowing models to clear paused or limited goals could bypass states that currently require explicit operator action.

- A fixed archive of full goal snapshots may grow session state and expose historical objectives/notes to every session-store consumer without a defined retrieval need.
