The issue asks Slack `room_event` handling to respect or clearly override `visibleReplies: "automatic"`, and to align Slack typing/status feedback with suppressed automatic final delivery.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep strict ambient `room_event` delivery unless maintainers intentionally revise that contract, and decide whether Slack should suppress, change, or document typing/status feedback during tool-only room events.
