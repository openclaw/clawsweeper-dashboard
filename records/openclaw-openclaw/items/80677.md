Keep open. Current main still has source-level paths that match the stale queued diagnostic state, active-reply visibility gap, and direct Telegram silent-reply symptoms, while the relevant fixes remain open PRs or require coordinated Telegram delivery behavior proof.

## What This Changes

Investigate a Telegram gateway bug where completed or compacted runs can leave lane state queued and produce misleading silent no-op replies after tool or delivery failures.

## Best Possible Solution

Land or replace the active-reply diagnostics and `noop/no_active_work` recovery fixes, then add Telegram delivery handling that proves compacted or failed turns clear queued state and do not emit silent no-op receipts for substantive follow-ups.

## Solution Assessment
