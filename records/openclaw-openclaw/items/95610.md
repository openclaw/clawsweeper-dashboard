## What This Changes

The issue reports that OpenAI/Codex prompt-cache reuse is defeated because per-turn message-tool descriptions and volatile system-prompt suffixes change inside the provider-visible prefix.

## Best Possible Solution

Stabilize `message` tool schemas across turns, move OpenAI/Codex volatile guidance out of the provider-visible prefix or make that path truly boundary-aware, and prove it with focused cache-trace plus live provider cache evidence.

Is this the best way to solve the issue? Yes at the direction level: stable tool schemas plus provider-appropriate placement of volatile OpenAI/Codex guidance is the narrow maintainable fix, but the exact owner boundary needs maintainer review.
