The issue asks OpenClaw to keep OpenAI prompt-cache prefixes stable by moving per-turn `message_tool_only` guidance out of tool schemas and volatile system-prompt prefix content.

## Best Possible Solution

Make the `message` tool schema byte-stable, move the per-turn visible-reply directive into OpenAI/Codex turn-scoped content after the cached prefix, and prove both message delivery and prompt-cache reuse.
