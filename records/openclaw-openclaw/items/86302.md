The branch filters memory-core REM dreaming candidates to live pending light-staged entries, records a separate REM-considered timestamp, and adds short-term promotion regression coverage.

Yes, the final patch is a narrow maintainable direction: it filters to pending light-staged live entries, falls back when staged keys are absent or stale, and avoids giving every considered key REM promotion credit.
