## Summary

Keep open. Current main and latest release still expose and consume model-supplied exec policy controls, and #76370 is open with explicit closing intent rather than merged implementation.

## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to stop exposing and honoring model-supplied exec policy controls such as `security`, `ask`, `host`, `elevated`, and `node`.

## Best Possible Solution

Review and land #76370 or an equivalent patch that removes or ignores model-supplied exec policy controls while preserving trusted config/session defaults and regression coverage.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. Source inspection on current main shows the policy fields are exposed in the exec schema, forwarded to OpenAI tool parameters, and consumed during exec dispatch into the reported denial path.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Splitting model-facing command/runtime args from operator/session policy is the narrow maintainable fix; a docs-only or message-only change would leave the boundary intact.
