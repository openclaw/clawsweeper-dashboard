## Summary

Keep open. Current main still exposes and consumes model-supplied exec policy controls, and the linked open PR is the active fix path rather than a reason to close before merge.

## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to keep exec policy/routing controls out of model-visible tool-call arguments and source them from trusted config or session defaults instead.

## Best Possible Solution

Review and land #76370 or an equivalent patch that removes or ignores model-supplied exec policy controls while preserving trusted config and session-default policy paths with regression coverage.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. Source inspection on current main shows the policy fields are exposed in the exec schema, forwarded into OpenAI tool parameters, and consumed during exec dispatch into the reported denial path.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The right fix direction is to split model-facing command/runtime args from operator/session policy and normalize or ignore model-supplied policy controls; a docs-only or message-only change would leave the boundary intact.
