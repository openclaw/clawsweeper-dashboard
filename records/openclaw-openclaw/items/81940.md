The issue requests fixing or clearly diagnosing iMessage `imsg rpc` readiness hangs when Gateway runs under the macOS LaunchAgent.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this open and make LaunchAgent iMessage startup either work through a supported macOS process identity or fail fast with explicit launchd/TCC/session guidance while cleaning up stuck `imsg` children.
