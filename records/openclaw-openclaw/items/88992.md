- **[P1] Preserve retry identity through collect drains:** `src/auto-reply/reply/agent-runner.ts:2335`
  - body: The one-shot guard only checks `followupRun.summaryLine`, but supported collect queues wrap same-target `user_request` followups into a new `FollowupRun` in `queue/drain.ts` without carrying `summaryLine`.
