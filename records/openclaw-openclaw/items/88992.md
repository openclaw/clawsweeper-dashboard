## Best Possible Solution

Defer stranded-reply retry draining until after the active reply operation clears, keep recovery default-off and persistence-suppressed, then get maintainer acceptance for the opt-in privacy semantics and transport proof.

Yes. Source inspection and the checked-in QA scenarios show the path: a `message_tool_only` turn produces a long final answer without a `message` tool call, current main warns but delivers no outbound, and the PR's scenario drives the recovery loop.

## Solution Assessment

No, not yet. The default-off recovery shape is plausible and safer than bypass delivery, but the current enqueue scheduling is unsafe and maintainer acceptance of replaying private finals remains required.
