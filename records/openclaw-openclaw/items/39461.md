## Best Possible Solution

Land or replace https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/78253 with a centralized QQBot data-root helper that honors a validated QQBOT_DATA_DIR, keeps diagnostics aligned with the effective path, and covers default plus override behavior.

Yes in direction. The narrow maintainable fix is to centralize the QQBot data-root decision in the existing path helper, but this issue should remain open until the linked PR lands or a replacement does.
