- **[P1] Guard manifest reads against symlink swaps:** `packages/memory-host-sdk/src/host/continuity.ts:635-651`
  - body: `buildContinuityManifest` starts from `listMemoryFiles()`, but then re-stats with `fs.stat()` and reads through `readHead()`/`fs.open()`.
