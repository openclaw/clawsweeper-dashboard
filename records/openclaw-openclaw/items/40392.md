## Best Possible Solution

Keep the timestamp direction only if the transition preserves or migrates legacy numbered backups, keeps bounded retention and owner-only permissions, rebases cleanly, and includes real fresh-install plus upgrade filesystem proof.

No. `src/config/backup-rotation.ts` is the right boundary for suffix changes, but the best fix must preserve or migrate legacy numeric backups instead of deleting them during the transition.
