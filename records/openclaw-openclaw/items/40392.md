  - body: `cleanOrphanBackups` now deletes every non-datetime `.bak.*` file, including shipped `.bak.1` through `.bak.4` backups.   - body: `rotateConfigBackups` writes directly to one millisecond timestamp path and swallows the rename result.   - confidence: 0.87
