## Best Possible Solution

Keep the JSON-before-schema split, classify by the top-level ActivityStreams event, acknowledge valid non-message hooks without dispatch, preserve 400 for malformed JSON or malformed supported messages, and add real signed-webhook proof.

## Solution Assessment

No, not yet. JSON-before-schema is the right layer, but `object.type === "Note"` is too broad because official reaction hooks can embed `Note` inside non-message ActivityStreams activities.
