Keep the JSON-before-schema split, classify supported chat messages by top-level ActivityStreams type, acknowledge valid non-message hooks including Like and Undo without dispatch, preserve 400 for malformed JSON or malformed chat-message payloads, and require signed-webhook or live Talk proof.
