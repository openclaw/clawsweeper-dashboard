## Summary

Keep open: current main still has Telegram delivery mirrors written as assistant transcript updates, while the session-message projection and latest-assistant transcript reader do not apply the transcript-only provider/model guard. The linked Slack guard and same-text dedupe are relevant prior fixes, but they do not cover the reported different-text earlier-turn mirror replacement path.

## What This Changes

A Telegram direct-chat bug report asks OpenClaw to stop delivery-mirror transcript-only assistant entries from being delivered back to Telegram and replacing the real reply.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep delivery-mirror transcript persistence, but filter provider=openclaw delivery-mirror and gateway-injected assistant records out of every Telegram-visible finalization, session-update, or latest-assistant replay path, with a regression covering a real reply followed by an earlier-turn mirror.

## Reproduction Assessment

No high-confidence runnable reproduction is available yet. The report has detailed production timing evidence, and source inspection shows likely unfiltered transcript-update/latest-assistant paths, but the preserved JSONL and live Telegram setup were not available in this read-only review.

## Solution Assessment

Yes, directionally: a structural provider/model filter before Telegram-visible delivery or finalization is the narrow maintainable fix. Same-text delivery-mirror dedupe should remain only a safety net because it cannot block earlier-turn mirror content with different text.
