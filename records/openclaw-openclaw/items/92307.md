## Best Possible Solution

Keep the startup warning, but make its host-ownership predicate mirror runtime session semantics: warn for gateway-owned main/no-session auto exec under `non-main`, and suppress only fully sandbox-owned or node-managed scopes.

## Solution Assessment

No. A startup warning is the right layer, but the predicate is too broad; the narrow maintainable fix is to mirror runtime gateway ownership before suppressing the warning.
