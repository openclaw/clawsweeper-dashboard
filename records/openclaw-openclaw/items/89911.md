Full review comments:

- **[P1] Carry scoped metadata in transcript updates:** `src/config/sessions/session-accessor.ts:263-266`
  - body: `publishTranscriptUpdate` resolves a scoped transcript target but emits only `sessionFile` plus the caller's `update`; it drops the scope's `sessionKey` and `agentId`. Preserve the resolved session metadata in the central publisher and cover that path.
