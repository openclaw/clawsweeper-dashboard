## Best Possible Solution

Use an explicit maintainer-approved command-scanner contract that is opt-in or safely migrated, runs with a minimal sanitized environment and effective cwd, disables scanner side effects for preflight, and is proven with real OpenClaw runtime output after rebase.

## Solution Assessment

No. A tirith-backed scanner may be useful, but this implementation should not merge until the default policy, sanitized environment, cwd-aware scanning, and side-effect-free preflight contract are settled and proven in a real OpenClaw run.
