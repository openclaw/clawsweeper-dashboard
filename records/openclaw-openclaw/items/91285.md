## Best Possible Solution

Define a durable, redacted recovery receipt for completed tool calls and failed post-tool continuation, then prove it with live provider diagnostics plus focused embedded-runner and gateway tests.

## Solution Assessment

No. Current main’s warning, replay-safe metadata, and partial empty-post-tool retry are the right safety direction, but they are not the durable recovery receipt and resume-state contract requested here.
