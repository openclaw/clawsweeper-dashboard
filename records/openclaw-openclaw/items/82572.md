Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.84

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Persist after clearing aborted summary state:** `src/auto-reply/reply/queue/drain.ts:342-356`
  - body: The new abort-drop acknowledgement persists right after splicing aborted items, but the summary cleanup runs afterward. With summarize mode, if the aborted item was the last queued item and older messages were already summarized, a crash between this persist and `clearFollowupQueueSummaryState()` leaves `items: []` plus stale `droppedCount`/`summaryLines` on disk, so restart can replay a summary current in-memory behavior would clear. Persist again after the clear, or move the acknowledgement after the summary state is cleared, and cover the summary+abort case.
  - confidence: 0.84
