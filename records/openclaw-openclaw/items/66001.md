Full review comments:

- **[P1] Preserve session-key-owned runs during reconciliation:** `src/agents/bash-tools.exec-runtime.ts:555-556`
  - body: `hasSession` only checks the bash registry by `ProcessSession.id`, but `runExecProcess` records supervisor ownership as `opts.sessionKey?.trim() || sessionId`. For any active run with a non-empty `sessionKey`, the new reconciliation call sees the supervisor record as untracked and cancels a legitimate process on the next exec/process-tool request.
  - confidence: 0.92
- **[P3] Narrow orphan cancellation reasons:** `src/process/supervisor/types.ts:12-14`
  - body: `reason?: TerminationReason` allows callers to pass natural terminal reasons like `exit`, `signal`, or `spawn-error` while `reconcileOrphans` still cancels the run. Restrict this to forced cancellation reasons so future callers cannot record a killed orphan as a natural exit.
  - confidence: 0.78
