Ensure Telegram forum-topic replies, previews, tool sends, and fallbacks preserve the inbound topic-qualified session's `message_thread_id` instead of falling back to General or another topic.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and fix Telegram-owned delivery so non-General forum-topic sends either retain `message_thread_id` or fail loudly across preview, final, media/poll/tool, and durable fallback paths.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment
