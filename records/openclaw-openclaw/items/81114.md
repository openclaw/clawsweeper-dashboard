## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to stop aborting or falling back Codex app-server turns that are still making progress or have completed output but are missing `turn/completed`.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the current-main Codex app-server liveness fixes and ship them in the next release; treat further operator probe UX as the separate feature tracked at https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/82710.
