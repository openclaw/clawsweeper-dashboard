Carry sanitized label/displayName through the shared status summary, render it in CLI/TUI status and footer/picker with the raw key still available as selector detail, then close or replace the stale linked PR.

## Solution Assessment

No, current main does not solve the remaining `/status` and TUI statusline/picker parts. The requested direction is maintainable, but the best fix needs a sanitized display-name contract and raw-key fallback/detail rather than only replacing keys with friendly text.
