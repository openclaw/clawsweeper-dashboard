## Summary

Keep open: current main and v2026.6.6 still retry unclassified Telegram spooled `ERR_MODULE_NOT_FOUND` and repeated poison failures, while the two closing PRs are open and not merged.

## What This Changes

The issue requests Telegram ingress spooled updates to dead-letter permanent module-missing and missing-harness failures, and to stop same-signature poison retries from freezing the gateway.

## Best Possible Solution

Land or replace the Telegram-owned fix that dead-letters `ERR_MODULE_NOT_FOUND` and adds a scoped poison-retry backstop, without adding new env/config surface unless maintainers explicitly choose it.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. Source inspection shows unclassified `bot.handleUpdate` failures go through `releaseTelegramSpooledUpdateClaim` and are kept for retry, while the classifier lacks `ERR_MODULE_NOT_FOUND` and poison-retry handling.

## Solution Assessment

Yes, mostly. The Telegram polling-session failure classifier is the right owner boundary, but the env-var request should remain a maintainer policy decision and the broader open PR needs review/proof before this issue can close.
