The PR's selection logic deliberately leaves custom OpenAI-compatible providers on PI, so those configs can still auto-enable or start the Codex plugin even though their runs will not use it. Reuse the official-route predicate here and cover a custom `baseUrl` case.
  - confidence: 0.82
