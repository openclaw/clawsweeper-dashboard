## What This Changes

The issue asks for yielded parent sessions to resume automatically after ACP or native child completion without a UI refresh or manual follow-up message.

## Best Possible Solution

Review, rebase, and land or replace https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/60402 for the narrow ACP heartbeat-disabled targeted wake gap, then coordinate the broader yielded-parent completion contract with https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/44925 and related open PRs.

## Solution Assessment
