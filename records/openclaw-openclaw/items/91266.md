## Solution Assessment

Is this the best way to solve the issue? Yes for the central timeout mismatch: the narrow fix should raise Codex `skill_workshop` dynamic-tool deadlines above the approval ceiling on both main-thread and side-thread paths, while broader chat approval and popup-detail work stays separate.
