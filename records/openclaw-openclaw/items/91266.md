Review and land or revise https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/91325 so Skill Workshop lifecycle approval can resolve or fail with an approval-specific result before the Codex dynamic-tool watchdog while preserving pending approval as the default.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The best direction is a narrow timeout/error-path alignment that keeps pending lifecycle approval as the default; the broad `approvalPolicy: "auto"` workaround should remain a trusted-environment workaround, not the product fix.
