## What This Changes

The issue asks for TUI chat scrolling to preserve a stable viewport instead of jumping during sends, streaming updates, and transcript redraws.

## Best Possible Solution

Land a coherent TUI transcript viewport and anchor-aware follow policy that preserves reading position across sends, streaming growth, history reloads, and long redraws, with real terminal proof and coordination with the open TUI scrollback issue.
