## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes. Reusing `resolveActiveReplyRunSessionId` after heartbeat preflight and returning the existing retryable `requests-in-flight` skip is the narrowest maintainable fix; broader replay or routing hygiene should stay separate.
