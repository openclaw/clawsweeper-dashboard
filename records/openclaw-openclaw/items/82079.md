## Best Possible Solution

Land this focused fix after live or equivalent Telegram proof, or an explicit maintainer override, confirms bot-self reply context is preserved and non-bot supplemental context remains filtered; leave the broader durable reply-metadata contract to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/88032 and https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/90745.
