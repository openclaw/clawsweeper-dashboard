## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Yes, with landing-order coordination. The per-response overlay is the right boundary for client-specific RTT and heartbeat data, and the current pending-timestamp approach avoids the earlier latest-ping-only failure while preserving the shared service health snapshot.
