Fix Feishu group reply @mention detection when Feishu omits the bot from mention metadata, while routing the bot response to the intended main-chat or topic target.

## Best Possible Solution

Land a current-main Feishu plugin fix that detects the dropped bot mention with bot-specific evidence, preserves root/thread facts through parsing, and lets scope-aware routing choose the reply target.

## Solution Assessment

No, not as originally proposed. The fix belongs in the Feishu plugin, but blanket parse-time `rootId` clearing is less maintainable than preserving context and choosing the reply target after group/topic scope is known.
