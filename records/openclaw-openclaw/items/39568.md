## What This Changes

The issue asks Feishu to detect a bot @mention in reply messages even when Feishu omits the bot from the structured mentions array, and to avoid routing the reply into the wrong thread.

## Best Possible Solution

Land or replace the Feishu-local fix against current main: add narrow bot-specific dropped-mention detection, keep root/thread facts intact through parsing, and let existing scope-aware routing choose the reply target.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level. A Feishu group reply payload with a non-empty mentions array that omits the bot leaves mentionedBot false, and mention-required group ingress can return before dispatch.

## Solution Assessment

No, not as originally proposed. The fix belongs in the Feishu plugin, but parse-time rootId clearing is less maintainable than preserving context and deciding the reply target after group/topic scope is known.
