## What This Changes

The issue asks Feishu to detect bot @mentions in replied group messages when Feishu drops the bot from structured mentions, and to avoid routing those replies into unintended topic threads.

## Best Possible Solution

Land or replace https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/39569 with a Feishu-local fix that uses bot-specific dropped-mention evidence and chooses reply targets after group session scope is known.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level. A Feishu group reply whose non-empty mentions array omits botOpenId leaves mentionedBot=false, and mention-required group ingress can then return before dispatch.

## Solution Assessment

No, not exactly as proposed. The fix belongs in the Feishu plugin, but broad parse-time rootId clearing and any-orphan placeholder detection are riskier than bot-specific mention evidence plus scope-aware routing.
