## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.9

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Handle leading em dashes without a leading comma:** `src/infra/outbound/payloads.ts:57-59`
  - body: Replacing `\s*—\s*` with `, ` makes messages that begin with an em dash render as malformed text, for example `— hello` becomes `, hello`. Add a leading-edge case or cleanup before this transform ships.
  - confidence: 0.92
- **[P2] Preserve trailing commas when no em dash was rewritten:** `src/infra/outbound/payloads.ts:59`
  - body: The final `.replace(/,\s*$/g, "")` runs for every comma-mode string, so `Hello,` becomes `Hello` even though no em dash was present. Only trim punctuation introduced by a trailing em dash or return early when there is nothing to rewrite.
  - confidence: 0.93
