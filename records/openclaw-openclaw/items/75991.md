## Summary

Keep open. Current main still has a source-backed silent delivery gap: routed final replies can be treated as successfully queued even when outbound delivery returns no delivered result, which also prevents transcript mirroring and can bypass Telegram fallback handling.

## What This Changes

Ensure CLI-backed session responses with a Telegram delivery context are actually delivered and mirrored, or retried/reported instead of silently remaining transcript-only.

## Best Possible Solution

Make non-silent final delivery prove at least one outbound result or enqueue a durable retry before reporting success, and ensure session-backed CLI/Telegram replies carry stable mirror metadata without making the fix Telegram-only.

## Reproduction Assessment

Unclear for the full intermittent lock cascade. A high-confidence source-level reproduction exists: mock deliverOutboundPayloads to return [], then routeReply reports success with no message id, no delivery mirror can be appended, and Telegram can treat the turn as queued.

## Solution Assessment

Yes, keeping this open for a shared route-reply/outbound contract fix is the narrowest maintainable path. A launchd watchdog may reduce lock contention but does not address the silent success contract or missing mirror metadata.
