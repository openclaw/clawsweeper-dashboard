The issue reports cron lane timeouts caused by event-loop saturation during workspace bootstrap/context preparation on a large binary-heavy agent workspace.

Keep this open and isolate the blocking prep component, then bound workspace scanning/reading with binary and size guards plus event-loop yielding so cron timeouts remain a safety net rather than the recovery mechanism.
