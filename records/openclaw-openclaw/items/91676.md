## Best Possible Solution

Choose one maintainer-approved default-deny sandbox opt-in or diagnostic path, then implement it with schema/docs/tests and real sandbox proof.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear. A default-off flag is plausible, but existing sandbox tool policy may be the better owning surface or maintainers may prefer diagnostics only, so the security/config boundary should be chosen first.
