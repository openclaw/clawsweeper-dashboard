## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the focused prompt-size/defaults bug until a maintainer-approved mitigation lands, preferably by reviewing the open tiered-bootstrap or minimal-tier/doctor-warning work with explicit local-model proof and upgrade-safe config behavior.
