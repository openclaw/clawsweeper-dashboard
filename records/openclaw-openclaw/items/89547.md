## What This Changes

Adds Telegram group-history modes (`mention-only`, `recent`, and `none`), defaults unset group history to mention-only, and applies the selected mode to cached prompt context, pending history, and recovered forum-topic dispatch.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the single resolved-mode implementation across all Telegram history paths, but merge only after maintainers explicitly approve the privacy-first behavior for existing unset configurations or revise the upgrade path, and after redacted exact-head live Telegram proof covers all three modes and topic recovery.
