Review and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/81831, or an equivalent focused Google provider update, preserving preview IDs only as compatibility aliases to the GA ID.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The best fix is the narrow Google-owned model lifecycle update already represented by the open PR: make the GA ID canonical, keep preview as a compatibility alias, and update docs/tests together.
