## Best Possible Solution

Land and ship https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/81831 after normal maintainer review, preserving preview IDs as compatibility aliases to the GA `gemini-3.1-flash-lite` ID.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The best fix is the narrow Google provider/model lifecycle update in the open PR: canonicalize the GA ID, keep the preview ID as a compatibility alias, and update docs/tests together.
