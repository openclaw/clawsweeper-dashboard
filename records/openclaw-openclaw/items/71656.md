## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial corpus hygiene, but the central fixed-score, content-dedupe, and fair session scan problems are still present, and related open issues/PRs overlap rather than supersede this canonical tracker.

## What This Changes

The issue requests memory-core dreaming changes to pre-filter and deduplicate noisy session-corpus entries, score content by quality and recency, and rotate session scanning fairly as the corpus grows.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this as the canonical memory-core quality tracker, then land a coordinated internal repair that preserves useful transcript ingestion while adding content-level dedupe, bounded content/recency scoring, and fair persisted scan progress.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainers settle the session-corpus promotion boundary. A narrow internal repair is the right direction for scoring, dedupe, and scan fairness, but it should coordinate with the open denoising/promotion PRs and related material-selection issues.
