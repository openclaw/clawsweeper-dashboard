Full review comments:

- **[P1] Preserve lifecycle outcomes during catch recovery:** `src/agents/subagent-registry.ts:1106-1115`
  - body: The new catch wraps the whole lifecycle listener, but its recovery callback always retries `completeSubagentRun` with an `ok` outcome. If the rejected await came from the blocked/error path after it had persisted an error outcome, `completeSubagentRun` can update the stored outcome and ended reason on retry, so an errored subagent may be announced and persisted as success. Capture the original completion params per branch or do not retry non-ok listener failures.
