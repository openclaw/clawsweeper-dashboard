## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

No, not against current main. The report gives detailed audit evidence and current source shows adjacent writer/reload paths, but the exact u4s wrapper startup sequence and first baseline-producing writer were not reproduced after the related restart fix.

## Solution Assessment

No complete solution is proven. The consume-once startup snapshot fix is the right narrow fix for the related in-process restart bug, and serialized/destructive-write hardening helps generally, but neither proves the wrapper plugin-enable sequence preserves the reported access-control fields.
