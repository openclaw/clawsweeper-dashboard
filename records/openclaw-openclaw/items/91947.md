## Summary

## What This Changes

The issue reports that QMD-backed memory_search still times out at a fixed 15s despite memory.qmd.limits.timeoutMs being raised for slow qmd query runs.

## Best Possible Solution

Land one focused memory-core fix that lets QMD-backed memory_search share a manager-owned whole-search deadline derived from the configured QMD timeout, while keeping default/non-QMD behavior bounded and leaving zero-hit policy and subprocess cancellation to their own tracked work.

## Solution Assessment
