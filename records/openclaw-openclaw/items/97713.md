- **[P1] Preserve Undici no_proxy precedence in the wrapper:** `src/infra/net/undici-global-dispatcher.ts:174`
  - body: Undici checks `process.env.no_proxy ?? process.env.NO_PROXY`, so an empty lowercase `no_proxy` intentionally shadows uppercase `NO_PROXY` and keeps requests proxied.
