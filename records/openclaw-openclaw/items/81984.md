The issue asks for `/stop` followed by `/new` to durably archive the prior transcript or loudly surface archive failures instead of silently losing session content.

No live end-to-end reproduction was established in this read-only review. Source inspection is high-confidence: archive rename errors are swallowed in `archiveSessionTranscriptsDetailed`, and `/new` calls that helper during previous-session rollover.

## Solution Assessment
