## What This Changes

The issue asks OpenClaw to deliver concise direct/background subagent completion messages when the requester session is inactive or locked, without mirroring long child output wholesale into the requester transcript.

## Best Possible Solution

Review and land a bounded, proven direct-text fallback via https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/92643, then keep or route the broader orphan/session-lock cleanup to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/86538 or https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/92395 as appropriate.
