The issue asks memory-core to stop turning an empty REM “No strong candidate truths surfaced” placeholder into a promotable long-term `MEMORY.md` entry.

## Best Possible Solution

Update, review, and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/80916, or land an equivalent memory-core fix that removes the empty REM candidate section and filters historical placeholder snippets.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The narrow maintainable fix is to stop writing candidate-like empty REM markdown at the source and defensively reject already-written placeholder snippets before promotion.
