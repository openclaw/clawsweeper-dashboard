## Summary

## What This Changes

The issue requests sanitizing or eliding malformed model-output artifacts before the bundled session-memory hook persists and re-injects prior-session context.

## Best Possible Solution

Land or replace https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/69961 with a focused sanitizer that reuses or matches the shared assistant-visible/history sanitization contract at the transcript and write boundaries, with regression coverage and redacted real hook proof.
