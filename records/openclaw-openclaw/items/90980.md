Land one canonical fix, likely by refreshing `https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/91015` or replacing it after deciding its relationship to `https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/66836`, with regression coverage, config/docs alignment if a setting is added, and redacted real wedged-Docker proof.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes. Source inspection gives a high-confidence reproduction path: a Docker child that never exits keeps `execDockerRaw` pending, and sandbox init reaches that helper without a deadline.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear as a complete fix. A Docker control-plane deadline is the right first layer, but maintainers still need to choose the timeout scope, fail-soft semantics, and whether any user-facing config surface is warranted.
