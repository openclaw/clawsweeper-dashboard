## Best Possible Solution

Land a narrower runtime-safety change that validates only effective post-policy/post-hook/post-approval params, reuses the shared stableStringify contract, catches steering failures, and either scopes the breaker here with maintainer approval or splits repeated-error policy to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/81844.
