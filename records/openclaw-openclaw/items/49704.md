- **[P1] Keep threaded text deliveries on the finalize path:** `src/cron/isolated-agent/delivery-dispatch.ts:561-562`

In isolated cron sessions without a loaded Telegram plugin, that documented target can still fall through without an explicit thread id.
  - confidence: 0.93
