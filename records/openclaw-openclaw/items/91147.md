Full review comments:

- **[P1] Flush hook output before forcing exit:** `src/cli/hooks-cli.ts:563`
  - body: `runNativeHookRelayCli` writes deny/unavailable responses with plain `stream.write()` calls, then this new line immediately exits the process. Await/end stdout and stderr, or otherwise prove they drained, before forcing the normal relay exit.
