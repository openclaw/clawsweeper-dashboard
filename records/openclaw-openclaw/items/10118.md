## Best Possible Solution

Document the current Shift+Enter and fallback newline behavior in the public TUI guide and /help, then decide whether OpenClaw should expose pi-tui keybinding overrides as the canonical configurable TUI keybinding surface; keep WebChat composer policy separate.
