## Summary

Keep this open: current main and v2026.6.8 already provide default Enter-submit and Shift+Enter-newline behavior through pi-tui, but the requested TUI docs, /help coverage, and configurable send/newline keybinding policy remain unresolved.

## What This Changes

## Best Possible Solution

Document the current Shift+Enter and fallback newline behavior in the public TUI guide and TUI /help, then decide whether pi-tui keybinding overrides are the canonical configurable TUI surface instead of adding a one-off toggle.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainers decide the public keybinding policy. Reusing pi-tui's existing keybinding contract plus documenting terminal fallbacks is the narrowest maintainable direction; adding a standalone enterSends toggle needs explicit product approval.
