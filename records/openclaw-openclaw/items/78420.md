The PR adds Telegram turn-local tracking of block-delivered media, filters final-reply `mediaUrls` against that set, and adds a pure helper with unit tests.

## Best Possible Solution

Finish this PR by clearing or recomputing legacy `mediaUrl` whenever deduping `mediaUrls`, covering that captioned full-overlap case, and adding the user-facing changelog entry before merge.
