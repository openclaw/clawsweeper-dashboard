## Summary

Keep open: current main and v2026.5.22 still admit Telegram Business update names through grammY defaults but do not process Business messages or support Business reply routing. ## What This Changes

Request Telegram Business support so OpenClaw can receive business_connection/business_message updates, route Business messages through Telegram inbound handling, and reply with business_connection_id.

## Best Possible Solution

Ship a reviewed Telegram plugin implementation that durably tracks account-scoped Business connection state, routes Business messages through the existing DM pipeline, adds Business-aware dedupe/sequencing, safely injects business_connection_id for supported outbound replies, documents setup/access semantics, and proves the flow with focused tests plus redacted live Telegram Business evidence.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level. Current main admits Telegram Business update names through grammY defaults but registers no Business handlers or outbound business_connection_id mapping, so Business chat updates cannot enter the Telegram pipeline or receive supported replies.

## Solution Assessment

Yes on the product boundary, but not as a minimal allowed-updates-only patch. The maintainable fix belongs in the Telegram plugin and needs connection state, Business-aware dedupe/sequencing, outbound reply mapping, access-control documentation, tests, and live proof.
