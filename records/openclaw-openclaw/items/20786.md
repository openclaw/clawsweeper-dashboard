## Best Possible Solution

Ship a reviewed Telegram plugin implementation that durably tracks account-scoped Business connection state, routes Business messages through the existing DM pipeline, adds Business-aware dedupe/sequencing, safely injects business_connection_id for supported outbound replies, documents setup/access semantics, and proves the flow with focused tests plus redacted live Telegram Business evidence.

Yes, at source level. Current main and v2026.6.5 admit Telegram Business update names through grammY defaults but register no Business handlers or outbound business_connection_id mapping, so Business chat updates cannot enter the Telegram pipeline or receive supported replies.

## Solution Assessment

Yes on the owner boundary, but not as an allowed-updates-only patch. The Telegram plugin is the right owner, and the maintainable fix needs connection state, Business-aware dedupe/sequencing, outbound reply mapping, access-control documentation, tests, and live proof.
