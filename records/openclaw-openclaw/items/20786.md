## Summary

Keep open: current main still lacks Telegram Business runtime handlers, Business-aware dedupe/sequencing, and outbound business_connection_id routing, so the requested Telegram Business workflow remains unsupported.

## What This Changes

Add Telegram Business bot support so OpenClaw can receive business_connection and business_message updates and respond in Telegram Business-connected personal chats.

## Best Possible Solution

Ship a reviewed Telegram plugin implementation that durably tracks account-scoped Business connection state, routes Business messages through the existing DM pipeline, adds Business-aware dedupe/sequencing, safely injects business_connection_id for supported outbound replies, documents setup/access semantics, and proves the flow with focused tests plus redacted live Telegram Business evidence.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level. Current main admits Telegram Business update names through grammY defaults but registers no Business handlers or outbound business_connection_id mapping, so Business chat updates cannot enter the Telegram pipeline or receive supported replies.

## Solution Assessment

Yes on the product boundary, but not as an allowed-updates-only patch. The Telegram plugin is the right owner, and the maintainable fix must include connection state, Business-aware dedupe/sequencing, outbound reply mapping, access-control documentation, tests, and live proof.
