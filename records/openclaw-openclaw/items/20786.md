Keep open: current main and the latest release still only admit Telegram Business update names through grammY defaults; they do not register Business handlers, model Business dedupe/sequencing, or support outbound replies with business_connection_id. ## What This Changes

The issue requests Telegram Business support by receiving business_connection/business_message updates, routing Business messages through the Telegram inbound pipeline, and replying with the required business_connection_id.

## Best Possible Solution

Ship a reviewed Telegram plugin implementation that durably tracks account-scoped Business connection state, routes Business messages through the existing DM pipeline, adds Business-aware dedupe/sequencing, safely injects business_connection_id for supported outbound replies, documents setup/access semantics, and proves the flow with focused tests plus redacted live Telegram Business evidence.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level. Current main admits Telegram Business update names through grammY defaults but registers no Business handlers or outbound business_connection_id mapping, so Business chat updates cannot enter the Telegram pipeline or receive supported replies.

## Solution Assessment

Yes on the product boundary, but not as a minimal allowed-updates-only patch. The Telegram plugin is the right owner, and the maintainable fix needs connection state, Business-aware dedupe/sequencing, outbound reply mapping, access-control documentation, tests, and live proof.
