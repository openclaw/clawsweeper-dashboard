## Summary

Keep open: current main and v2026.6.5 still admit Telegram Business update names through grammY defaults but do not process Business updates or provide durable Business reply routing. The linked target PR was closed unmerged and the open fork mirror is not a safe canonical landed fix.

## What This Changes

Add Telegram Business support so OpenClaw Telegram bots can receive `business_connection` and `business_message` updates, route Business messages through the inbound pipeline, and reply with the correct `business_connection_id`.

## Best Possible Solution

Ship a reviewed Telegram plugin implementation with account-scoped durable Business connection state, Business-aware inbound routing, dedupe/sequencing, safe outbound `business_connection_id` injection, docs, focused tests, and redacted live Telegram proof.

## Reproduction Assessment

Yes, at source level. Current main and v2026.6.5 admit Telegram Business update names through grammY defaults but register no Business handlers or durable outbound `business_connection_id` mapping.

## Solution Assessment

Yes on the owner boundary, but not as an allowed-updates-only patch. The Telegram plugin is the right owner, and the maintainable fix needs connection state, Business-aware dedupe/sequencing, outbound reply mapping, access-control documentation, tests, and live proof.
