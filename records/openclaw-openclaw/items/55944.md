Rebase onto current main, keep platform classification in gateway policy, move any canvas default through the canvas plugin/type surface, add focused policy/audit tests, and attach redacted real-device proof for the Vision Pro gateway flow.

Not yet. The gateway policy seam is the right place for platform classification, but the branch needs a current-main rebase that preserves plugin-owned canvas policy and maintainer confirmation of the new spatial defaults.
