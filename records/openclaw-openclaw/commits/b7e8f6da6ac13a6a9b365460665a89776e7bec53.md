- Kind: bug

- Evidence: `apps/ios/version.json:2` pins iOS to `2026.5.16`, but `apps/ios/CHANGELOG.md:3` still starts at `2026.5.12` and has no `Unreleased` fallback. `scripts/lib/ios-version.ts:150` only accepts the pinned version heading or `Unreleased`, and `node --import tsx scripts/ios-sync-versioning.ts --check` fails with `Unable to find iOS changelog notes for 2026.5.16`.
