# Commit e6e1696c28

## Summary

Found one retry-accounting regression in the new capped QQBot typing keepalive budget. The cap is meant to preserve reply capacity, but failed keepalive attempts consume the budget before a successful `input_notify`, so token-refresh or transient failures can stop typing renewals early while the response is still running.

## Findings

### Medium: Failed typing attempts consume the capped renewal budget

- Kind: regression
- File: `extensions/qqbot/src/engine/gateway/typing-keepalive.ts`
- Line: 91
- Evidence: `send()` retries failed sends through `sendAttempt()` at lines 68-77, but `sendAttempt()` decrements `renewalsRemaining` before awaiting `sendInputNotify()` at lines 91-93. If the last remaining slot is spent by a failed attempt, the retry enters `sendAttempt()`, sees `renewalsRemaining <= 0`, stops, and sends nothing.
- Impact: a normal recoverable failure, such as an expired token followed by token-cache clearing, can reduce the intended three successful renewals to two or skip the final retry entirely. That regresses the QQBot C2C typing behavior this commit is trying to fix: long replies can lose the typing indicator earlier than intended after a transient failure.
- Suggested fix: decrement `renewalsRemaining` only after `sendInputNotify()` succeeds, then stop when the successful-send budget reaches zero. Add a fake-timer retry test where a failed first attempt plus successful token-refresh retry consumes one renewal and does not stop early.
- Confidence: high

## Details

- Do we have a high-confidence way to reproduce the issue? yes. A focused source probe against current `main` called the private `send()` path directly: after two successful sends, a failed third attempt caused `attempts: 3` and `successfulSends: 2`, with no retry success because the budget had already been exhausted.
- Is this the best way to solve the issue? yes. The budget is explicitly for successful keepalive renewals; moving the decrement after the awaited network send preserves the cap while keeping token-refresh retry semantics from the base commit.
