## Summary

Current `main` already parses `[[audio_as_voice]]`, preserves the flag through outbound normalization and delivery, and Telegram now routes compatible audio through `sendVoice` with regression coverage for both generic outbound delivery and Telegram transport behavior.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/infra/outbound/payloads.ts:136](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7ac35b4f69009490180a35bf404638d4280a4865/src/infra/outbound/payloads.ts#L136)

  - file: [src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts:190](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7ac35b4f69009490180a35bf404638d4280a4865/src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts#L190)
