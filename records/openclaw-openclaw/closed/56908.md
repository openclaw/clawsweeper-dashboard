## Summary

Current `main` already implements ordered cross-provider failover chains under the existing model-failover system: providers rotate auth profiles first, then fall through `agents.defaults.model.fallbacks` across provider/model refs, with documented config/CLI surfaces and shipped test coverage in `v2026.4.23`.

## Evidence
