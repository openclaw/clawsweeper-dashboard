## Summary

Current `main` already includes targeted self-recovery for cooldowned providers and stale auth-profile failure state, which covers the reported "gateway stuck until restart" behavior after an upstream outage.

## Evidence

- **Cooldowned primary providers are actively re-probed instead of being skipped forever:** `runWithModelFallback` now decides whether to probe a provider whose profiles are all in cooldown, and when allowed it runs the model with `allowTransientCooldownProbe: true` rather than requiring a process restart.
