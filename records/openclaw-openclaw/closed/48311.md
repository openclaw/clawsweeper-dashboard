## Summary

Current `main` already addresses the reported macOS LaunchAgent lifecycle asymmetry. `gateway stop` now disables and stops the LaunchAgent without unloading it in the normal path, and `gateway start`/`gateway restart` on Darwin attempt LaunchAgent bootstrap repair when the plist exists but launchd reports the service as not loaded.

## Evidence
