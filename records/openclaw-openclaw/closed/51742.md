## Summary

Current `main` already avoids adding native OpenAI strict-tool shaping on LM Studio/OpenAI-compatible local routes, which is the behavior implicated by this report. The fix is present in code, covered by regression tests, and shipped in `v2026.4.22`.

## Evidence
