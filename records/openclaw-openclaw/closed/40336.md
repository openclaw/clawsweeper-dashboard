## Summary

Current `main` already has both pre-prompt auto-compaction for oversized sessions and timeout-recovery compaction for high-context timeouts, and the changelog says the timeout-recovery fix shipped in `v2026.3.28`. The Z.AI/OpenAI-compatible transport also maps `finish_reason: network_error` into the timeout classification path.
