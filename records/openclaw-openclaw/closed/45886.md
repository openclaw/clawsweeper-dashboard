## Summary

Current `main` already handles stale cron `runningAtMs` state: job completion clears the marker, startup clears persisted stale markers and replays interrupted recurring jobs, and timer re-arming now avoids the former zero-delay hot loop when a past-due job is blocked by a stale in-memory lock. The same cron files are present in release `v2026.4.22`, so this appears shipped already.

## Evidence
