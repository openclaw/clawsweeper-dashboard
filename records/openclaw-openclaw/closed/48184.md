## Summary

Current `main` already implements the requested behavior with a broader session-scoped `TelegramTransport` design that preserves sticky IPv4 fallback across polling restarts, passes account network config into transport resolution, and adds restart/rebuild coverage. The changelog also records this exact fix as landed on main under `#48282`.

## Evidence
