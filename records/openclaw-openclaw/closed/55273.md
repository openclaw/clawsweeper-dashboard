## Summary

Current `main` already resolves per-agent heartbeat config ahead of global defaults, tests cover distinct per-agent intervals, explicit channel heartbeats do not fall back across channels, and the irregular cadence in the report is better explained by documented wake-driven heartbeats than by an interval-resolution bug.
