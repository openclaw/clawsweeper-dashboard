For https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/81945, fix the managed-service update handoff pending-to-final status cache regression. In `src/gateway/server-restart-sentinel.ts`, when the scheduled retry reads a final non-pending update sentinel after an earlier pending `managed-service-handoff-started` or `restart-health-pending` sentinel, record or refresh `latestUpdateRestartSentinel` with the final payload before removing the sentinel file and dispatching the continuation. Add a focused regression test in `src/gateway/server-restart-sentinel.test.ts` that simulates first reading a pending control-plane update sentinel, then a later retry reading a final `ok` sentinel, and asserts the cached status exposed through the update-status path is final rather than pending. Preserve the existing CLI handoff design, do not change product semantics, and do not add unrelated changelog/docs churn.
