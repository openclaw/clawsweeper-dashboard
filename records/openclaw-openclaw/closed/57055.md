## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not propagate the global `browser.ssrfPolicy` into the sandbox browser bridge. The sandbox bridge builds its own `ResolvedBrowserConfig` without an `ssrfPolicy`, and the sandbox config types have no field to carry that policy. That means sandbox browser navigation cannot honor `browser.ssrfPolicy.dangerouslyAllowPrivateNetwork: true` for private-network targets, even though the user-facing browser config/docs say that opt-in should allow them.
