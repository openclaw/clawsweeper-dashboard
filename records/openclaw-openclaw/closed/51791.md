## Summary

Current `main` already handles OpenAI-family/vLLM `prompt_tokens` and `completion_tokens` aliases in the WebSocket response path, preserves totals from normalized usage, and keeps session `totalTokens` prompt-only. That makes this PR's partial fix obsolete on `main` and avoids its semantic regression of storing request+response totals as context size.
