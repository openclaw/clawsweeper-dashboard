## Summary

Current `main` already treats undici `TypeError: fetch failed` as a transient unhandled rejection, so the gateway logs and continues instead of exiting. The behavior is covered by regression tests, documented for Telegram operators, and listed in the changelog as shipped in `2026.2.22`.

## Evidence
