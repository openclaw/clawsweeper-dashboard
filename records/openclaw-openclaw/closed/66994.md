## Summary

Current `main` already addresses this area well enough: exec approval gating is modeled as the merge of requested `tools.exec.*` policy plus the host approvals file, `approvals.exec.enabled` is documented and wired as forwarding-only, and cron/headless exec now handles no-route approval cases inline or with an explicit fix hint instead of silently depending on interactive approval cards.
