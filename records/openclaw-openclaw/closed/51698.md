## Summary

Current `main` already implements the reported cleanup: a successful WebSocket connect followed by read-scope failure (`missing scope: operator.read`) is classified as degraded-but-reachable, not as full gateway unreachability.

## Evidence

- **Probe separates WS connect success from follow-up read RPC failure:** After `hello-ok`, the probe records `connectLatencyMs` and then runs detail RPCs separately; a later RPC failure does not erase the fact that the WebSocket connected.
