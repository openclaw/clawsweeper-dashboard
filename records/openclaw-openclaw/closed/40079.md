## Summary

Current `main` has a durable outbound delivery queue, startup recovery, and restart deferral work that address part of the report, but I could not find high-confidence coverage for the issue's specific Telegram/iMessage channel-restart path. The only production reconnect-drain wiring I found is for WhatsApp, so this is not strong enough to auto-close conservatively.
