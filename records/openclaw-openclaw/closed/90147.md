## Best Possible Solution

Land node-pairing throttling only after the auth-rate-limit contract is explicitly approved and documented or adjusted, paired-reconnect compatibility remains covered, and redacted non-loopback gateway proof is added.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Unclear as merge-ready. Throttling before the node-pairing storage lock is the right layer for the pressure point, but reusing the documented failed-auth limiter for successful pairing requests needs owner approval, documentation, or a safer contract before it is the best fix.
