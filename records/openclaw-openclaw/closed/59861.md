## Summary

Current `main` already implements the requested behavior: rate-limit/quota failures escalate into model fallback, and `LiveSessionModelSwitchError` is normalized into the fallback path instead of killing the session. The changelog shows the relevant live-switch fix shipped in `v2026.4.5`.

## Evidence

- **Rate-limit exhaustion escalates to model fallback:** The embedded runner now logs rate-limit rotation exhaustion and throws a `FailoverError` with `reason: "rate_limit"` specifically to escalate into model fallback.
  - file: [src/agents/pi-embedded-runner/run.ts:632](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/a155b84cdadef068de50274318952032cd867788/src/agents/pi-embedded-runner/run.ts#L632)

  - sha: [a155b84cdade](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/a155b84cdadef068de50274318952032cd867788)
- **Failover policy routes rate limits into fallback:** The failover policy returns `action: "fallback_model"` for retry-limit failures when fallbacks are configured, and assistant-side rate-limit failures fall back after profile rotation is exhausted.
  - file: [src/agents/pi-embedded-runner/run/failover-policy.ts:111](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/a155b84cdadef068de50274318952032cd867788/src/agents/pi-embedded-runner/run/failover-policy.ts#L111)

  - file: [src/agents/model-fallback.ts:921](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/a155b84cdadef068de50274318952032cd867788/src/agents/model-fallback.ts#L921)
