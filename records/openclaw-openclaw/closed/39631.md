## Summary

Keep this issue open. Current main has a partial safeguard: acknowledged failed mutating-tool replies suppress the standalone warning, but unacknowledged or success-looking failed `edit`/write payloads still produce a user-facing `⚠️ ... failed` error payload, and Telegram still sends final `isError` payloads through normal delivery. The merged #72410 covers the narrow acknowledged-failure case; open #73750 remains the active maintainer follow-up, while #39406 tracks broader configurable delivery policy.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this open until maintainers review and land #73750 or an equivalent focused fix. The fix should prevent raw failed-edit/tool warning text from leaking into Telegram when a clean assistant-level failure response exists, keep full details in logs/debug surfaces, preserve a visible non-raw fallback when the assistant claims success or leaves the failed write unresolved, and leave #39406 open for broader configurable delivery-policy work.
