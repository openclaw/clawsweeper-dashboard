## Summary

Do not close. Current `main` has already fixed or bypassed part of the `NO_REPLY`+media complaint in shared outbound normalization, but the Microsoft/WhatsApp voice-note portion still looks unfixed on `main`, and the per-channel routing concern is not disproven by the current routing code. That is not strong enough evidence for any close reason.
