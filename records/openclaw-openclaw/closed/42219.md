## Summary

Current `main` still matches the reported Discord startup/mention-gate bug. The bot identity fetch path logs and returns `undefined` on `fetchUser("@me")` failure, startup continues with that undefined `botUserId`, and guild mention enforcement still short-circuits on `botId` before honoring `mentionDecision.shouldSkip`. I did not find evidence of a shipped fix on `main` or in `CHANGELOG.md`.
