## Summary

Current `main` already carries the iMessage attachment-root plumbing that addresses this regression. The iMessage plugin defaults inbound attachment roots to `/Users/*/Library/Messages/Attachments`, exports those roots through the channel messaging contract, and media-understanding merges channel inbound roots into the local media allowlist used for attachment reads.
