## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a strong basis to close this issue: several reported problems are still present in code, even though one subclaim about swallowed initial-message errors is partly outdated on current `main`. The remaining concerns around stuck mock calls, disabled stale-call reaping by default, persistent `processedEventIds` growth, and silent Twilio stream playback on empty synthesized audio are not clearly fixed.
