Full review comments:

- **[P2] Prefer Provider over Surface when resolving steer channel:** `src/auto-reply/reply/agent-runner.ts:900-904`
  - body: When `OriginatingChannel` is absent, this fallback checks `Surface` before `Provider` or `followupRun.run.messageProvider`.   - confidence: 0.92

  - confidence: 0.9
