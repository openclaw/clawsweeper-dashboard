## Summary

Current `main` now covers the reported macOS auto-update outage path: post-core plugin/config work resumes in the updated install, and the macOS restart/install paths explicitly re-bootstrap unloaded LaunchAgents instead of assuming `KeepAlive` will recover after a plist-triggered unload.
