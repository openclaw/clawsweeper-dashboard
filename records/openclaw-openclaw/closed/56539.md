## Summary

Current `main` does not match this PR's premise. The xAI catalog defines `grok-4-fast` and `grok-4-1-fast` as canonical ids, while `grok-4-fast-reasoning` and `grok-4-1-fast-reasoning` are documented and tested as legacy aliases that normalize to those canonical ids.
