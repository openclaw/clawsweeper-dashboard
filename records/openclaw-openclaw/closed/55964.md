## Summary

Current `main` now persists restart continuations through a session-delivery queue, replays queued continuation work after startup, and resumes restart-aborted main sessions waiting on tool/model work. That matches the reported Telegram symptom around restart/recovery windows closely enough to close as implemented on `main`.

## Evidence

- **Durable restart-continuation handoff on current main:** Current restart-sentinel handling now enqueues continuation work before deleting the sentinel, preserves delivery context, and drains the queued continuation after the restart notice path.
