## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` appears to fix the duplicate final-text send after a Feishu streaming card closes, but the issue also reports standalone tool/progress flooding, and the Feishu path still does not enable the generic tool-progress suppression/coalescing path that other channels use.

## Evidence

- **Feishu duplicate-final guard exists on main:** The Feishu reply dispatcher tracks delivered final texts and skips sending duplicate final text payloads; when streaming is active it closes the streaming card and records the final text instead of sending a second plain-text reply.

  - file: [extensions/feishu/src/reply-dispatcher.test.ts:352](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/fa976e5b93b3819f9e137fe08635764edee38290/extensions/feishu/src/reply-dispatcher.test.ts#L352)
