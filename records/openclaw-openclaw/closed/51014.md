## Summary

Current `main` already has the recovery and user-visible fallback paths this Telegram wedge report asked for: compaction-timeout grace and snapshot recovery in the embedded runner, visible external-channel failure replies plus session-reset guidance, Telegram fallback delivery when dispatch fails, and documented/manual session reset flows with Telegram direct-session recreation on next inbound message.
