## Summary

Current `main` already carries `--verbose` in the bundled `claude-cli` fresh and resume argv, and the live-session argv builder preserves that flag. The PR's reported default-path regression is therefore already implemented on `main` through backend defaults rather than by appending `--verbose` inside `buildClaudeLiveArgs()`.
