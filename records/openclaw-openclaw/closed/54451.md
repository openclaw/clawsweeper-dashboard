## Summary

Current `main` already handles the reported symptom: empty assistant turns no longer leave the session looking hung. The embedded runner now retries empty/zero-output turns and otherwise surfaces an explicit user-visible error, and the MiniMax Anthropic-compatible path disables implicit thinking to avoid `reasoning_content`-only MiniMax replies.

## Evidence
