## Summary

Current `main` already covers the reported model-config drift: model-related config edits are hot-reloaded, the model catalog refresh regenerates `models.json` from current config, the picker is scoped to the effective allowlist, and destructive allowlist replacements are now blocked/merged instead of silently clobbering entries.
