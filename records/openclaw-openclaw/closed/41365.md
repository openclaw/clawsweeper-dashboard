## Summary

Current `main` already resolves the reported cron announce truncation with a different design: text-only announce delivery can collapse to `finalAssistantVisibleText` for channels that opt in (Telegram does), and non-opt-in direct channels preserve all successful payloads instead of dropping to the last chunk. The PR's `mergeNonErrorTextPayloads` approach is therefore superseded on `main`.
