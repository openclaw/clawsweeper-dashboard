## Summary

Current `main` already preserves Telegram topic thread IDs through delivery recovery, maps them to Telegram `message_thread_id`, and strips legacy `telegram:` / `telegram:group:` prefixes that could otherwise confuse recovery targets.

## Evidence

- **Recovery keeps queued threadId:** `buildRecoveryDeliverParams` forwards the persisted queue entry `threadId` back into recovery delivery params, so SIGTERM replay does not drop topic metadata before re-send.
  - file: [src/infra/outbound/delivery-queue-recovery.ts:112](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/6a61cb73c5622749d7940fd8d8b90c93adbb5081/src/infra/outbound/delivery-queue-recovery.ts#L112)

  - file: [src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts:754](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/6a61cb73c5622749d7940fd8d8b90c93adbb5081/src/infra/outbound/deliver.ts#L754)
