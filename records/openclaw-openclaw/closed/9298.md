## Summary

Jami support is a reasonable idea, but current OpenClaw scope routes new messaging-platform integrations to plugins and ClawHub rather than adding them to core/bundled surfaces by default.

## Evidence

- **Vision scope:** `VISION.md:54`-`61` says optional capability should usually ship as plugins, that plugins should be hosted and maintained in their own repository, and that the bar for adding optional plugins to core is intentionally high.
