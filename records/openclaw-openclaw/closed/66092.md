## Summary

Current `main` already preserves TTS voice-note media through outbound normalization, flushes pending tool audio at agent end, and routes Telegram `audioAsVoice` replies through `sendVoice`; the fix appears to have landed in commit `db8f7478b43550d2949c04b2a9c9ca7fd474dad3` after the reported `2026.4.12` build.

## Evidence
