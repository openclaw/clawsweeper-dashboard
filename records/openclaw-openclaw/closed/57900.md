## Summary

Current `main` already routes `sessions_spawn` subagent runs through the shared model-fallback path, explicitly treats `LiveSessionModelSwitchError` as a fallback condition instead of a fatal error, and includes committed tests covering embedded/subagent rate-limit escalation and fallback continuation.
