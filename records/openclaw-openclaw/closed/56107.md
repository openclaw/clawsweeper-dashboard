## Summary

Current `main` already covers the reported fallback-gate failure through a different implementation: assistant-side `surface_error` provider failures are converted into `FailoverError` even when `fallbackConfigured` is false, and the outer fallback loop continues while candidates remain.

## Evidence

- **Assistant surface_error now throws even without configured inner fallback:** `handleAssistantFailover` converts non-timeout, non-abort `surface_error` outcomes into `FailoverError` instead of falling through to a normal return, which removes the need for an extra `externalFallbackActive` flag in this path.
