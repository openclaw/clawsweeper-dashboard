## Summary

Current `main` already suppresses WhatsApp self-number quoted replies from counting as implicit mentions in `selfChatMode`, preserving `requireMention: true`; this is covered by regression tests and noted in the 2026.4.1 changelog.

## Evidence

- **Current gating logic suppresses self-reply implicit mentions in self-chat mode:** `applyGroupGating()` computes `implicitReplyToSelf` when `selfChatMode` is enabled and the sender overlaps the linked self identity, then blocks `quoted_bot` implicit mention matching in that case.

  - command: `nl -ba extensions/whatsapp/src/auto-reply/monitor/group-gating.ts | sed -n '150,190p'`
  - sha: [5dfc1b90e176](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/5dfc1b90e176a9e4d13b9e91ad6affec6b92f213)
- **Regression test covers the reported scenario:** The WhatsApp monitor tests explicitly assert that self-number quoted replies in group chats do not process when `selfChatMode: true` and `requireMention: true`. A neighboring test confirms reply-to-bot still works for other users.
