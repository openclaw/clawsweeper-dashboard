## What This Changes

The PR replaces the embedded-runner prompt-content boolean with a skip-reason helper, skips blank visible non-runtime prompts before provider submission, adds focused helper/runner tests, and adds a changelog entry for the linked bug.

## Best Possible Solution

Land this PR, or an equivalent shared embedded-runner prompt-submission guard, after maintainer review and relevant CI are green or unrelated failures are triaged.
