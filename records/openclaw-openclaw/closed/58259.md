## Summary

Current OpenClaw already covers the reported approval-friction request. On shipped `v2026.4.23`, gateway/node host exec defaults to `security=full` and `ask=off`, persisted host approvals support per-agent allowlists plus policy knobs, and the CLI/docs expose `openclaw approvals` and `openclaw exec-policy` flows to inspect and edit that behavior.
