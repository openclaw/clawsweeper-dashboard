## Summary

PR #69552 is superseded by the later open PR #70851 from the same author. Current main still needs the picker cleanup, but #69552's one-line `key.includes(":subagent:")` filter is incomplete and #70851 is the broader canonical follow-up that covers internal sessions and the active-session exception. 

## Best Possible Solution

Close #69552 as superseded and continue the remaining work in #70851 or an equivalent maintainer-approved fix: classify internal/runtime sessions at the session source, hide that classification from the normal chat picker by default, keep the active internal session selectable, and preserve admin/debug visibility for internal rows.

## Evidence
