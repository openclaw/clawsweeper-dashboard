## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still classifies scope-limited gateway probes as unreachable in `openclaw status`, so the reported symptom remains plausible even though `openclaw gateway status` was improved separately.

## Evidence

- **`status` still gates reachability on `probe.ok` only:** The status scan snapshot sets `gatewayReachable` from `gatewayProbe?.ok === true`, with no scope-limited exception.
  - file: [src/commands/status.scan.shared.ts:131](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/ebd7f19a3b20001960474ec9ba9cc424e45f7b23/src/commands/status.scan.shared.ts#L131)

  - file: [src/commands/status-all/format.ts:362](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/ebd7f19a3b20001960474ec9ba9cc424e45f7b23/src/commands/status-all/format.ts#L362)
