## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `7ac35b4f69009490180a35bf404638d4280a4865` still hardcodes voice auto-response generation to agent id `"main"`, and the plugin config/docs do not expose a routing field such as `responseAgentId`.

## Evidence
