## Summary

Issue #27389 is superseded by the later Android Play-distribution cleanup in PR #39660. Current main intentionally does not advertise or dispatch Android screen.record, and the Android manifest still has no media-projection foreground-service permission/type.

## Evidence

- **Current Android manifest lacks media projection foreground-service declaration:** At current main, the Android manifest declares FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC, and FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE, and NodeForegroundService is declared with foregroundServiceType="dataSync|microphone". There is no FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PROJECTION permission or mediaProjection service type.
