## Summary

Current main no longer silently drops Promise-returning plugin `register()` calls: the loader now enforces synchronous registration, the webhooks plugin is synchronous and regression-tested, and the shipped changelog for `v2026.4.23` calls out this exact fix under `#67941`.

## Evidence

- **Runtime loader rejects async register:** `runPluginRegisterSync(...)` calls `register(api)`, detects Promise-like returns, and throws `plugin register must be synchronous` instead of letting async side effects race or disappear.
