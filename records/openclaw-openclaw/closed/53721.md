## Summary

Current main already keeps explicitly configured bundled channels such as Telegram eligible for gateway startup even under restrictive `plugins.allow`, and gateway startup applies that logic on every restart. The issue’s symptom pattern matches the allowlist startup gap called out in the thread, and the current code/tests cover the fixed behavior.
