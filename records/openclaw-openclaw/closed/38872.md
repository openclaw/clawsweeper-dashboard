## Summary

Current `main` already contains overlapping Feishu duplicate-delivery fixes: Feishu reply dispatch now suppresses duplicate final text after streaming close, same-target messaging-tool replies are deduped for Feishu even when the tool reports generic `provider: "message"`, and the changelog records both Feishu-specific duplicate-reply work and later channel-wide replay-dedupe hardening that includes Feishu.
