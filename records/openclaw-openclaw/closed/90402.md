Keep the shipped web_fetch wrapping, provider-payload whitelisting, and LLM-boundary details stripping in place; broader prompt-injection architecture can continue in the existing security design discussions.

Yes. The current fix is at the right boundary: normalize web_fetch provider output before returning it and strip transcript-only details before anything crosses the LLM provider boundary.
