## Best Possible Solution

Choose the public meaning of `channelMaxRestartsPerHour: 0`, then align schema, runtime semantics, docs, and focused config/monitor tests around that contract.

## Solution Assessment

No, the suggested Zod-only fix is not the best complete solution. The stale-threshold half is already handled by a better interval-relative rule, while zero-as-unlimited needs runtime, docs, and tests after maintainer approval.
