## Summary

Current `main` does not show a Feishu-specific `tools.alsoAllow` regression. The observed webchat-vs-Feishu difference is explained by uniform owner-only tool gating: `gateway` remains owner-only, webchat operator sessions are treated as owners, and external channel users only become owners through `commands.ownerAllowFrom` or equivalent owner identity resolution.
