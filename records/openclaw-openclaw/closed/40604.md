## Summary

Current `main` already implements force-termination for spawned sub-agents via the `subagents` control surface, which covers the requested behavior even though it is not named `sessions_kill`.

## Evidence

- **Tool surface includes kill:** `subagents` exposes `action: "kill"` alongside list/steer and routes kill requests to the subagent control path.
