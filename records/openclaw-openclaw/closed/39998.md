## Summary

Current `main` has partial fixes for this report: it now classifies `model_context_window_exceeded` as a context-overflow signal and routes it through overflow compaction/retry. But the TUI-specific parts of #39998 are still not implemented cleanly enough to close: the footer still renders raw `totalTokens/contextTokens` with no freshness bit, no reserve-aware warning threshold, and no special handling for stale fallback counts after an overflowed run.
