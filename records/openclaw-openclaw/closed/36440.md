## Summary

Current `main` already implements image upload in Control UI/WebChat: clipboard paste, file-picker selection, drag-and-drop, preview/removal UI, and `chat.send` attachment serialization are present in the browser chat code, with follow-up fixes for preserving uploaded images across history reloads.

## Evidence

  - file: [ui/src/ui/views/chat.ts:206](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7d471837369885c12d540d6bafa319ff41129b89/ui/src/ui/views/chat.ts#L206)
  - sha: [7d4718373698](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/7d471837369885c12d540d6bafa319ff41129b89)

  - file: [ui/src/ui/views/chat.ts:1230](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7d471837369885c12d540d6bafa319ff41129b89/ui/src/ui/views/chat.ts#L1230)
