## What This Changes

## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

No. This patch makes the job valid by changing Silent into isolated/no-delivery, but the narrower maintainable fix is to preserve a distinct Silent preset shape, likely `main` plus `systemEvent`, or get maintainer direction to redefine the preset.
