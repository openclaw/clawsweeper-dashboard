Keep the current-main completion-delivery recovery and let the next release carry it, with any new post-main failures filed with fresh run IDs, logs, and the subagent JSONL/session state.

Yes. Fixing the delivery/recovery layer is the right boundary because callers should continue to rely on push-based completion rather than adding polling loops in parent agents.
