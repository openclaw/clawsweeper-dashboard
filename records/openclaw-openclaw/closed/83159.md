The issue reports Telegram outbound delivery selecting transcript-only OpenClaw assistant mirror rows and replacing the real reply with duplicate or earlier-turn text.

## Best Possible Solution

Review and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/83266 or an equivalent shared-reader filter, with focused regression coverage and preferably live Telegram proof, then close this issue as implemented.

## Reproduction Assessment

No high-confidence live reproduction is available in this review; the reporter's preserved JSONL was not provided. ## Solution Assessment

Yes. Filtering transcript-only OpenClaw mirror/injected rows before latest-assistant candidate selection is the narrow maintainable fix; same-text dedupe cannot cover the reported earlier-turn fragments.
