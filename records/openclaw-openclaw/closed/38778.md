## Summary

Issue #38778 is not actionable enough to keep open: the report contradicts itself on expected vs actual behavior, provides no logs or concrete Feishu delivery evidence, and the follow-up discussion points to a Feishu platform limitation rather than an OpenClaw regression.

## Evidence
