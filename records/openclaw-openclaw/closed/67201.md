## Summary

Current `main` shows the reported once-per-day Telegram group rollover is expected session-reset behavior, not a compaction or fs-safe-triggered transcript reset. OpenClaw defaults sessions to a daily 4:00 AM local reset, archives the prior transcript on stale/daily rollover, and QMD/fs-safe sync failures are logged as warnings rather than session-reset triggers.

## Evidence

- **Default session policy is daily reset at 4 AM local:** `resolveSessionResetPolicy()` falls back to `DEFAULT_RESET_MODE = "daily"` and `DEFAULT_RESET_AT_HOUR = 4` when no explicit reset override is configured; freshness marks a session stale once it predates that daily boundary.

- **Session init explicitly rolls over stale daily sessions:** `initSessionState` evaluates freshness with the reset policy and treats non-fresh entries as scheduled/daily resets, capturing the previous session so its transcript is archived afterward.
