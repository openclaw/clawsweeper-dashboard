## Summary

Keep #69235 open. Current main still has separate streaming/block-reply thinking-tag parsers that do not recognize the `<antml:thinking>` namespaced form called out by the linked fix PR, even though the final-text sanitizer handles it. The issue is recent, non-maintainer, unprotected, and the reported Slack/channel streaming leak remains plausible on current main.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and land or rework #69288: consolidate streaming/block-reply tag stripping with the shared reasoning-tag parser, add `<antml:thinking>` and attribute-bearing tag cases to the streaming harness/tests, and verify the Slack block/native streaming delivery path no longer emits reasoning text.

## Evidence
