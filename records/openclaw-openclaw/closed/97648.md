## What This Changes

The PR wraps Mistral SDK response bodies with a 16 MiB streaming byte guard and adds synthetic plus loopback bounded-stream tests.

## Best Possible Solution

Land the bounded fetcher after maintainers accept or tune the 16 MiB Mistral response limit, keeping the guard core-internal and covered by the loopback plus synthetic tests.

## Reproduction Assessment

## Solution Assessment

Yes, with a compatibility caveat. Injecting the SDK-supported HTTPClient fetcher is the narrowest maintainable interception point for Mistral SDK responses; adding a config knob would be extra product surface unless maintainers reject the fixed threshold.
