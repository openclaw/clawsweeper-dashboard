The issue requests automatic cleanup or an equivalent root-cause fix when session JSONL locks are detected as stale during high-concurrency `sessions_send` and `sessions_spawn` workflows.

Keep the remaining work on the narrower canonical trackers for max-hold reclaim, subagent delivery durability, synthetic-flush lock lifetime, and the session/transcript migration instead of preserving this broad auto-cleanup umbrella.
