## Summary

Current main already avoids the reported provider-wide skip: same-provider fallback candidates are probed during transient cooldowns (`rate_limit`, `overloaded`, `timeout`), and rate-limit cooldowns can be scoped to a specific model. The same behavior is present in the shipped `v2026.4.22` tag, which is newer than the reported `2026.3.13` build.

## Evidence
