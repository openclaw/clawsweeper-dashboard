## Best Possible Solution

Land after maintainers explicitly accept the opt-in cleanup default, or adjust the patch to preserve a non-destructive compatibility cleanup path while still preventing cross-subdir deletion.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear until maintainer policy review: removing write-time pruning is the clean owner-boundary fix for silent deletion, but the unset-default retention change is compatibility-sensitive.
