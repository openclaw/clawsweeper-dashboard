## Summary

Current main, and the shipped `v2026.4.23` release, already turn silent LLM/Ollama idle timeouts into a visible final error instead of leaving the TUI turn hanging. The runner also retries one silent idle-timeout once, and docs/tests cover the behavior and the `agents.defaults.llm.idleTimeoutSeconds` mitigation.

## Evidence
