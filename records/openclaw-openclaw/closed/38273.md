## Summary

Current `main` already routes `google-vertex` through the bundled Google provider normalization and forward-compat resolver, and the shipped `v2026.4.22` tree includes a regression test that specifically resolves `google-vertex/gemini-3.1-pro-preview` instead of falling through to an unknown-model error.

## Evidence

- **Google provider owns google-vertex hook path:** The bundled Google provider declares `hookAliases: ["google-antigravity", "google-vertex"]` and sends aliased providers through `resolveGoogleGeminiForwardCompatModel`, so `google-vertex` now shares the Google forward-compat resolver instead of bypassing it.
