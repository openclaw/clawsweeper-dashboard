## Summary

The issue’s stated blockers do not match current `main`: `memory-core` already ships managed dreaming with transcript ingestion and deep promotion, embedding-backed `memory_search` is documented and configurable, `active-memory` is already bundled, and there is no `dreaming-guard` surface in the repo.
