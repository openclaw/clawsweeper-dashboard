## Summary

Current `main` already contains dedicated iMessage outbound-echo suppression for this failure mode, with shipped release evidence and regression coverage. The fix appears to have shipped in `v2026.4.10` and is still present unchanged in the latest release/code for the relevant iMessage monitor files.

## Evidence

- **Inbound iMessage echo/self-chat suppression on current main:** `resolveIMessageInboundDecision` drops normal outbound `is_from_me` rows, self-chat agent echoes, scoped echo-cache matches, and reflected assistant-content markers before dispatch.

  - sha: [a155b84cdade](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/a155b84cdadef068de50274318952032cd867788)
- **Outbound sends populate the per-conversation echo cache:** Every delivered text/media chunk records `sentText` and `messageId` into the iMessage sent-message cache immediately after send, which is the core mechanism used to drop reflected outbound replies when they come back through the inbound poller.
  - file: [extensions/imessage/src/monitor/deliver.ts:49](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/a155b84cdadef068de50274318952032cd867788/extensions/imessage/src/monitor/deliver.ts#L49)
