## Summary

Current `main` already implements the substance of this request: proactive compaction before/near context exhaustion, reactive overflow compaction with retry, chained summaries via `previousSummary`, and configurable preservation of recent context.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/agents/pi-embedded-runner/run/attempt.ts:2305](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/59523e66da03591bc7bcc183a11f690fa0aae641/src/agents/pi-embedded-runner/run/attempt.ts#L2305)
