## Summary

Current `main` already replaces the duplicated WhatsApp listener-map pattern with a process-global singleton, adds regressions for duplicate module/runtime instances, and includes duplicate-send hardening in the outbound path. The report’s cited root cause is implemented and covered on `main`.
