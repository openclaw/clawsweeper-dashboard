## Summary

Current `main` already routes subagent lifecycle calls through the normal gateway client path, resolves the target from `OPENCLAW_GATEWAY_PORT` or `gateway.port`, and documents the gateway as a single multiplexed WS+HTTP port rather than separate control-UI and main-agent ports.

## Evidence
