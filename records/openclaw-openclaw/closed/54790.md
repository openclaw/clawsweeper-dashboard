## Summary

Current `main` already covers the reported npm-upgrade failure cluster: packaged-update stale chunk handling is in place, `memory-lancedb` no longer depends on `dist/package.json`, packaged workspace templates are shipped/resolved from the package root, and package-root/plugin-runtime resolution is hardened against stray `dist/package.json` files.
