## Summary

Current `main` already supports multi-bot Telegram setups through `channels.telegram.accounts.<accountId>` with per-account tokens and policy, plus isolated routing/session behavior and topic-level `agentId` routing for different agents. The request's core outcome does not require separate top-level channel keys anymore.

## Evidence
