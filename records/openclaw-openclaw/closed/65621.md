## Summary

Issue #65621 is superseded by the author’s linked open PR #65641, which explicitly says it fixes this issue and carries the exact remaining implementation/test work: preserving literal `deviceIdentity: null` in `GatewayClient` and threading early `onConnectError` into the final `callGateway` 1006 error. Current `main` has related partial loopback/backend device-identity cleanup, but the full diagnostic/null-preservation work is not shipped on `main`, so the best maintainer cleanup is to track the remaining change on the PR rather than keep the duplicate issue open.
