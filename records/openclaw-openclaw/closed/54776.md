## Summary

Current `main` already implements the two recovery pieces this report is asking for: teardown cleanup now clears pending tool-call state instead of persisting synthetic `missing tool result` entries on idle-timeout shutdown, and restart-sentinel recovery now wakes the interrupted session and dispatches a routed continuation after restart.

## Evidence
