## Summary

Current main already includes the Telegram webhook recovery work: `webhookCertPath` is part of Telegram config and is passed through to `setWebhook`, and the health monitor only treats channels as `stale-socket` when they publish transport-liveness timestamps. The changelog records this as shipped in `2026.3.7`.
