## Summary

Current `main` already fixes the macOS LaunchAgent KeepAlive mismatch and adds repair paths for stale installs. The LaunchAgent plist builder now writes `KeepAlive=true`, doctor audits LaunchAgent config drift and reinstalls the service to recommended defaults, and daemon start/restart can re-bootstrap an installed-but-not-loaded LaunchAgent.
