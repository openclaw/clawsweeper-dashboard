Land a completion-only patch that applies the existing skip-env contract to doctor-owned cache writes, preserves explicit plugin completion rebuilds, removes unrelated cron/SDK churn, and leaves https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/82070 open for broader cold-start work.
