## Summary

Current `main` already handles assistant-side auth failures from streamed terminal errors: expired-token text is classified as `auth`, the embedded runner converts assistant-side `surface_error`/`fallback_model` outcomes into `FailoverError`, and the outer model-fallback loop continues on those classified failures even when the provider did not originally throw.
