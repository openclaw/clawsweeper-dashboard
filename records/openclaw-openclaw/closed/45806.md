## Summary

Current `main` already prevents heartbeat/system-event turns from overwriting the remembered external route, and cron `delivery.channel: "last"` resolves from the job session/main session route rather than a synthetic heartbeat target. That covers the reported `@heartbeat` misrouting path.

## Evidence
