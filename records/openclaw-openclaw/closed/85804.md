The issue asks OpenClaw to notify or cleanly reset a Telegram-backed main session when restart recovery cannot safely resume after a Codex app-server startup failure.

## Best Possible Solution

Review and land https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/85805 or an equivalent channel-agnostic recovery-notice fix, while keeping the upstream Codex startup retry exhaustion tracked separately at https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/83959.

## Solution Assessment

Yes. The maintainable fix belongs in main-session restart recovery as a bounded channel-agnostic abandoned-session path, not by folding this downstream behavior into only the Codex startup retry issue.
