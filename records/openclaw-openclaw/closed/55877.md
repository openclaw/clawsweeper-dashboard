## Summary

Current `main` already addresses the reported startup/readiness regression. Gateway bind/listen now happens before sidecars, channel startup, and model prewarm are awaited; RPC stays available during sidecar startup, readiness stays red until startup sidecars settle, and the docs/manifests now distinguish `/healthz` liveness from `/readyz` readiness.

## Evidence

- **Current startup path no longer blocks overall serving on sidecars/prewarm:** `startGatewayPostAttachRuntime` launches sidecars via `setImmediate(...).then(...)` and only awaits them when `awaitSidecars === true`; normal startup returns without awaiting that work. This means bind/listen can complete while sidecars continue in the background.

## Risks / Open Questions

- The Fly.io example `fly.toml` does not declare an explicit readiness path, so operators who keep relying on `/` or platform-default checks may still observe misleading rollout health unless they switch to `/readyz`.
- The model prewarm is still awaited inside the sidecar startup band (`src/gateway/server-startup-post-attach.ts:250-257`); the fix is that serving/readiness semantics now isolate that work, not that prewarm was removed.
