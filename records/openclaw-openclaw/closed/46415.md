## Summary

Current `main` already fixes the reported Ollama fallback-stream misrouting through a newer provider-owned stream resolution path, and that implementation is present in the shipped `v2026.4.22` release. The PR's proposed one-line `isOllamaCompatProvider(...)` routing change is obsolete on `main` and would be broader than the shipped fix.
