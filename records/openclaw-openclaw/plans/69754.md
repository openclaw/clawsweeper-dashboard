## Plan

Fix https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/69754 with a narrow approval fallback repair. On current main, Feishu document-comment/native-card degradation and shared approval fallback text can leave users with label-only approval buttons or literal `/approve <id>` placeholders.
