## Plan

Fix https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/64609. Implement a Telegram-owned prompt/skill/topic lookup that falls back from `channels.telegram.accounts.<accountId>.groups` to `channels.telegram.groups` for prompt payload construction, normal replies, and native commands, while preserving the existing multi-account routing/access-control guard from https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/30673. Do not restore wholesale channel-level group inheritance through `mergeTelegramAccountConfig`, do not loosen `enabled`, `allowFrom`, `groupPolicy`, or delivery routing semantics, and keep DM topic resolution unchanged.
