## Plan

Preserve support for exact hosted opaque origins such as `chrome-extension://<id>`, `tauri://localhost`, and `electron://app` by normalizing the request Origin to `scheme://host` when `URL.origin === "null"` and a host is present. Keep this PR's Gateway WebSocket handshake coverage or equivalent coverage. Do not add scheme wildcards; leave `chrome-extension://*` to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/39739.
