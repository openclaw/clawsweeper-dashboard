Current `src/cli/cron-cli/shared.ts` validates the parsed number before multiplying by the unit factor, but a long pure-digit value such as `1` plus 302 zeros plus `d` produces `Math.floor(Infinity)` and callers in `schedule-options.ts` and `register.cron-edit.ts` treat it as valid.
