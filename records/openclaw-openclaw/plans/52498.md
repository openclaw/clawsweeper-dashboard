## Plan

Fix https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/52498 with a narrow additive `sessions_send` attribution change. Current main records `sourceSessionKey`, `sourceChannel`, and `sourceTool`, and Control UI can render a top-level `senderLabel`, but `sessions_send` does not produce a display-safe sender/target label.
