## Plan

Current main drops explicit `cacheRetention` for direct `amazon.nova-*` IDs in `resolveCacheRetention` and wraps direct non-Anthropic Bedrock IDs with `createBedrockNoCacheWrapper`. Keep the policy provider-owned except for small typed shared helpers, and update tests, public docs, and changelog together.
