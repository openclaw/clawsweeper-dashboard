## Plan

Fix the heartbeat busy-skip retry race from https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/40611. Keep the no-drift behavior from https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/pull/39182, but stop `requests-in-flight`/session-busy heartbeat retries from immediately firing 1 second after active conversation work drains and grabbing the session path before the next user turn. Prefer a typed busy-skip debounce/backoff or idle/quiet-window mechanism in the heartbeat wake/runner boundary; preserve fast retry for unrelated transient failures and cron/dependency skips. Leave full non-blocking heartbeat execution to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/52046 and activity-based heartbeat policy to https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/14051. Add focused regression coverage for the new busy-skip timing and keep docs/config unchanged unless the chosen implementation introduces a documented user-facing setting.
