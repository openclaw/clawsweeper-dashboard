## Summary

Keep open. Current main still has source-visible paths where a gateway can accept a WebSocket or have a listening port, but the probe and doctor diagnostics collapse pre-hello handshake failures into unreachable or not-running output.

## Plan

Fix the misleading gateway reachability diagnostics reported in https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/issues/79099. Preserve gateway auth, pairing, and protocol rejection behavior; do not weaken `gateway.auth.mode: none` or treat failed handshakes as successful operator access. Add an explicit probe/doctor classification for accepted WebSocket or listener-present-but-handshake/auth/protocol-failed states, render `gateway probe` as reachable-but-degraded when appropriate, and stop `doctor` from saying `Gateway not running` when the evidence shows a listener or accepted connection with a specific failure reason. Add focused regression coverage and a user-facing changelog entry.
