## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.88

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Honor sync includes before uploading archives:** `internal/providers/smolvm/sync.go:21`
  - body: `sync.includes` is a whitelist, but SmolVM builds its upload manifest through the wrapper that calls `BuildSyncManifest` without passing `cfg.Sync.Includes`. A user who configured includes to limit what leaves the repo would still upload the whole tracked/unignored workspace to the hosted SmolVM API; use the include-aware manifest helper like the existing archive-sync providers.
  - confidence: 0.93
- **[P2] Route remote stderr to stderr:** `internal/providers/smolvm/client.go:255-256`
  - body: `ExecStream` appends `result.Stderr` to the same writer used for stdout, and `Run` passes `b.rt.Stdout` there. That makes remote diagnostics appear in stdout output and breaks callers that pipe or compare stdout from `crabbox run`; route stderr through `Runtime.Stderr` instead.
  - confidence: 0.86
