     9	`apple-vz` is an experimental local SSH-lease provider. Crabbox shells out to a
    10	small macOS helper, boots an Ubuntu cloud image with `Virtualization.framework`,
    11	exposes guest SSH through a host-local VSOCK proxy, then reuses the normal
    12	Crabbox SSH sync and command path.
    13	
    14	The provider is local only. It never uses the coordinator or cloud
    15	credentials.

    18

    22
