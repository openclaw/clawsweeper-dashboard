Full review comments:

- **[P1] Preserve Tart claim state when cleanup fails:** `internal/providers/tart/backend.go:272-275`
  - body: ReleaseLease discards errors from both stopVM and deleteVM, then removes the lease claim and stored key. If `tart delete` fails for a real VM due to a busy VM, permissions, or a transient Tart error, Crabbox forgets the lease while the VM can remain running; tolerate only confirmed missing-VM stale claims or keep state on failure.
  - confidence: 0.91
- **[P1] Honor keep on late acquire failures:** `internal/providers/tart/backend.go:162-164`
  - body: The earlier waitForIP/injectSSHKey branches preserve keep=true VMs, but the next failure paths still stop and delete unconditionally. `prepareLease` includes the SSH readiness wait, and claim/endpoint writes happen after the VM is started, so a keep=true VM can still be destroyed after a late readiness or claim failure.
  - confidence: 0.88
- **[P2] Clean up launched kept VMs on startup cancellation:** `internal/providers/tart/backend.go:433-437`
  - body: If the context is cancelled after `cmd.Start()` in keep mode, startVM returns without killing the Tart process. Acquire then tries a bare delete and ignores failure, so a started VM can be left without a claim; stop or kill the launched process, or make the cleanup path claim-aware before returning.
  - confidence: 0.8
