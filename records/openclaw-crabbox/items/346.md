Full review comments:

- **[P1] Do not use the gateway name as the env.allow safety check:** `internal/station/gate.go:124-126`
  - body: `AuthorizeModelAccess` claims to enforce that model/tool credentials never come from ordinary `env.allow`, but this loop only rejects an allowlist entry equal to `ModelAccess.Gateway`.
