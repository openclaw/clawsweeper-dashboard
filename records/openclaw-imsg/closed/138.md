## Best Possible Solution

## Solution Assessment

Yes, with a proof caveat: moving Objective-C/Foundation/IMCore work out of the constructor is the narrow maintainable fix for dyld initializer ordering, and the added test locks that boundary. The remaining question is whether maintainers require macOS 26.5 proof before merge.
