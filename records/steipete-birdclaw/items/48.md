## Best Possible Solution

Maintainers should choose the intended Links `Today` boundary and keep tests aligned: accept this UTC boundary if link-insights should follow UTC `created_at`, or preserve local-day behavior and adjust the flaky fixtures around local midnight.

## Solution Assessment

Unclear. `setUTCHours` is a narrow mechanical fix for UTC-day semantics, but it is only the best solution if maintainers want Links `Today` to mean UTC day rather than the local-day behavior used by the digest path.
