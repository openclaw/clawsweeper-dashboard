## Best Possible Solution

Keep the renewable provider-scoped lease design, but merge only after redacted live Actions evidence covers its complete lifecycle and maintainers explicitly accept the fail-closed state dependency and recovery timings.

## Solution Assessment

Yes in design direction: a renewable provider-scoped lease is narrower than restoring a repository-wide worker queue and addresses lease expiration during long reviews. It is not merge-ready until live lifecycle proof and the fail-closed operational tradeoff are accepted.
