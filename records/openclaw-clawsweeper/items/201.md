- **[P1] Preserve live-worker override headroom:** `src/repair/limits.ts:96`
  - body: This makes `repair_live_runs.hard_cap` the max of the new lane defaults. Keep the defaults at 1, but cap explicit overrides at `workers.max` or another intentional high ceiling so existing repair operations do not fail on upgrade.
  - confidence: 0.92

  - body: The new live-mode gate runs after `detectClusterSource()` and the `--from-gitcrawl` selection path, both of which call sqlite3. Move mode validation and `CLAWSWEEPER_FEATURE_CLUSTER_REPAIR_ENABLED` enforcement ahead of cluster-source detection, selection, mkdir, and other import work.
  - confidence: 0.9
