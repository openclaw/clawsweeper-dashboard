## Best Possible Solution

## Reproduction Assessment

Do we have a high-confidence way to reproduce the issue? ## Solution Assessment

Is this the best way to solve the issue? Yes: exporting a stable code, predicate, and `missing-capability` versus `unadvertised-model` reason is narrower and more maintainable than downstream text matching while preserving strict errors for explicit unsupported model ids.
