- **[P1] Preserve the cron.run id/force payload:** `src/OpenClaw.Shared/OpenClawGatewayClient.cs:774-778`

Gateways that accept `jobId` can lose forced-run semantics, and gateways that reject it with a different error may never reach the fallback.   - confidence: 0.93
