Full review comments:

- **[P2] Canonicalize quoted senders before storing:** `internal/app/sync.go:441`
  - body: storeParsedMessage already normalizes chat and sender IDs through `a.canonicalStoreJID` before writing message rows, but this new field writes `pm.ReplyToSenderJID` directly from WhatsApp ContextInfo.
