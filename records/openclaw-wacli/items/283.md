## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.84

Full review comments:

- **[P2] Keep detached connect attempts cancellable:** `internal/wa/client.go:154-156`
  - body: `connectForSync` and `ReconnectWithBackoff` now enable `DetachSocket`, which makes `ConnectContext` use `context.Background()` during the actual WhatsApp connect. A slow dial or handshake can therefore ignore SIGTERM or `--max-reconnect` until whatsmeow returns; detach the established socket lifetime without removing caller cancellation from the in-flight connect attempt.
  - confidence: 0.86
- **[P2] Keep available presence within the sync lifetime:** `internal/app/sync_events.go:103-107`
  - body: The `Connected` and `PushNameSetting` handlers send `PresenceAvailable` with `context.WithoutCancel(ctx)`. Because the handler remains registered while shutdown cleanup sends `PresenceUnavailable`, a late event can still send available after cancellation and leave the linked device marked active; use the active sync context or remove/drain the handler before cleanup.
  - confidence: 0.8
- **[P2] Bound the final unavailable presence send:** `internal/app/sync.go:207`
  - body: The final cleanup send is synchronous but uses `context.WithoutCancel(ctx)`, which strips both cancellation and deadlines. If `SendPresence` stalls while the socket is unhealthy, `wacli sync` can hang during graceful shutdown instead of warning and exiting; wrap this detached cleanup in a short explicit timeout.
  - confidence: 0.82
