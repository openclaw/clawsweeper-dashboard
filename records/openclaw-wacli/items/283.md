Full review comments:

- **[P2] Gate DetachSocket off for auth bootstrap:** `internal/app/sync.go:238`
  - body: `connectForSync` is also used by `wacli auth`, which passes `AllowQR: true`. Setting `DetachSocket: true` unconditionally means QR and phone-pairing connects no longer use the signal-cancellable context and can keep dialing for the fixed 30-second window after SIGTERM; keep detachment to already-authenticated sync/reconnect paths.
  - confidence: 0.89
- **[P2] Respect caller deadlines during detached reconnect dials:** `internal/wa/client.go:158-159`
  - body: With `DetachSocket`, `ConnectContext` gets a background context canceled only by the 30-second timer. A shorter `--max-reconnect` or caller cancellation while inside one connect attempt is ignored until that timer fires; detach the established socket lifetime without removing cancellation from the in-flight dial.
  - confidence: 0.86
- **[P2] Guard in-flight available sends before cleanup:** `internal/app/sync_events.go:103-107`
  - body: Removing the handler before final unavailable prevents future events, but it does not wait for an already-dispatched `Connected` or `PushNameSetting` callback. Since those callbacks send available with `context.WithoutCancel(ctx)`, a slow in-flight send can still complete after cleanup and leave the linked device marked active.
  - confidence: 0.78
