- **[P2] Reset activity after stale reconnects:** `internal/app/sync_idle.go:33`
  - body: After this reconnect succeeds, `lastEvent` is still the old timestamp unless WhatsApp immediately emits a tracked message/store event.   - confidence: 0.9
