## Review Findings

Overall correctness: patch is incorrect

Overall confidence: 0.78

Full review comments:

- **[P1] Constrain slide image origins before sending the daemon token:** `apps/chrome-extension/src/entrypoints/sidepanel/slide-images.ts:17-21`
  - body: This derives the stable slide URL origin from any incoming `/v1/slides/` or `/v1/summarize/` URL. Keep the rewrite/fetch scoped to the configured `127.0.0.1:<port>` origin, or reject non-local daemon paths, and add a regression test for a custom local port.
  - confidence: 0.78
